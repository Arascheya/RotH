Lemon Ginger Cookies

Ingredients:

2.5 cups sifted all purpose flower

2 teaspoons baking soda

0.5 teaspoon salt

3 teaspoons ground ginger - could use frshly grated ginger, crystalized ginger, or powder

1 teaspoon cinnamon

1 cup firmly packed brown sugar

1 egg

0.25 cup molasses

1 tablespoon grated lemon rind

0.25 cup granulated sugar

Directions:

Preheat oven to 350

Sift Flour, baking soda, salt, ginger, and cinnamon together.

Beat butter with brown sugar and egg until light and fluffy. Beat in molasses and lemon rind until well mixed. Add dry ingrediants, half at a time. mixing well.

Roll dough, one tablespoon at a time, into balls and roll in granulated sugar. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheeets.

Bake about ten minutes. Do not over bake.
