BANA CAIDA - Bagna Cauda (Italian Fondue)

Ingredients:

1/2 c. olive oil

1/4 c. butter

3 lg. or 4 smaller cloves garlic, chopped

1 can anchovies

Directions:

Heat oil and butter and saute chopped garlic. Add anchovies and cook on simmer until anchovies cook and stir mixture until soft smooth fondue. Anchovies should "disappear".

Use as a dip for:

Italian bread chunks

Celery hearts

Lettuce wedges

Green pepper slices
