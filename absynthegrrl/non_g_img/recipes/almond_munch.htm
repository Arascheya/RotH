Almond Munch

Ingredients:

½ cup slivered almonds

1/3 cup margarine

1 cup sugar

¼ cup corn syrup

1/3 cup water

1 teaspoon vanilla

¼ teaspoon almond flavoring or extract

3 cups crispix

Directions:

In large skillet over medium heat, cook the almonds with the margarine, sugar, corn syrup, and water, stirring constantly, until the mixture starts to boil. Cook and stir for 10-12 minutes longer, or until the almonds start to brown. Remove from heat. Carefully stir vanilla and almond flavoring into the hot mixture. Add the cereal and gently stir to coat. Spread mixture onto a foil lined 10x15 cookie sheet that has been sprayed with vegetable spray. Cool completely, then break into pieces. Store in air-tight containers.
