Minute Fudge Frosting
Mrs. Loren Casey, wife of State Representative, Wolf Point, Montanna
Montana Centennial Edition Women's CookBook

2 squares baking chocolate
2 cups sugar
2/3 cup milk
1/2 cup crisco
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla

Bring slowly to a full rolling boil and boil for 1 minute. Add vanilla, and beat until thick enough to spread. If frosting beomes thick, add about a tblspn of cream.
