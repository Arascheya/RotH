Russian Pancakes

from Dimitri

These are the thicker style, not the thin crepe-like version

Ingredients:

2 cups buttermilk

1 egg

1 cup flour - to start

3 teaspoons sugar - approx.

salt - 1 teaspoon - approx.

1 teaspoon baking soda

white vinegar

Directions:

Use regular table setting teaspoon. Mix egg and buttermilk, add flour to make batter, adjust flour to get right consistency, should be heavy cream-like. Drizzle vinegar over teaspoon of baking soda and add to mix. Shouldn't have too much vinegar.

Cook in pan, using butter as lubricant for cooking. Turn slightly before the usual pancake turning point.

Top with desired toppings - savory or sweet, can be used for main course, desert, or both. Some good items are roasted eggplant with garlic, cheeses, smoked fish, sausage, sweet preserves, etc.
