Marylou French Bread

Ingredients:

2-3/4 cups water

5 teaspoons dry yeast (2 pkgs)

3 tablespoons sugar or honey

6 to 7 cups berad flour

3 tablespoons olive oil

4 teaspoons salt

Directions:

Put warm water, yeast, salt, oil, and sugar in bowl. Add 3 cups of flour and mix together. Add enough flour to get to kneading consistency. Turn out and knead until smooth and elastic, about 8 to 10 minutes.

Cover and let rise. Punch down and divide. Let rest ten minutes, then form into desired loaves by roling out and roling up like jelly roll. Bake at 375 until done.
