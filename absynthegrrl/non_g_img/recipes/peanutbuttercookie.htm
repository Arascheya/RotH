Peanut Butter Cookies - Gluten Free

Ingredients:

2/3 cup honey
1/3 cup butter or oil
1 cup peanut butter
1 egg
1/4 t Salt
1/2 t vanilla
1 3/4 cup mix of flour, ground walnuts, ground almonds

Directions:

Mix together, roll into balls, mash flat with fork. Bake at 375 for 10 minutes and check for done.
