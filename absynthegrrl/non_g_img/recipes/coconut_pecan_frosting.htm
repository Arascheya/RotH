Coconut Pecan Frosting

Ingredients:

1.5 cup (12 ounce can) evaporated milk

1.5 cup sugar

4 egg yolks

3/4 cup butter

1.5 tsp vanilla

2 cup angel flake coconut

1.5 cups pecans

Directions:

Combine all ingrediants in sauce pan. Cook over medium heat until thick and golden brown, about 12 minutes, stirring constantly.

Remove from heat and stir in pecans and coconut. Beat until cool and of spreading consistency.
