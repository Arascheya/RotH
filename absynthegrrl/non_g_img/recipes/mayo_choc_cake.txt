Mayo Chocolate Cake

3 cups flour
1-1/2 cups sugar
2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa
2-1/4 tsp baking powder
1-1/2 tsp baking soda
1-1/2 cups mayo
1-1/2 cups water
1-1/2 tsp vanilla

Grease 2 9 inch layer pans and line bottoms with wax paper. Sift together dry ingredients and stir in mayo. Gradually stir in water and vanilla until smooth and blended. Pour into pans and bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes.
