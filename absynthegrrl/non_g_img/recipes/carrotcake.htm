Carrot Cake

Cream together

1-3/4 cup sugar

1-1/4 cup oil

add 4 eggs and beat together

sift in

2cups + 2 Tblspns flour

2 tsp baking soda

2 tsp cinnamon

1 tsp salt

fold in

3 cups grated carrots

1 cup chopped pecans (large pieces)

optional

1/2 lemon zest and juice

Frosting

8 oz softened cream cheese

1/4 margarine

milk as required

vanilla

add powdered sugar until cream cheese flavor is at desired level

chocolate variation

as above, adding 1 square semi-sweet chocolate melted into mixture
