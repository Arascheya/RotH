Reuben Balls

2 slices rye bread crumbled
3/4 pound beef, cooked and ground
3 cups sauerkraut, drained and chopped
1 1/2 cups Gruyere cheese, grated
1 tsp. caraway seeds
1 onion, minced and browned in 2 tbsp butter or oil
3 tbsp parsley, minced
1 tbsp Dijon mustard
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup dry vermouth
1 tsp dill
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup flour
rye breadcrumbs
oil for deep fry
batter - recipe follows

combine meat, onion, parsley, mustard, sauerkraut, caraway seed, dill, and rye breadcrumbs in food processor fitted with metal blade

transfer to skillet and heat. blend in milk and vermouth. cook until thickened

over low heat, stir in cheese and cook until it melts. taste and adjust flavors

place mixture in bowl and chill until cold. at least an hour

shape into 1" balls and roll in flour

dip the balls in batter and then in crumbs. chill balls for at least an hour

deep fry and serve with Mustard Mayo Sauce

Batter
2 eggs, beaten
2/3 cup water

combine in small bowl

Mustard Mayo Sauce
1/2 cup mayo
1/2 cup yogurt
1 tbsp Dijon mustard
1 tsp shallots
1 tsp dried dill
zest from 1 lemon, finely grated
white pepper to taste

combine all ingrediants, adjust to taste
