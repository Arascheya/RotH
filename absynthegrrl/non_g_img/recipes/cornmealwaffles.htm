Corn Meal Waffles

Ingredients:

3/4 tsp salt

1/2 c all-purpose flour

1 large egg

1 1/2 c plain white cornmeal

1 1/2 c milk

2 tbsps sugar

2 1/2 tsps baking powder

Directions:

Step #1 Stir together first 5 ingredients In a large-ish bowl.

Step #2 Stir together egg & milk; add to cornmeal mixture, stirring just until dry ingredients are moistened.

Step #3 Bake in a preheated, oiled waffle iron just until crisp.
