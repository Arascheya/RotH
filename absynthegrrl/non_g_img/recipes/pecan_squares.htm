Pecan Squares

Substrate

Ingredients:

1 cup butter

1/2 cup light brown sugar

3 cups flour

pinch salt

Directions:

Mix together and chill for half an hour.

Bake at 325 until lightly browned.

Topping

Ingredients:

1/2 cup buttter

1/2 cup light brown sugar

6 tablespoon honey

2 tablespoons heavy cream

2 tablespoons granulated sugar

Directions:

Mix together and bring to a boil, stirring constantly.

Add 1/2 teaspoon vanilla and 8 oz. chopped pecans.

Pour over baked crust and bake a 325 until topping is bubbling all over.
