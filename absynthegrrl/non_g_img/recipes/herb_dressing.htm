Buttermilk Herb Dressing

Ingredients:

2 green onions, thinly sliced

2 tablespoons cilantro leave, tightly packeds

1.5 tablespoons terragon leaves, lightly packed

2 tablespoons italian parsley leaves, tightly packed cilantro leave1/4 cup dill fronds, medium pack

1 cup mayo, divided

2 tablespoons sour cream

1/2 cup plus 1 tablespoon buttermilk

1 1/4 tablespoons lemon juice

1 tablespoon red wine vinegar

3/4 teaspoon kosher salt

1 1/4 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper

dash sugar if too sharp

Directions:

Rinse green onions under warm water and drain. Place herbs in a small food processor and process until fine. Add 1/2 cup mayo and continue processing until well mixed. Remove from processor and place in small bowl. Add remaining mayo and sour cream and whisk until smooth. Add remaining ingredients and whisk until combined. Adjust seasoning if needed/desired. Refrigerate until ready to use.
