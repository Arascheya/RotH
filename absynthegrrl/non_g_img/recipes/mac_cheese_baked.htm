Simple Baked Mac and Cheese

Ingredients:

small onion - chopped

2 eggs

2 Tblspn butter

spices - as desired

shredded cheese

box mac

1/2 cup evap milk

sausage, ring balogna, etc. - optional

Directions:

Cook mac, drain, add cheese and rest of ingredients, stir until cheese is melted and everything is mixed, bake at 350 for 45 min or until brown on top.
