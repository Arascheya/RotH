Pizza Dough

Ingredients:

1 Cup warm water - approx. 110 deg F.

1 pkg yeast

1 teaspoon honey

1 tablespoon olive oil

3-4 cups flour

Directions:

Combine yeast, honey, and water. Let proof in a warm place until foamy (5 to 10 minutes). Put 3 cups flour in bowl, make a well in the center and poor in oil and yeast mixture. Stir flour into yeast mixture adding flour to make a slightly sticky dough. Kneed dough 5 to 10 minutes or until smooth and elastic. Form into a ball and let rise until double in bulk. Punch down and form into pizza. Bake at 400 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes or until crust is brown and crisp.
