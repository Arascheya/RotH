Date and Brazil Nut Cake
Mrs. H.E. Smith, Chamber of Commerce, Hot Springs Montanna
Montana Centennial Edition Women's CookBook

3 cups brazil nuts
1 lb dates
1 cup drained maraschino cherries
3/4 cup sifted flour
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
3 eggs
1 tsp vanilla

Put nuts, dates and cherries into a large bowl. Sift dry ingredients on top of fruit and nuts. Mix thoroughly with hands. Beat eggs until foamy, add vanilla, and stir into bowl. Mix thoroughly and turn into greased and wax paper lined pan. Bake at 300 degrees for 1 hour 45 minutes.
Cool completely before slicing into thin slices.
