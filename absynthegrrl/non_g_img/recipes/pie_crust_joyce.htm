Pie Crust from Joyce

This one is excellent. This recipe makes 4 generous crusts.

Ingredients:

5 cups flour

2 tsp salt

1 cup butter

6oz shortning

3/4 cup cold water

Directions:

Cut together the flour, salt, and grease using a food processor or pastry cutter.

Add in water and mix to crust consistency without over-working. Chill and roll out.
