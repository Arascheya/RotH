Fudge Frosting
Mrs. Gene Huntley, wife of State Senator, Baker, Montanna
Montana Centennial Edition Women's CookBook

2 cups sugar
1/4 cup cocoa
1/4 cup white corn syrup
1/4 cup butter or shortening
1/4 cup milk
1/4 tsp salt

Mix and heat until shortening is melted. Bring to a full boil and boil for one minute. Cool. Add 1 tsp vanilla and beat until spreading consistency.
