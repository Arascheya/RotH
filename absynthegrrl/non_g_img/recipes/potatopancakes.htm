Potato Pancakes

Ingredients:

3 medium potatos, shredded

2 eggs

1 cup onion, chopped

tablespoon flour

high temp oil for frying in

Directions:

Can either shred the potatoes through a shredder or ground with the onion in a food processor until fine ground. Squeeze out about one cup of water from the shredded/ground potato if shredded.

Mix shredded potatos, egg, flour, pinch slat together. Heat oil to hot and drop potato mix into skillet. Spread around until relatively thin and cook until browned on each side. Serve warm with adjuncts such as applesauce, sour cream, syrup.

Harry's Diner version

More fancy, but the version with cooked potatoes tasted more like flour and egg than potato

Ingredients:

5 eggs

1/2 cup diced onion

1/2 cup flour

1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

1 teaspoon dried parsley flakes

1/4 teaspoon salt (to taste)

1/8 teaspoon black pepper

12 ounces (4 cups) shredded potatoes

Butter or olive oil for frying

Directions:

Combine eggs, onion, flour, baking powder, and seasonings and beat until well combined. Mix in potatoes.

Heat skillet or griddle to medium and add oil. Ladle in potato batter to 1/4 inch thickness and 6 inch diameter. Cook about 4 minutes, turn over, and cook another 4 minutes.
