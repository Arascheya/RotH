Tawny Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients:

1.25 cups cooked or canned pumpkin. Cinderella pumpkin works great!

3/4 cup sugar

1/2 tsp salt

1/4 tsp ginger

1 tsp cinnamon

1 tsp flour

2 eggs, slightly beaten

1 cup evap. milk

2 tablespoons water

1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:

Combine pumpkin, sugar, salt, spices, and flour. Add eggs and mix well. Add evap. milk, water, and vanilla.

Mix and pour into 9 inch unbaked pie shell. Bake at 400 degrees for 45-50 minutes, or until a knife comes out clean.
