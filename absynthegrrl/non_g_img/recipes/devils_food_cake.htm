Devils Food Cake

Mrs. Virgil Hanks, Wife of State Representative, Gallatin Gateway Montanna

Montana Centennial Edition Women's CookBook

Ingredients:

1 cup sour cream (not commercial)

1 cup sugar

2 eggs

2 squares chocolate

1/2 cup boiling water

1-1/2 cups cake flour

1 tsp baking soda

vanilla

Directions:

Whip cream and add sugar, eggs, and chocolate that has been melted in hot water. Add flour and soda. Beat at mixing speed on electric mixer after each addition. Bake 25-30 minutes in a 400 degree oven. Yields 2 nine inch layers.
