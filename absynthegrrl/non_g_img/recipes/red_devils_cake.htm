Red Devils Food Cake

Ingredients:

2 cups sugar

1.5 cup butter

3 eggs

1 cup sour milk (1/2 cream if desired)

2.5 cups sifted flour

2 cup angel flake coconut

1 heaping tsp soda

2/3 cup cocoa disolved in 1/5 cup boiling water

1 tsp vanilla

1/4 tsp salt

Directions:

Cream butter, add sugar, the well beaten eggs and milk. Disolve soda in a little hot waterand add to mixture. Then add disolved cocoa, flour, and vanilla.

Bake 35 to 40 minutes in a moderate (350 degree) oven. This makes a lovely red cake.
