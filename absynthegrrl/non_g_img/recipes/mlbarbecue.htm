Mom's Barbecue Sauce

Ingredients:

1 gal. catsup
1/2 gal. water
3 C. sugar
2 Tblspn salt
2 Tblspn pepper
2 drops tabasco
2 tblspn chili powder
2 tspn ground allspice
1 tspn ground cloves
1 C. white vinegar
1/2 C. liquid smoke

Directions:

Heat all ingredients to boil and simmer for 4 to 5 hours.

This is the original recipe. Mom now adds more tabasco and either puried roasted jalapenos or allepo peppers.
