Tres Leches Cake

Ingredients:

1.5 cups all purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking powder

0.5 teaspoon sea salt

6 eggs

1 cup sugar

0.25 cup water

3 teaspoons vanilla

1 - 14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk

1 - 13 oz. can evaporated milk

1 cup heavy cream

Directions:

Spray bottom and sides of 13x9 pan with cooking spray

Place flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl and whisk together.

Combine eggs and sugar in mixer and beat on high until doubled in bulk. Reduce to low speed and add water and one teaspoon of the vanilla. Mix well. On very low speed or by hand, fold dry ingredients into batter. Pour into prepared pan and bake for 35 to 40 minutes in a 350 degree oven.Cake should be firm and pulled away from the sides of the pan when done.

Remove and cool for 15 minutes.

Whisk together the sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and 1 cup of cream. Prick the cake all over with a bamboo skewer and, taking as much time as necessary, pour the milk solution on the cake until it is saturated. Refrigerate at least two hours before serving.
