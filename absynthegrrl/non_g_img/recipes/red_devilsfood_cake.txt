2 cups sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
1 cups sour milk (take half cream if desired)
    can use buttermilk from powder or carton as substitute
2-1/2 cups sifted flour
1 heaping tsp soda
2/3 cup cocoa dissolved in 1/2 cup boiling water
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp salt

Cream butter, add sugar, then well-beaten eggs and milk. Disolve soda in a little hot water and add to mixture. Then add disolved cocoa, flour, and vanilla. Bake 35-40 minutes in 350 degree oven. Makes a lovely red cake.
