Sour Cream Chocolate Cake
Mrs. Loren Casey, wife of State Representative, Wolf Point, Montanna
Montana Centennial Edition Women's CookBook

Beat 1-1/2 cups sour cream and 2 eggs together.
Sift together:
    1 cup white sugar
    1-3/4 cups flour
    pinch slat
    1 tsp soda
    5 tblspns cocoa

Sift into beaten eggs and cream. Beat well. Add 1 tsp vanilla. Bake in 350 degree oven for about 25 minutes.
