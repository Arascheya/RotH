Simple Caramel

Ingredients:

1 cup butter

1 pound (2.25 cups) brown sugar, packed

dash salt

1 cup light corn syrup

14 ounces sweetened condensed milk

1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:

Melt butter in a heavy pan. Mix in sugar, corn syrup, and salt. When well mixed, slowly stir in the milk. Cook and stir over medium heat until mix reaches 240 degrees F. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla. Pour into pan or use for recipes requiring caramel.

(for straight up caramels)pour into buttered 9x9x2 pan for mixture.
