White Cake
Mrs. Helen F. Anderson, State Senator, Wibaux, Montanna
Montana Centennial Edition Women's CookBook

1/2 cup shortening
1-1/2 cups sugar
2-1/4 cups sifted cake flour
1 cup cold water
5 egg whites
3 tsp baking powder

Cream sugar and shortening, add flour alternately with water. Add baking powder to egg whites and fold into mixture. Bake in loaf pan in a moderate (350 degree) oven.

Filling
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup sour cream
5 egg whites
1/2 cup nuts

Cook in double boiler until thick. Spread on cake when cool and cover with 7 minute icing.
