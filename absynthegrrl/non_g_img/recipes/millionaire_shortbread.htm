Millionaire Shortbread

Notes:

double recipe to use full can of milk

Substrate

Ingredients:

1/2 cup unslated butter, softened

4.5 Tablkespoons super-fine sugar

can be made by placing sugar in food processor

1 1/3 cups flour

Directions:

Preheat oven to 350. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Stir in flour with wooden spoon and press into a 10x6x1.5 pan.

Bake 20 to 25 minutes until golden brown and cool.

Toffee layer

Ingredients:

1/2 cup unsalted butter

4.5 Tablespoons superfine sugar

2 tablespoons corn syrup

1/2 can sweetned condensed milk

1/4 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:

Combine butter sugar, corn syrup, and condensed milk in a heavy sauce pan. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Boil for 5 minutes. Watch carefully, it will scortch very easily.

Stir in vanilla and quickly pour over cooled substrate, spreading evenly. Cool.

Chocolate layer

Ingredients:

7 oz. semi-sweet chocolate

Directions:

Melt chocolate and pour over toffee layer, spreading evenly. Let cool.
