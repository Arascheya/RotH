Warm Borscht - Polish style

Ingredients:

Directions:

Brown chucks of meat in pan, (beef, venison, whatever), add onions, garlic, cellery, carrots, continue cooking and browning until meat has carmelized on outsides and formed yummy glaze on pan and onions are clearish. Add potatoes, shredded beets and water to cover. Add thyme leaves, bay leaf, a bit of pepper. Simmer until beets are done. Add shredded cabbage (green or red) and cook until tender, maybe twenty minutes. Add lemon juice and a bit of sugar at the very end.

Serve with dollop of aour cream.
