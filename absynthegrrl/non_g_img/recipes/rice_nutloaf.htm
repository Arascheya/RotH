Rice-Nut Loaf

Ingredients:

3 cups cooked brown rice

2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

4 eggs, lightly beaten

1 medium onion, chopped

1 cup shredded carrot

1/2 cup Italian style bread crumbs

1/4 cup chopped walnuts

1/4 cup sunflower seeds

1/4 cup sesame seeds

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

2 cups red pasta sauce

Directions:

Preheat oven 350 degrees.

Mix all ingredients except the pasta sauce together and pack into a 9 inch oiled loaf pan.

Bake 50 to 60 minutes, cool 10 minutes, unmold, slice, and serve with pasta sauce over the top.
