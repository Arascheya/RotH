Lemon-Ricotta Pancakes with Raspberry Coulis

Ingredients

6 ounce frsh or frozen raspberries

3 tablespoons honey, or to taste

1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice

4 eggs

1 cup ricotta cheese

2 tablspoons sugar

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1/2 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest

1/8 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup flour

Instructions: Raspberry Coulis

Place raspberries, honey, and juice in blender. Blend until berries are pureed. Strain through sieve to remove seeds.

Instructions: Pancakes

Beat eggs, ricotta, sugar, vanilla, lemon zest, and salt together until well blended. Add flour and mix thoroughly.

Ladel batter onto hot, lightly grease griddle in 1/4 cup amounts. Cook as regular pancakes: waiting for small burst bubbles on top before turning.
