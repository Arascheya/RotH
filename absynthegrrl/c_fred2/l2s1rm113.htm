Level 2 Section 1 Room 113

There is a statue of a woman poised in mid-step, holding daggers in each hand, with a faint smile on her face as though she is trying to decide whether to dance in front of you or kill you. She wears a necklace of grinning skulls.

She is one of the doors to the cantina. If you touch each dagger simultaneously a door will appear next to her. It will last a minimum of 5 minutes or until everyone in the room has gone through.
