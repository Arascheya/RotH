There is a mahogany chest with a carved dragons head. On either side of the dragons head there is a pearl and a ruby.

To open the chest first the left pearl then the right pearl then the left ruby and then the right ruby must be pressed.

The chest contains 1 tiger skin worth 100 Planets, 10 doses of Green (op) Liquid (oil etherealness), 7 Doses white (trans) liquid (growth), and a secret compartment containing a circuit card #TC-24-96 which fits into a slot on the inside of the chest behind the dragon's head. It recites a message.
