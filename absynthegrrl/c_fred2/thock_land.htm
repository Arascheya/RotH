Thock Landed here

After taking massive falling damage Thock washed up here, unconcious and with very few HP left.

At the first door he comes to after awakening he will hear the following conversation. The three talkers are orcs.

1 - At last, we get some visitors.

2 - Hush, they might hear you. Besides, the old fool pays us whether we work or not.

3 - I like the pretty one. She has a wicked sense of humor.

2 - But she isn't the one paying us.

1 - I don't think the old guy likes her much anymore.

2 - Shut up you. You know it isn't wise to talk about him
