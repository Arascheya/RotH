Labyrinth Dust

This dust is a fine powdery feeling grey dust. It has the feel of the perfect dust on a country road in the warm sun. It makes you want to walk bare foot through it, just for the sensuous treat of it. The dust moves and flows into a smooth surface. Tracks disappear in minutes after they are made. The dust is anywhere from one to three meters deep depending on location. Dust skimmers swim through the dust in random patterns.

All non-living things will sink into the dust. This includes dead bodies, so looting must be accomplished fairly quickly.

To allow non-living items to remain available for the various visitors to the labyrinth Tyana can activate a protective field to keep them on the surface. If it is an item to be taken, the field will drop as soon as a character picks up the item. For stationary items the field remains in place.
