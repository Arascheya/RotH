26c - Controller's Chambers - Gate room

Hut Room Details

This is the Controller's personal Gate. It will activate only at the will of the Controller and can be set to the Twelve Factols in Centrys where She... can sometimes be found, a shadow-instance of the Hut, the Great Repository, or any other location where there is a recieving Gate. The NavSys will specify a default location depending on the location of the Hut.
