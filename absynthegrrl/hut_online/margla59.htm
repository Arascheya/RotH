59 - Angelique - Desert Home

Hut Room Details

This set of rooms mirrors Imotep's house, where Ange spent her late childhood and adulthood. In the bed area is a stair leading down into the crypt bedroom. The main entry leads out into the desert area. It is set up as a home around a central courtyard, with living for servants, storage, kitchens, and general living.
