25 - Plant and Herb Garden

Hut Room Details

Each section is high and majikally lit with the conditions necessary for the plants in it.

A - Stun Cones and similar vegetation. Mostly non-jungle, daylight flesh eaters.

B - desert, dry climate food plants

C - mediteranean climate food plants

D - tropical climate food plants

E - contains the most dangerous and effective of the plants.

The food plant sections are where the favorite fresh food items for the crew are grown. More sections can be added as needed to support additional favorites and growing conditions.
