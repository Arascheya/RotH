44 - Koria's Rooms - Bed Chamber

Hut Room Details

This suite is done in brooding gothic, heavy, dark, and creepy. The bed is a gigantic four poster with heavy black curtains with red roses embroidered on it, blood red bed spread with black roses embroidered on it. A brooding armoire holds clothes and a large table contains an ornate mirror and holds various items of jewelry and the like. Flickering candles light the room from wrought iron wall, table, and floor holders. The window is covered with heavy black velvet drapes. Outside the window is a dead garden with plant stems still sticking up into the roiling storm cloud filled sky. Subtle rose incense and a vague hint of old house and stone permeates the room.
