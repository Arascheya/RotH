21 - The Conservatory

CH, hemispherical dome 275' high; IL, dim, equals twilight or overcast day (see below; ND).

Hut Room Details

A conservatory of rare and exotic plnats. It is built on a series of platforms spanning a deep void. The air is humid and warm with a heavy miasma of plant perfumes. Soft animal sounds come from the clusters of thick growth.
