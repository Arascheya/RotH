26b - Controller's Chambers - sitting room and Gate

Hut Room Details

This is a spacious and airy chamber with scattered low tables, large pillows, and rich tapestries. On one of the low tables is the Hut Master Key. To the left of the door from the stairs is the door to the Throne Room. Near the wall to the right is the Sphynx Gate. This is the Controller's personal Gate. It will activate only at the will of the Controller and can be set to the Twelve Factols in Centrys where She... can sometimes be found, a shadow-instance of the Hut, the Great Repository, or any other location where there is a recieving Gate. The NavSys will specify a default location depending on the location of the Hut.

There is a balcony, directly across from the entry, that overlooks the Courtyard. This is the second level balcony visible in the Courtyard image.
