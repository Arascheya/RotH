17 - Formal Dining Hall

CH, 10' flat in room A, 20' in room B; IL, I CL in room A, see below for room B.

Hut Room Details

This area is a large, formal dining hall. To one side there is an archway to a smaller room with a table for two or three in a more intimate setting. This alcove overlooks the Formal Garden. On the table is a purple cloth bound book with a quill pen and a bottle of red ink sitting next to it.

One end of the dining room has a single door, the other has a small hallway with two stairwway and three doors.

EXITS:

1 - Hallway Stairs up - Mountains

2 - Hallway Door - Kitchen

3 - Hallway Door - Conservatory

4 - Hallway Door - Recreation and Dance

5 - Hallway Stairs down - Main Stairway

6 - Single Door - Throne Room
