Gameplay for Online Version of the Hut

Concepts

Define "what this is" and what it is not

What it is

adventure based

players see differences in story depending on when they enter

What it is not

a first person shooter

an experience point/money grub

a static, everyone sees exactly the same thing, canned set of adventures

game play features

"leveling" controlled by actions you take

need to make it less of an end-all-be-all and more of a background tracked item

player actions effect story direction and progress

must be in-game ways of dealing with Griefers

diary system - allows player to take notes in the Hut Diary, the one Irena left them

Key will allow toggling a "logging" mode where conversations with/around the Player will be transcribed to a Book of Conversations available in the Controller's Sitting room

Consider mechanism to export any Player Authorable book data to a text file

need to have story-line entry hooks defined at player start. make sure there is something to keep them busy right from the start, try to reduce the "i'm bored" kvetching - even if scenarios aren't completely done, make sure there are paths and activities that the players can launch into. Also, NPCs will have zero tolerance for whining - either ignoring the PC or hurting them bad enough to force a return to the Hut if the NPC is nasty enough
