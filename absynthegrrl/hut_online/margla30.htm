30 - Alchemical Laboratory

CH, 10' flat overall; IL, I CL centered on ceiling.

Hut Room Details

The alchemical laboratory is outfitted with most of the standard equipment that one would expect to find here (lab tables, shelves full of glassware, retorts, etc.). Explorers will find 3-12 potions here; some of them poisonous or cursed, but all completely indistinguishable from one another. Rare powders, liquids, and devices may also be found here, though unless one knows what to look for, most of this material will be ignored.

Located on one of the shelves under the lab bench is a Spirit Binding Crystal.

NOT VALID for Madison scenario - Scattered about on the table and leaning in a corner are the following items: a katana, a shortsword, a dagger, and a ring. These are set so that each character may pick one item. If a character picks up one of the other items after choosing one, the first item selected will be randomly transported to a different location in the Hut.
