61 - Waterfall Room

Hut Room Details

This mirrors the waterfall room near the living areas of CF2 - Level 2. It is an adjunct to the main bathing chamber, providing hot (39.7c - 103.5f), warm (37.7c - 100f), and cool (26.7c - 80f) waterfalls and soaking pools. The room is lit by column candles, in brass floor holders. The various pools have back water sections without much current and the areas ner the waterfalls, with stronger currents.
