in game debug console

1) Press the "~" key to open the ingame console

2) Type: DebugMode 1

3) Type: debugtext

4) Type: printlocalvars

printlocalvars, however, will only display the localvars assigned your PC.

Additionaly, there is the printglobalvars command that displays the localvars assigned to the module object.

I'm afraid I don't have a quick answer for displaying the localvars of a targeted creature or item ... I don't see such a command in the list of commands. That would certainly make a nice addition to the ingame console.

The one time I needed to see the locals on some other object, I was able to simply adjust my script to assign the locals I wanted to see to my PC instead.
