44 - Koria's Rooms - Sitting and Entry

Hut Room Details

Large high backed armchairs are drawn up to a low heavy table near a fireplace. Flickering candles light the room from wrought iron wall, table, and floor holders. The window is covered with heavy black velvet drapes. Outside the window is a dead garden with plant stems still sticking up into the roiling storm cloud filled sky. Subtle rose incense and a vague hint of old house and stone permeates the room.
