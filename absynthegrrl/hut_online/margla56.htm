56 - The Graveyard

CH, hemispherical dome 275' high; IL, dim, equals twilight or overcast day (see below; ND).

Hut Room Details

This graveyard is situated in a deep forest. It is a memorial to those the the crew of the Hut have lost. New markers will spring into being when a crew member begins to grieve the loss of someone.

The graveyard is a section of the Lake area. It is a direct path from the rest of the Lake rather than being a Hut link.

Contains memorials to Valerie Cantrazel and Sarah Draybin on the central monument in the larger clearing. In the master version, Valen will light candles to them on their birth and death days.

EXITS:

1 - Main Stairs - one way return link

2 - Lake - direct path
