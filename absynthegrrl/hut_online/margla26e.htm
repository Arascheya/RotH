26e - Controller Private Stair

Hut Room Details

This is a worn stone stairwell linking the Controller's Bed Chamber, Sitting Room, Toilet, and Courtyard.

EXITS:

1 - Arched Opening (top of stairs, right) - Controller's Bed Chamber

2 - Arched Opening (top of stairs, left) - Private Lav

3 - Arched Opening (middle landing, right) - Sitting Room

4 - Arched Opening into short passage (bottom of stairs, right) - Controller's Bed Chamber
