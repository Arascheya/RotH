26a - Controller's Chambers - bedroom

Hut Room Details

This room is a spacious bed chamber with a large. low bed on a raised platform. White qauze curtains surround it and the scent of water and flowers drifts in on breeze puffs from the grilled windows. Large cushions are scattered around the floor and rugs cover the intricate tile floor near the bed.

There is a balcony that overlooks the Courtyard. This is the screened balcony-like window in the image of the Courtyard
