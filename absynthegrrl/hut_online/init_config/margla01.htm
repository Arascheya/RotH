01 - Core Room - ZeroRM

Hut Room Details

This is a techno heaven, or hell, depending on your perspective. The floor is crowded with various cabinets, racks, boxes, and pieces of techno gear. There is a small console like affair near one corner of the room with a chair and a bench nearby. Cables, conduits, tubes, and pipes run everywhere, and underlying everything is a deep throbbing rumble, almost like the purring of an enormous cat.

Those with any skill at power perception are almost overwhelmed by the magic energies coursing through this room, intertwined into the techno equipment. Many pieces of equipment are marked with various forms of writing, some Ancient runes, others some other types of script not recognized as anything you have encountered.

On what looks to be the main screen is displayed text in Ancient Runes.
