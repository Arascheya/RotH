* Many Born *

Faces in the mirror

Vignettes of places I've never been, now

Old pains, old joys

Wind blown hair on a high mountain

Death in a lover's arms

Shared dangers

A mother watching her child watching her burn

The joys of childbirth

The horrors of war

Countless battles, countless deaths

Nameless worlds, how many races, how many lives?

Windows into the past stretching ever back

Remembering - brings continuity - death does not need to be

Feared

To not remember would be as final death, unbearable

How could one live, assuming only one lifetime?

* Who Am I? *

Lost in defenses, trapped by my own shields

Screams in the dark, pain at odd moments

"Hold me" I whimper, "Don't fucking touch me!" I scream
