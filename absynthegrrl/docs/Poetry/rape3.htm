* Rape - Exploration 3 *

Hard table

Rough hands

Rope and wrists


Face down wood grain

Sandpaper roughness

Deep tears and pain

Screams echo


Slaps, fists, "Shut up slut!"

Another one, tearing, burning

Smell of blood

Slick warmth runs


Struggle, try to turn

Sharp pain distant

Agony and fire

Through back, through chest


Pinned to table

Old knife, my blood

Pull and tear

Door and haze,

So far away


In distance voices

Closer, light, others ahead

Fall, cannot rise

Try to move


One sees me

Silent horror

Whose hand twitches?

Whose blood is this?


Blackness, peace at last...
