Rape Trilogy

* Rape *

hot pain, cold hands, bright knives

laughing faces, frantic hands

deep hurts, tearings, pullings

hardness, sharp pushes, raw


pain from a distance

voices from far away

blackness...


rough boards, cold air

so COLD, shivering


torn clothes wrapped around

aches, emptiness, dullness

so COLD


staggering, ignored

hooker, quick walk, hardness

watchful, quick glances, SEES me


words, I don't hear

guided, head down, empty

so COLD


undressed, distance, not feeling

hot bath, washed, wrapped in cloth

distant touches, empty movement, a bed

so COLD


hand held in hers

a touch, non threatening

a hint ... of ... WARMTH



* Rape 2 *

rape

a simple word

countless words have been used

to try and describe its horror


fear, pain, humiliation, violation

just more words

a glimpse at the feelings

not the feelings


random, senseless, domination

it's about power

psychobabble to sanitize it

they say

give the rapist another chance


i say

why?

what chance did i have?

how do i get my second chance?


trust

you talk to me of trust?

why? why should i?


every new encounter

a cold knot of fear

will it happen again?

will i survive again?

dare i trust this one?


always i wonder why

why does it have to be this way?



* Rape 3 *

Hard table

Rough hands

Rope and wrists


Face down wood grain

Sandpaper roughness

Deep tears and pain

Screams echo


Slaps, fists, "Shut up slut!"

Another one, tearing, burning

Smell of blood

Slick warmth runs


Struggle, try to turn

Sharp pain distant

Agony and fire

Through back, through chest


Pinned to table

Old knife, my blood

Pull and tear

Door and haze,

So far away


In distance voices

Closer, light, others ahead

Fall, cannot rise

Try to move


One sees me

Silent horror

Whose hand twitches?

Whose blood is this?


Blackness, peace at last...
