After

Bright light shone in my eyes and a warm breeze tickled my hair. For long moments I didn't remember anything of how I had gotten here. I heard water in the distance. The sound of it flooded my senses bringing thirst like I'd never known. Stretching, I opened my eyes and looked for it. I saw a fountain bubbling with crystal clear water, bathed in the warmth of afternoon sunlight.

As I looked, I knew I didn't want to drink. If I drank I'd forget everything - Annie, Shelly, all our times together. Just then I saw Annie getting up and moving to the fountain. As I shouted, "Don't drink.." she reached forward and gulped a huge draught. I curled forward into a ball as I saw her image shimmer and fade. I was still calling brokenly, "Don't drink, please don't drink..." when a soft but powerful voice spoke, "Do you love her so little that you would ask her to be untrue to herself?"
