Reminisces of a Military Couple

The following segments represent a series of memories from the viewpoint of Mirya Koren, a major in the Armed Forces of an imaginary world, not unlike 1990's Earth. These segments are written as if she were viewing a photo album and telling stories based on the pictures she saw there. Each memory would trigger another and thus point to another picture and story.

The following order is chronological.

To view these in the order they were originally written start with the last segment and follow the embedded links.
