My name is Alexandra, Alex to those who know me, and I am the Recorder for the Dark Carnival. How I became the Redorder is a story for another time. This is a series of conversations around the campfire at the Dark Carnival Winter Quarters. The Winter Quarters are located in a heavily wooded, mountainous region of the Sphere of Concordant Discord, The Discord, as it is commonly called. The Discord is a hollow sphere,with regions located on its inner surface. Floating in the center of The Discord is Sphere, the city at the center of the multi-verse. The outer surface of The Discord connects to various Realms and places throughout the multi-verse.

Before we get to the list of the recorded conversations, I should say something about the participants.

Three of the participants are the Three Sisters, the only true gods in the multi-verse. The Sisters are:

Stability, she is the face of created things and order. She is believed to reside in Sphere

Possibility, she is the face of the darkness and the potential held in unrealized things.

Balance, she is the face of Balance between the various polarities of the multi-verse. If any polarity gains too much power, they could end the multi-verse. Balance,or the Keeper of Balance, or The Keeper, keeps watch and moves to prevent this.

Another common participant is Ishra, Demon Queen of Spiders. Ishra began as a godling, but through a series of unintended events has risen to near-godess status. Ishra is a very close and true friend of The Keeper.

Old Yara, the Mistress of the Dark Carnival,is also a common participant. She suggested that I record the conversations and the others did not disagree.
