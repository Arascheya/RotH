The Other

He stood at the window, staring out into the clouds surrounding the city. It was a fair day. What little wind there was stirred the clouds without swirling them up to block the sun. The beauty of the day was lost on him, however. Somehow the captain had escaped with her ship of fools before he could corner her. She was the last known survivor of Gilshebra and her family had held the book. Only a few knew the significance of that particular volume, the knowlegdge of forbidden magic, from the time before the collapse. It's very existence threatened the order of the orthodoxy. It's recovery was critical.

He spun to face the two men in the room with him. "Later you will answer how she escaped you. For now, gather your most trusted and persue her. Do not fail in this."
