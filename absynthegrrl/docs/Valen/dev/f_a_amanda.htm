Character - Amanda

Amanda - taller than Farni, but somewhat short, probably no more than 5'4" or 5' 5",long blonde hair, waist length, sim. Amanda, Knute's daughter, build lighter, but still somewhat solid, medium boned, green eyes. Wicked sense of humor, retiring at beginning, fairly together by time with Farni, definitely her own person. Hooked up with Servalan because saw good in her, cared for her, Servalan headed places, filled a void at the time. She gave Servalan everything she could, selling herself out in the process, still it didn't seem to be enough. She was just beginning to wake up to the fact when the Star One crisis occurred and she left when Servalan disappeared.

For Servalan Amanda was a convenient toy when she tired of the boys, a controllable companion and NOT Avon. After Servalan Amanda swore to herself she would not sell herself in another relationship. For several years she avoided deep or lasting commitments, then she met Farni...

Amanda likes to cook when she has the time. She plays some type of musical instrument
