General Ideas

Fatimah set out with three friends to visit Miritha's Hope, another village near the Kharatoum Habaya. They found the village destroyed and grotesque things prowling the wreckage. These cretures were shuttling equipment into a deep tunnel, leading to the non-pressurized Tube Train tunnel under the mountians. This section of Tube Train tunnels was a dual pair, one open to atmospher and the other still functional. The Dark Forces had discovered it and were setting about using it for covert travel.
