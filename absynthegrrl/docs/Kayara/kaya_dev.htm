/* set during the big evac - this could be either a full narative or small bits and pieces from the time */

"We aren't going to get all of them out, you know." Kayara said as she stared out into the deepening night.

"I know," Elentaryn answered in a tired voice. "What are your estimates?"

"Thirty million most likely. We don't have enough space on the ships, in suspension, or transportation to get them here. Then there are the ones that won't leave."

"Still? Even after the explanations?"

"Even after. In some it's a death wish. The ones that amaze me are those that think they will survive it."

"We could force them..."

"To what end? They'd end up taking spaces that could be used for those that want to leave. Besides it's not our way."

"Has it started yet?"

"The looting and the disorder? Not yet. So far most people are well enough informaed and things are still moving along. I expect it to fall apart as soon as they realize they all won't make it."
