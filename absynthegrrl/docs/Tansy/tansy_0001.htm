The Beginning

I grew up in a poorer than peasants family. My father was a thief, although not a very good one. He had his hand removed when I was a baby when he tried to pick the pocket of the wrong man. My mother was a sweet but simple woman who put up with my father's schemes and moods. She had apprenticed me to the village healer and wise woman, Meg, called Mad Meg by those seeking the blessings of the priest Oswald. Meg had a second aprentice, a girl a few years older than me, named Gwendolyn, who was the miller's daughter. She was more conventional than I was, but not quite as quick to learn. Meg taught both of us to defend ourselves, on the quiet, since we could all be burned for it.

Everyone in the family was expected to steal when possible. My earliest memories are of my father showing me how to slip small items off merchant tables. He would explain new methods and targets as I grew older. I expected to continue until I married and had children of my own to care for.

I was sixteen when my father changed all that. He sold me to a traveling peddler for the price of a horse. At first I couldn't believe it. It wasn't until my hands were tied behind me and a lead rope tied around my waist that it became real. I tried to protest, yelling to anyone nearby but the town constable and the priests sided with my father. As we approached the edge of the village I struggled against the rope, pulling, hauling, and yanking against it until my captor slammed his fist into my guts, doubling me over. "Hold still, cunt. You're mine now."

Things became much worse once we left the village. My new owner dragged me into the nearby forest and to a clearing where another woman was tied to the trees. She stared at the ground in front of her, blood stained the front of her dress and her face showed bruises and small cuts. While I watched, a man stood from near the fire, walked over to her, grabbed her legs, and hauled her to a laying position. He shoved her dress up to her waist, hoisted his tunic, and entered her. She didn't even move, just laying there as he sawed in and out of her. As he neared climax, he slapped her, shouting, "Move damn you." She remained motionless as he slapped her face, rocking her head back and forth. He stiffened, grunted, and slumped down long moments later. He hauled her dress back down and left her to struggle back into a sitting position.

My purchaser shoved his face near mine and said, "I hope you're more spirited than her." He pushed me to an empty tree and tied my rope to it. "Hey boys, we have a fresh one. I get her first, then the rest of you can draw lots for the next in line."

I tried to fight against him when he came for me, but it did no good. He hauled my legs apart and shoved into me. Pain exploded in unbearable waves. His residue let the next one slip in with less pain.

This time my body started to respond sooner, but I didn't, drawing back deeper into the recesses of my mind. I would have prayed, but I never believed what the church had said. It preached obedience and humility, yet its servants and the nobility used the rest of us as they liked, in ways counter to what they said they believed. With the reduced pain I drifted farther, not really called back by the slow responses my body was giving.

I wanted to give up, relax into madness, and forget what was happening. For a moment, it felt like I could, then the rage came, bubbling up from a dark pit at the center of me. Ice flowed from the black, chilling and calming me, separating the pain of my flesh from what I was.

Alone in the darkness I started to plot how to get free. I had seen one of them lead the other girl off into the woods when she had to make water. It took longer than expected which meant either she did more than make water or the thug took advantage of the time with her.

My captors started in on the barrel of beer as soon as they were finished with me. I lay there raw, aching, oozing, and realizing if I was going to empty my water, it would have to be soon, before they were too drunk to lead me away. Blood erupted, splashing over my hand and into my face. I twisted and even more blood sprayed out. He sank back, blood spraying, then flowing, from the long tear in his inner thigh.

I was less prepared for it than for the shakes and upset. I raised his dagger to the stars and almost howled in triumph until I remembered the camp.

I turned to run, then thought of the other girl, with her sightless eyes and empty expression. The icy anger returned and turned me back toward the camp. I circled, to approach from where the trees were thickest and almost ran straight into the sentry. My father had taught me well in the art of quiet and I made it to the tree he was leaning against without any sound betraying me. He was half asleep, leaning against the tree and looking more toward camp than back along the path from the road.

He became aware of me when the knife cut deep into his throat. I had cut from behind, but when the blood sprayed I darted in front of him, wanting to feel his loathsome life flood out. The shakes weren't as bad this time, although I tried to empty my guts again. I grabbed his knife and started back to the camp.

I took my time, staring at the camp from the thick trees. Most of the men were half asleep from drink. No one seemed to have missed the one I left with. The other girl was near the edge of the light. The man watching her was flat on his back, snoring. It took a long while, but I managed to work my way around and slip up behind the tree she was tied to. She didn't even blink when I leaned around and whispered, "I am going to cut you loose and give you a knife. Then I'm going to take care of these pigs." Her hands dropped limply to her sides and refused to close over the knife handle. I left it near her and slid toward the man near her. I was able to cut his throat without him making any noise.

Made bolder by my success, I darted to the next closest figure. I wasn't as lucky and he got off a partial shout before I finished. Two stumbled to their feet and staggered toward me. I danced back, but stumbled on a rock, going down hard. The first one landed on me and I was able to get the knife into his leg. He rolled off, howling in pain as blood pulsed from the wound. The second man kicked at my face. I twisted, but his boot caught me with a glancing blow. My vision swam as I tried to regain my senses. His fist swung toward my face and I dropped back, just managing to get out of the way.

He screamed and fell back with the other girl driving the dagger into his throat from behind. Her first cut was too shallow, but the second wasn't. That left three men, fanning out and facing us. The leader spoke, "Put the knives down girlies, before Sven puts arrows into you." The third man was stringing his bow. With a desperate shout I charged at him. Pain exploded from my side and I stumbled, but managed to reach the bowman. I let my momentum carry me forward and into him. My blade entered just under his ribs and ripped down to his groin as I fell. His insides spilled out over me, as I slid into the dark.

I didn't die, but I didn't wake up for a long while either. Strange dreams, dark and twisted, haunted me as I struggled to wake up. A white haired woman with red eyes, spiders of impossible sizes, warmth, cold, and voices, speaking in strange languages, all drifted through. The one that remained solidly in my mind was the dream of small spiders crawling under my skin, in and through my insides, chewing and weaving webs and chewing some more. I tried to wake enough to scream, but I couldn't.

The distinctive smell of long dead bodies woke me. I was on my back, staring up at the leaves of a large tree. To my right the girl was struggling to wakefulness. As I slowly sat up I saw the bodies of our captors spread out where they had fallen. Animals had started to strip them of flesh and it looked like days had passed since their deaths.
