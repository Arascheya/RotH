"Jump, I can't hold the gate for long." Kaltereth's voice echoed in Shandi's ears as she landed in a roll in a dark alley. Behind her there was a faint pop as the gate closed. Her eyes darted around taking in the tall buildings, the brick, stone and dust. She relaxed as she saw the alley was empty of life.

Slowly the awareness faded from her eyes as she slipped back into the almost asleep state she had come to know. With a vacant half smile she began walking down the alley toward the widening out she saw in the distance. She wa half way there when a shadow detached from a nitch and moved to intercept her. "Well now, what do we have here?" The man took in the thin, average height woman in front of him. She was dressed in simple dark loose fitting pants and a tunic. When she slowly turned to look at him she had a blank, vacant look on her face. "Maybe we should have a little fun. What's your name, girlie?"

"Not girl, revenge. I revenge."

"What kind of name is that? No matter, we can still have some fun together," he said as he moved closer to her. Her face lit up into a delighted smile as he reached out to slide his hand under her tunic and gave a sharp gasp as pain exploded in his chest. He saw the the woman raise a bloody chunk of meat to her lips and take a big bite out of it as he fell backwards to the stone of the alley. The last words he heard were said in a strangely child-like voice, "I like the heart blood best."

Shandi dropped the remains of his heart on top of his cooling chest and continued her walk down the alley.
