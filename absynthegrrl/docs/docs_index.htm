Tales from the Hood

The Hood is an isolated area and a center of poverty and crime. It is cut off from the rest of the city by the highways that pass over it, by the toxic lakes and streams to the west of it, by the ruined factories and highway to the south and by the lake to the east.

Many call the old factory housing buildings home. Rich landlords from the more affluent parts of the city control the properties, collecting rents but refusing to maintain them. Criminal gangs of various factions and strengths controlits streets and keep a kind of truce going.

These are the stories of some of the residents.
