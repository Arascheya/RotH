Nyissana - New Game

Nyissana staggered into the office, coffee cup clutched in one hand. She dropped into her chair, leaned back and stared blank-eyed at her computer screen. After a few more sips of coffee, she leaned forward and logged in. Flashing on the screen was the meeting notice that had brought her in early. She rose with a muttered, "oh, right", and made her way through the cube farm to the conference room, coffee still tightly in hand.

"Nyis, Nyissa, hey Nyissana!"

It took the third call before she focused on the woman at the head of the table. "Jaime? What are you doing here?"

Nyissana was on her feet moving toward the woman as she spoke. Jaimie grabbed her in a tight hug and said, "You'll see," in a mischievious voice. Nyissana walked back to her coffee and sat back down.

Bob, the CEO, walked in next. Once the last of the stragglers had entered, he began, "As you know we've been looking for a new concept to develop. We had several ideas but no funders. Ms. Solitaire has come to us from Rani Corporation with a property they want to develop. We don't have to take it, but they will pay all development costs and have guaranteed a generous profit stream for at least three years after launch. Ms. Solitaire, if you would be so kind."

"Certainly Mr. Eveson. Rani Corp would like to hire your team to produce a new MMO for us. It is based on a property belonging to one of our employees. The game is based in a multi-verse containing magic and technology, ancient civilizations, and conflict between power groups. I could stand up here and talk for hours about the property, but it would be more effective to pass out the Non-Disclosure Agreements, then make the data available to you. Once you've had a few days to review it, I can return and see what was decided." She looked straight at Nyissana, "Nyis, you will recognize the material." She passed around a stack of forms, pretty much the standard non-disclosure boiler-plate text. Most of the employees signed them immediately, being used to the forms. Once all theforms were returned Jaime set a portable drive on the table. "Go ahead and load this onto a secure server and start your review. Bob will call me when you are ready with your answer."

Nyissana started back to her office, to be stopped by Jaime's call, "Nyis, got a couple of moments?"
