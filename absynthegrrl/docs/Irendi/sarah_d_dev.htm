her mom, Alice Draybin, worked for Artemis Gideon of Gideon Properties, one of the worst slum-lords

Alice was going to turn him in, however, she contacted the wrong officers and was raped and killed instead

Sarah witnessed part of it, including the faces of the killers

Sarah ran, loosing herself in the area around her home until the corrupt police launched a hunt for her

she kept running, driven furher and further by the police until she stumbled into The Hood

terrified, because she knew what it meant, she hid in the highest part of an abandoned factory until Jaime came across her

Jaime took her home and from there Sarah latched onto the enforcement arm of the Headmaster's organization

she has a head for tactics and combat and a coldness that serves her well

Sarah finds it hard to connect with people because of serious trust issues

Sarah and Jaime became best friends

Jaime didn't have close friends because she was the daughter of the Headmaster

Sarah could see past that because of her circumstances
