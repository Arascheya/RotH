/* discussing the need for a Tac Team/Intell Force */

They were gathered in the crew lounge, seated in their chairs, circling the inscribed pentagram. "We need to recruit a tactical team we can send into the more complicated situations," Irena said.

"Why? Can't you pick up what we need with the omniscience you have as the Balance Keeper?" Krystal asked.

"Omniscience is a three edged weapon. Too much and I can't sort the falling leaf from the dying planet. Too little and I lose the big picture. The third edge is any sentient can alter what is happening by their actions as well. A small group of trained beings could slip in, research potential recruits, scan the locale for issues, and conver up any recruitment. It would free us up to deal with the bigger issues."

/* Aztol - either shortly before or shortly after aquiring the Spider Ship */

"The leader is being clever, staying out of sight or in large groups of retainers. An assassination won't be easy to achieve," Krystal reported.

"The solution is simple. We kill the leaders, then kill the next ones to take charge. We keep doing this until we get a leadership we can use or until there are none left. In either case, we have access to what we want," Aztol answered.

"But what about the innocents?" Krystal asked.

"Innocents? That's a laugh. All of them are guilty of something, besides, humans breed like rabbits. Why should we care about a few that get in the way? There are always plenty more."
