Tales of the Hut

Keepers of the Balance

These are the adventures of Sophia Nelson in the Realms of the Hut. The Hut is a strange device that is able to cross dimensions and time in the multi-verse devised for pen and paper fantasy role playing.

The worlds of the Hut span dimensions and time from the beginnings of a galactic empire to the shattered remains of the same.

The original game used rules from ADND, Arduin, Traveler, and Rune Quest original rules. As a result the stories will have that sword and sorcery, fantasy role playing game feel to them.

The scenarios, worlds, items, and characters portrayed here are the property of the blog's author and the gamers who played in the multi-verse over the years. The game has faded into the depths of time, however, the characters refuse to go quietly into retirement. Thus was born this blog.
