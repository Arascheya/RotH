NPCs - look at Gamasutra article on Characters in Game as a reminder of how to do this better

Wizard Lord faction

Lady Ayrthane

Hard, cold as nails, - think Lady - it appears she seeks the Artifact for war, in truth she seeks it as protection from Shalmar, a greedy, blood-thirsty Lord to the south of her realm

Mogron

The perfect toady, but with a brain, Lady A respects him enough to listen if he raises a serious issue. Mogron is a capable wizard in his own right, just nowhere near as powerful as his lord. Mogron is a rather talkative sort when not in the presence of Lady A. He is a strong supporter because he knows the stakes.

Commander Elzeth

The head of Lady A's forces and her most trusted retainer. Elzeth is having second thoughts about her Lord and her actions, particularly this quest to recover one of the great weapons from the cataclysm. A careful PC could influence her into removing the Perimeter Wards and possibly revealing Lady A's true purpose. Charisma is a good first step, however, charm and no-bulshit is a good second. Conversations need to be written so the changing of her position is not an immediately obvious option.

Others faction

Mishana

succubus, who has forgotten most of her background. She has an item which allows her to retain human shape. If it is stolen from her she will instantly revert. Sometimes she will revert to relax. While one of the test subjects she learned enough healing to make her valuable to the residents of hovelton. Mishana lives in a twisted tangle of the under tunnels, trying to feel a safety that doesn't exit

Mishana knew of Tonie's sister and her name, but was instructed to keep it quiet until she gives the key to the PC. She can tell the PC about her mom and a bit about Dorotea.

Yantara

the rogue orc twin - she is the brighter of the two

Yintara

the basher of the twins, she is always ready to rumble, particularly against outsiders

Maritya

crazy tya, she talks to her birds and mumbles in bits of vision. if she can be focused, she is actually a capable seer and can provide hints and clues to the purposes of Lady A and the exact location of the artifact. Maritya lives at the top of the ruined tower, up with her birds.

Maritya fears and despises all Wizard Lords. She has forgotten that she knew Dorotea before everything started. She will council the PC to avoid any interactions with Wizard Lords for fear of enslavement or worse.

Barreth

old now, Barreth knew the under-tunnels well in his youth. He serves a barely acceptable brew in his under-hovel establishment and can be prompted to talk with the incentive of a new item or a sack or two of truly fresh grain. He knows of a back way into the vault area and could be persuaded to reveal it for a cut of the non-Artifact items from the vault and a particularly good trinket as an up front payment. this back way has the final vault key stashed in a locked location.

Mom

Born Tonie Ayrthane, she is a silent character, known only from her diaries and notes, she was the older sister of Lady Dorotea Ayrthane

Module Description

Life in the hovels around the ruined city of Tharaneta is hard, tedious, and relentless. Days are spent searching the ruined city for minor items to sell to the few traders brave enough to venture beyond the Wizard Lord holdings. Rumors hint that a major artifact, which laid waste to the city, resides somewhere in the ruins. No one in the hovels truly believes it until two Wizard Lords arrive.
