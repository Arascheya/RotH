Chapter 4

Alyssa awoke suddenly, a chill along her back. She rolled, looking for Laura and found only empty bed instead of a warm body and the hay where they had gone to sleep. The barn loft had been replaced by a large room, the hay with an enormous curtained bed, and the rich smells of the barn with a faint hint of flowers on a soft breeze.
