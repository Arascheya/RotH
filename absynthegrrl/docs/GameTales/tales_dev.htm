/* Alyssa's mom speaks about meeting Irena */

I was about your age when Irena and Krystal came out of the woods. It was sunset, the fall chill was in the air, and I had slipped out of the kitchen to calm down after another fight with your grandmom. Two figures stumbled down the dirt track leading up the valley toward the mad hermit's place and the high mountains beyond. For a moment the tales of bandits and worse had me backing toward the door until I saw them stumble. I dashed over to help them and found they were two women, leaning on each other for support. They had dark fine wool blankets pulled around themselves. Dried blood stained their faces and the odd clothing they wore under the blankets. Exhaustion poured off them as the swayed in the night air. The woman on the right took everything in with a direct, penetrating look. She spoke strange words to the dark woman on her left who spoke in halting Lotus Tongue, "We seek a healer. We have seriously injured friends."

I am shamed to say I hesitated for long moments, all my father's warnings playing about stranges, but in the end I replied, "I am an accomplished healer. Show me to those in need."

"Please hurry," the dark one whispered. "I didn't have enough to heal everyone." They turned and hobbled back down the road.

"You could stay here..." I said to the dark woman.

"I am the only one who can speak to you."

"Where are you from? What happened?"

"Later, after I rest."

I saw Irena again at the final battle of the Hell Gate Wars. Those of us with troups in the battle were meeting to decide the final strategy. It was just a holding action, a distraction, while the Heroes slipped in to seal the Gate, but we did want to minimize our losses. We had just gathered in my tent when Irena walked in. She smiled at me and took an empty chair at the table and sprawled out in a lazy poze.

Ilsandia looked her up and down as she settled in another chair. 'I don't remember seeing you. What force do you represent?'
