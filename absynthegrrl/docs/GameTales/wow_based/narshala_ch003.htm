Once I arrived, it wasn't enough to look at it, I had to try it as well. With a single touch the magic of it twisted me through space and deposited my in a ruined courtyard. Dark grey skies spit a chill rain through the shattered roof. An overgrown courtyard lay beyond the only open door and a forlorn wind howled softly through shattered stone.

I followed a group of these creatures through the door and past shattered statues, bricked up doors, and through ruined chambers until the passed through a door between two grotesque figures. Each one pulsed and shimmered with a sickly green aura. In their hands were enormous clubs. Glazed, bleary eyes peered from the sliding flesh of their ruined faces. Bones stuck out from their rib cages and viscera hung from gaping abdominal wounds.
