Moved Troll start to Darkspear Isle, beginning training starts there - Hunter trainer is Ortezza - Zro's parents are Vol'jin and Vanira - hunter trainer, Ortezza, is older sister - Echo Isles grow the very best ganj because of the raptor droppings that fertilize the island - Kijara and Tegashi grow ganj as a second profession, next to raptor care - in story, replace older sister in Klahra's village with grandfather, Canaga Earthcaller

Techyan loved the feel of the undercity slime on her flesh - it cools the burning desire to let magic run wild through her. If she can't bath i it at least once a week she starts to get crabby, crazy, and starts to physically cool down as her body cries out for magical energy. She will slow to a torporous movement, then fall and become fetal, pass-out, and die - resurrection will only partly reset the clock on the chilling effect

Trolls returned to Echo Isles five years before Zro heads out of the village. She was 12 at the time.
