Zroteyla - Origins

I was born the second child, of three, to the troll chief Vol'jin and his mate Vanira. When the shattering happened and the land changed, a new Horde warchief was selected. Some of his policies were not to my father's liking and he led our people to the Echo Isles, where we rebuilt the ruins into our new villages. There was one in particular, Klahraka, an orc girl of my age, that I missed most. She was an odd one, quiet, a bit cold, but more adventurous than the others. We would sneak out, into the rough areas around the orc village, and spy on the other residents. Klahra was much better at it than I was. If we had to run, it would be because I had been spotted.

I walked back into Klahraka's village, after my first solo hunt, covered in scratches and bites. Once I presented the scorpid tail stinger to my older sister and hunting trainer, Sen'jin, Klahra ran up. I had met Klahra earlier in the summer, when her parents had come to see my father, the headman of my village. We had talked, well I had talked, while our parents discussed village matters. We continued to hang out since I had been coming to her village for training.

I would have lived out my life in my home village of Sen'jin if I hadn't met Klahraka when I was fourteen. Instead, I ended up telling her a lot about me, my family, and the rest of my village. By the end of the afternoon we were friends.

We would visit each other as the summer grew warmer. Most of the time I was going to her village with my older sister, Jen'shan, hunting trainer. She was training some of us for our rites of passage. Mid summer was my first solo hunt. Sen'jin sent me out to bring back a tail from one of the scorpids roaming the areas outside Klahra's village. Finding my prey was easy, killing it was much harder. Its claws were everywhere, swiping at my face and arms, while the tail stinger snapped past my face and chest. Once it grazed my arm with shooting pain and dizziness from the venom. By the time it was dead, I was covered in small bites and scratches.
