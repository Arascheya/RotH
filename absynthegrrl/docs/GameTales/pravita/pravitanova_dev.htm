Alexandra (Shandi on the Pravita Nova server) Zareva - Tiefling - Guest

Alexandra was born in Centrys, the City of Doors, to a common whore, working one of the lower class brothels in the Hive. Her father came in through one Door and left through another. She was orphaned at an early age when her mother was over-used by a wealthy being. The madame let her stay, at first as a fetch and carry girl, then as a display prop, dressing her in miniature adult clothes and sending her to mingle with the guests. Alexandra ran when she was twelve, just before her freshness could be sold off, and landed with a group of common thieves. Eventually she struck out on her own and began working for hire by the time she was sixteen. At eighteen she accepted her first commision as an assassin. Since then she has become a respected resource for covert operations.
