General Ideas

Angelique Lestrade - great great great grandaughter of Inspecter Lestrade, studying in Seoul for a year when things go down

Sequence is - T-7 days - bee and lightening to garment on end of bed; T-4 days - apartment trashed, sitting in corner, fireworks; T-2 days - control of energy; T-1 day - visitor, based on choice of faction

Tuppeny Dreadful books - located in Darkside, London - has been in the Lestrade family for generations. The Lestrade family in this timeline is an alpha variant of the Mega-Corp Ascendency thread - in this line, the Hut did not appear and Mereret was in London for the duration of Lestrade's life. He married another woman, although she knew of the strange affair between Lestrade and Mere, and approved. Lestrade and his wife had several children, including a girl named Angelique Mereret. The main character is named for this great great great aunt. The book store sells popular books from the font romms and houses an extensive occult and historical collection in the back rooms. Many of these are not for sale but can be accessed for a maintenance fee. Copies can be provided in electronic format for transcription fees. Ange spent many childhood hours reading through various items in the back libraries, including the case notes of Inspecter Lestrade.

Bugs, Issues, and Wishlist items

WISH - Please add bare feet and bring the hot pants back - I like the long T-hirt and Hot Pants look.

WISH - Please return the bsic inventory to the earlier beta size.I have never understood the need to keep personal storage small in MMOs.

WISH - Longer Term - Locale/region/geo-filtering detection so those of us in non-prude countries don't have to pay for lowest common denominator silliness with regard to nudity.

Some of us appreciate the social aspects of MMOs but like to go solo, either because of inclination or because of real-world time constraints.
