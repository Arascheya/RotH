from Irena to Kreel - Something Interesting

I went looking yesterday. I'm sorry I wasn't around last night. I had errands to run during the day and had to cover a night shift last night. Sometimes being manager sucks.

Shandi has the entire series of MYST games, which I remember seeing her play fairly often. Not finding anything in the way of books, I was going to play MYST, just to see what it would tell me. When I opened the case there was the game disk, but there was also an unmarked data disk as well. I kind of wish I hadn't found that. I thought it would be notes, like in Kiril's books. Instead it was her diary from around the time we first met. I don't know why she hadn't encrypted it, it was the first thing she taught me when I got a computer.

So much happened to her that she didn't tell me about. I mean, she didn't have to tell me, we aren't lovers, or family, or anything like that...it's just I might have been able to help, make it less painful and hard, if she could have talked about it.

I'm sorry, I'm rambling, it's just..I don't know...it just hurts to know my best friend was in so much pain and I wasn't given a chance to help. I'm going to take another look tomorrow, after she's gone to work, and see if she mentions anything related to D'Ni, and try and not to read any more personal bits.

Irena

Update and Confusion

I sure wish there was a way to jack a laptop into a KI and direct-upload data, ah well...

Shandi spent most of the day working from home so I didn't get much chance to look through her stuff. Instead I spent the day at a local coffee shop, surfing the web for information. Now, I really am confused. I found lots of references to Cyan Worlds, MYST - the game, yet I also found detailed timelines and descriptions of events that seem too detailed to come from any game I'm familiar with.

Back home I had a chance to finish looking through the game cases. There was nothing else.

When Shandi returned home last night, I asked her about MYST. She said they were games and that I could play them if I wanted. Then she added: "But they didn't get it right, not really. The puzzles are close, but the characters in the game were just cardboard cutouts of the real people. It was criminal how they were trivialized. But, play the games. When You're done I'll tell you what really happened."
