The ring was a delcious coolness following close on its heels. Endorphines surged, wrapping her in a warm blanket, keeping the aches at bay for a few moments of bliss. Slowly they faded and the throbbing ache of a fresh wound intruded. for a moment she felt regret until she saw the purple stone flashing from the ring in her belly. She smiled up at Jasmine, "Wicked awesome, thanks."

"Now I'd tell you about care and healing, but since this is Eternia, healing is wildly different from person to person. I have seen piercings close up here, so if you want to keep it you will want to keep something in most of the time."
