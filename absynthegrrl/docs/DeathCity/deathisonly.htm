She turned to return to where she had awoken and froze. In the center of the glade a body sprawled back, sightless eyes staring up at the sky. Curiosity pulled her forward and fear held her back. As she edged closer she began to make out the features. A crash of panic and terror ripped the clouds away as she recognized herself. An inhuman shriek tore through her throat and hurled itself skyward. Blackness swallowed her once more.
