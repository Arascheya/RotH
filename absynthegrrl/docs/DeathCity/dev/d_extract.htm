Extraction Team

Group of trouble-shooters, warriors that go in to assist other Eternia teams that are under attack by demonic or other forces. This is a very recent kind of Eternia group. At first it wasn't even an organized group, rather just a list of people and assets that could be pulled when needed. As the incursions became more concentrated and common it was formalized. This was about the time Kali was rising to prominence in the group. She ended up becoming the leader as much from no one else wanting it as anything else.
