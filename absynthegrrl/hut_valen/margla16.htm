16 - Kitchens

CH, 15' flat overall; IL, see below.

Hut Room Details

Room A is kept clear of obstructions. It contains side boards, serving platters, and other final preparation items. Food is moved from here to the main dining area.

The kitchen itself is room B; the dim light here comes from the wood-burning stoves and a few candles on the shelves. A stairway rises to area 32 from this room. Little treasure may be found here, except what is carried by the cooks themselves as protection from flame (e.g., rings of fire resistance or similar devices). A deadly array of cutlery, ranging from knives to sword-sized cleavers, lies around this area.
