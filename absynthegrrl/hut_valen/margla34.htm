34 - Cloisters

Hut Room Details

This is based off the central court of the level 7 library. There is a central covered area with a cobra fountain in the center. Seven covered halls branch out and end in a surrounding hallway. In the sections between the arms are various garden arrangements. The central section is lit by late afternoon sunlight through laticework windows in the domed ceiling.

Doors in each of the points between halls lead to various Hut sections. There are also doors at the end of each hallway that lead into the Hut. A secret hatch near the fountain leads down to the Brain Scan Chamber

Six of the central doors lead to the Library and the seventh leadsd to the Gate Room. This area is one of the few accessable from the Gate Room for beings entering through the Sphinx Web. Beings following this path may access the Library, the Cloisters, and the Brain Scan Chamber. The outer walkway doors appear to be blank walls to non Hut members. Entry through the Sphinx Web requires direct permission from the Hut Controller.
