20 - Servants' Chambers

CH, 10' flat overall: IL, 4 CL's, one over each exit.

Hut Room Details

This area housed Margla's personal servants. She freed them just before the final jump. Most of these rooms are now empty except for the nests in the walls that contain the Hut cleaning beings. These are large and small scrubbing bubbles and refuse eaters that magically move throughout the hut and keep it at the cleanliness level set by the Hut Controller.

Room A is still occupied by Klktzash, the librarian. Her room is furnished in a middle eastern harem style.
