18 - Food Storage 2

CH, domed ceiling, 25' at center; IL, four torches, one on the wall between each pair of food bins.

Hut Room Details

All foods besides meats and beverages are stored here in eight special containers set against the walls. The food bins will cause perishable foodstuffs placed inside them to be preserved and replenished indefinitely, so that vegetables, grains, breads, pies, eggs, fruits, and vegetable oils may be taken from the bins without needing to refill them. There are no guards here, but there is a 30% chance of an encounter here, since snacking is a common pastime among the Hut's inhabitants.
