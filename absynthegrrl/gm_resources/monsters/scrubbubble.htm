Scrubbing Bubbles maintain Hut cleanliness. They live in small chambers in the walls of the various Hut chambers and move around by a limited form of "tunneling". They are a component of the basic fabric of the Hut and are usually on automatic control. They can be used as snooping devices or assigned specific tasks by the Controller. This is a non-transferable benefit.
