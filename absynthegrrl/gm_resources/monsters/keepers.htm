Keepers

The Keepers are drider-like beings, recruited from the realms of Ishra. After all - knowledge is the root of all evil according to many religions.

The Keepers maintain the Great Repository and tend the Gatherers. They provide support and assistance to any researchers currently using the Great Repository. They know where all the various data section are and how best to track down the specifics of data. The are the Great Librarians for lack of a better title.

Once in a while a reseracher will be elevated to the position of Keeper. This occurs if the researcher is unique or extremely capable. Klktzash has been elevated in this way.
