Dust Skimmer

Dust skimmers circulate throught the labyrinth cleaning up dead organics from within the dust. They are long and thin with vacuum like snouts. They have large, powerful flippers that propel them slowly through the dust. Similar to earth worms the filter the dust through their bodies and extract the nutrients. Skimmers come in many sizes with some able to process the remains of large beings. They are smart enough that they will deposit the bones and other non-digestables in a target location where Tyana can transport them to the room of bones and the master treasure chamber.
