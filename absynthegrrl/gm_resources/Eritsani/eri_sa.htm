Eri'sa

This is the race of the wanderers of the Lotus Empire. They are a hybrid of actual T'Sani and Static. They make their living from the desert, the oases, and the wadis of the Lotus Empire. They are herders, caravaners, and hunters after the forgotten places.

Eri'sa live at the edges of the Kharatoum Habaya and the Galhai Wastes. Their villages are small, ovten backing up against mountains and hills where they can retreat to caves when the sandstorms come through. They are often poorer than the statics of the Empire.

Eri'sa use the desert Habyanisha for food and transportation into the deep desert.

True T'Sani will deal with the Eri'sa, but do not consider them true wanderers.
