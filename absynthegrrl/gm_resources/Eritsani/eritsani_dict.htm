Eritsani Dictionary

Pronunciation

"a" as in father

"e" pronounced "ay" as in hay

"i" as "e" in me

"o" as in tone

"u" pronounced as "oo" in too

"y" pronounced "yee"

Words

Ai Kreshalkt - an exclamation of surprise, kind of like holy shit, holy fuck, etc.

Alsheth - Beg

Ila - in

Ilakt - out

Feqt - ofal, digusting something, worthless item

Ghai Ya (guy Yee ah) - exclamation of dismay

Gawakt - grounder, literally greatly crazy, all non-Eritsani are considered crazy and dangerous, too unstable to be allowed on ships

Kala - black, dark

Kalimayani - black or dark wise mother

Kara - derivative of Kare

Karakinska - literally - red clan leader or blood clan leader

Kare - blood, blood red, bloody

Kahn - suck, vacuum

Kahnar - sucking

Kinska - clan leader

Kulprash - spirit, courage, heart - the word does not translate well into standard

Ky’ris (KYEE rees) - final justice to which there can be no appeal, also the arbiters that assess this level of justice

Mayani - wise mother - name/title given to the head woman of each major clan

Mayanish - the Void, the universe

Mishtakt - bullshit, excrement

Nash - son

Nasha - sons

Nish - daughter

Nisha - daughters

Nyet - no, not

Nygarth - write

Nygaresh - written

Prelaten (pray lah TAYN) - safety, security, sisterhood, attachment, and protection. This does not completely translate as it signifies a tie between the speaker and the hearer that goes beyond the words the Empire has for similar concepts. An Eritsani that utters this will come to the aid of the other regardless of the peril if asked. The catch is the assistance must be asked for. Without the asking there is no obligation.

Pulatare - offspring of no mother, the ultimate in Eritsani pejoratives. you have no mother, thus you have no tribe and no place with us. in the early times of the Eritsani this usually meant to be cast out as well

Ska - leader, leaders - is appended to the name of the leader - ie if Kara were a leader it would be Karaska - although usually the leader goes by the family name - ie if Sara Vilyachi was the leader of the Vilyachi she woul dbe called Vilyachiska rather than Saraska. leaders are always women

Taishakt - (Tzani more than Eristasni) - litteraly Static, used as a term of derision for landed, settled non Tzani, basically gaijin or Gadje

Tredaln - literally life, often used for air as well

Wakt - crazy, dangerous, too disturbed to be trusted on a ship or near others

Yani (yee AH nee) - mother

Yash (yee ASH) - raw sewage, unprocessed waste

Yput (YEE poot) - fuck

Yputar (YEE poot ar) - fucking

Yputes (YEE poot ays) - fucker

Eritsani Sayings

Ghana Paisht Ya - mother fucking pile of shit style exclamation

Inrish te nygaresh ila tredaln (This is written in air). - also ila tredaln (in air - shortcut form, also used in formal litanies as an equivalent to Bilal Kaifa or So Say We All) - This is an absolute thing. Air is the most valuable commodity to the Eritsani. Without air there is no life. This has kept its form, even in the Tzani who have become more planet bound wanderers.

kia yput kias yani - you fuck your mother

kias nish te wakt - your daughter is crazy

yput kia - fuck you

yput kias yani - fuck your mother

You do not beg the void for mercy. - as direct and unyielding as it sounds. This is often spoken when someone wrongly seeks mercy.

Kis prelaten - a binding oath of friendship and assistance. Usually a token is passed to verify this bond although this is not strictly necessary. See the description of Prelaten for more detail.

There are lies more believable than truth - when it is suspected that a lie is being propagated

May your names be forgotten - curse. Eritsani history is based on oral traditions to be forgotten is to have no history and no remembrance.
