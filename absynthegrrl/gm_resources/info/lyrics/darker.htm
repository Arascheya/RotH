Darker Than This - Spike's Song

Marysia

Story Behind the Song:

This song was written about Spike from Buffy the Vampire Slayer and his obsession with the Slayer, inspired in particular by the episode Fool For Love. It was recorded live in July 2001 using what crude methods and bad guitar playing were available to me :)
