Inner Child

Lyrics by Kali, scoring by Love's Bitch, choreography by Kali, effects by Claire and Dementia, vocals Kali and Ennui

/* written about the inner wreckage Kali sees when looking at her inner child - dark, ominous, and foreboding, this piece needs to be rich in imagery */

Screams in the dark

Calling me

Down dark hallways

A dark door

A shattered city

Grey under grey skies

Taste of ash on fitful winds


A figure in the street

Stirs under the cold wind

Burned blackened skin

Cracking, weeping blood

Empty eyes stare

Mouth open in a silent scream
