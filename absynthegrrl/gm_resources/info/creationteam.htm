Creation Team

No one knows much about these beings and whether or not they actually existed. If they did, they are credited with creating several of the proto-races, the older ones, such as the Drkzyrkth and the Irdisinthyish.

All major religions deny the existence of any type of living beings as "creators". Any supposed evidence is destroyed and the finders killed. Valerie Cantrazel, in the time of Valen, found conclusive evidence of a Creation Team bio-mechanical ship in the ice of Icicle.

Legends from various places claim one of the ships still drifts in the far reaches of the void. None of the legends indicate if it is a derelict, under command, or has been appropriated by other groups.
