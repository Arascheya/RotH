YES - YoYoDyne Engineering and Systems

YES is the other major hyperdrive and systems manufacturer, providing direct competition to General Electro-Dynamics. YES hyperdrives are slighty better than GED ones, however, the Alliance has contractual ties to GED that make them the de-facto standard in the Core Systems. Similar to their sister company DSC, YoYoDyne is favored by the Fringe Systems purchaser for their lower cost and higher reliability.

DSC and YES are the linked extensions of ITC from the old home world.
