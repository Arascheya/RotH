Greyhound Spaceways

Greyhound Spaceways is the galatic equivalent of a bus service. It is slow, inexpensive, and often crowded. In a pinch it will get you to where you need to go, just not fast or terribly comfortably.

Greyhound Spaceways typically uses Starbus 2000’s equiped with GED 6850 hyperdrive with GED 285 backup.

Each ship has passenger space for 100 w/ limited cargo. They contract with Empire Burger for catering of the on-board meals. The fare is simple but decent.
