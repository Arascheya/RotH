multiverse or multi-verse - from wikipedia

A term originally coined in 1895 by the American philosopher and psychologist William James. This term indicates all times, all dimensions, all items in the entirety of a reality set. This includes the creation of it, the gods of it, the places in it, and all the interconnected additional bits. By this definition there is only one multi-verse as it contains all variations of time and space.

Multiverse, Realms, and Worlds

The Multiverse is the entirety of this cycle of creation and form. It encompasses all worlds, time-lines, and Powers.

A World is a set of similar places in multiple timelines that have a similar basic set of races, circumstances, and Lesser Powers.

A Realm is a single instance of a World. Each being is from a Realm, although there may be echoes of each being in multiple Realms. For example a being could go to another Realm of its home World and meet itself. The self it met would not be itself, it would be that Realm's version of itself.

Realms of the Hut

The Realms of the Hut is a fictional multiverse that encompasses the Tales of the Hut and various other story worlds and time lines. There are some that have a linear relationship to each other and others that are more loosely connected. Some have been visited by the Hut of All Worlds and some have not.

Common Terms

One-Dim - Dims- entities only recognizing the World on which they live. They may 'believe' in the Multi-Verse, but have not experienced it in any real way.

Multi - Multiversally Aware - Awares - those touched by and experiencing the true reality of the Multi-verse. Residents of places such as Sphere automatically apply. Others may be born aware or awakened through items, actions, or experiences.
