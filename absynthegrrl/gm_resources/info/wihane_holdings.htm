Wihane Holdings

Wihane Holdings is an investmentand management group composed of Wahaiians. It is intended to keep land and other assets in the hands of long term Wahaiians. Originaly the intent was for only Wahaiian natives to benefit, however, the mission was expanded to accept long term residents as well.

Rani Corp has been an ally in this, including selling Turtle Bay Resort to Wihane for a very fair price.
