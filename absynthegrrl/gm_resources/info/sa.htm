Strange Attractor

Strange attractors, or Stranges, are beings able to sense when a dangerous entity enters the realm they are in. The Strange will be drawn to the danger and the danger will be drawn to the Strage.

Stranges are part of a realm's defense against absolute chaos. Stranges are expected to eject or destroy dangers entering their realms in whatever ways are necessary. This can be single being combat or enlisting the help of Awakened in the realm to assist.
