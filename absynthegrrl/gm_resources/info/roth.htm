Sandbox

Sandbox is a broad term and encompasses the entirety of the world. The sandbox aspect allows players to challenge current power groups and supplant them through combat, tactics, or subterfuge. All power blocks are held by characters controlled by CyCore or Rani Corp personel at the launch of the game. After that, all positions are fair game except for the Three Sisters.

Sandbox also allows for things such as plays who just want to set up in a city and provide shops, services, or other items for other players. Figure out methods for these players to advance, should they desire to.

Missions

Missions are "canned" items available to players at various points in the world. They are a way to learn more about the world, see new places, and acquire items. Missions are intended to enhance and supplement the sandbox apects of the environment. Missions are available from found items, places, NPCs, or CyCore run Player Characters.

New players will encounter missions immediately after Character Creation. The first mission will be to fix their Hut. A few additional missions could be included for tutorial aspects before the new players are exposed to the sandbox.

Misc Ideas

Return Home - either first time in Hut or first time can return to Place of Origin

This is fairly simple to imagine. What if your old home, whether that room you were in was a house or an apartment complex, became more than a backdrop for the game's opening?

I can imagine some possibilities for this. The first would turn your apartment into a quest location, where another person or group of people have become the owners of the place, and you're called to recruit them into your cabal because your place apparently likes making anima-infused people. What if your apartment was haunted? What if, like in Cabin in the Woods, you went back to get a memento, and the memento you choose in the game made you face a terrible horror based on that memento?

It's a fun idea, and one that tries to add layers to the backstory of your character.
