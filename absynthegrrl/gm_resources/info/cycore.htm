CyCore Games

Game production company in the middle of the Meriksas. They are known for Conflict for Mars and the Shadow Journey games. Currently they are working as a contractor for Rani Corp on Realms of the Hut.

CyCore games is located near Waverly's Pub. They rent the offices from Tom O'Malley and later Irena.

Forever City, one of the episodes of the Shadow Journey games, came with a world editor. Forever City started as a single player game, then became episode 1 of the online Shadow World series. The world editor was modified to support the online model of the Shadow Journey games. It was easy to use, supported a rich and powerful editing environment and allowed Shadow Journeysto become the most modded game series of all time. Forever City takes place in a hive city, think Kowloon Walled City on steroids, and follows the path of a kid growing up in the lower sections. Jaime will want to borrow heavily from Forever City for a location in Realms of the Hut. The game was formative for her.

The world editor of Forever City is one of the assets Jaime wants to use in Realms of the Hut.
