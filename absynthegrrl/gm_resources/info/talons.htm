Talons of the Celestial Dragon

A conglomerate of seven families holding various groups and companies. This is basically an organized crime syndicate.

Led by Nilos Tarkrantol (president of the seven)

The Talons are composed of 7 cells. They evolved from the tongs of pre-space Karanal (legendary homeworld - see Karanal write-up for specifics) symbol is seven pointed star or oriental gold dragon. They are honorable and do not engage in non-medicinal drug trade. The Talons do produce and smuggle pharmaceuticals, guns, alcohol, etc. Sometimes employed by Drakon Pharmaceuticals as part of their alternate distribution system - to get meds into restricted areas/systems.

Leaders are elected by their respective talon. The President is picked by the leaders - term is 10 years with one renewal possible. Current president came from smuggling.

The Talons also maintain various legitimate operations as cover for their other activities. See below for more details.
