Ceremony - First Elder Position Transferral

First Part

First part takes place in Council Chambers. Open to public. Pendant is transferred. Entirely done in candle light. Some type of ritualized words about key to past knowledge, past holds key to future, roots of knowledge stored underground in trust for future generations since the dawn of time. Takes place at night. Moons are down only stars are out.

Oath of office - play with from here -

I, Eltaryn, swear to guide the people of Zeltrop in times of peace and tranquility, to lead them, to the best of my abilities, in times of trouble, to preserve our culture and help it grow and mature. I swear to protect the planet of Zeltrop and to continue healing the damage from the Last War. I swear to never let another war like the Last War happen on this planet as long as I hold the office of First Elder.

I, Eltaryn, take this office and it’s responsibilities until I retire and turn them over to another or until I die.

Second Part

The old First Elder or the Acting First Elder takes the candidate down to the Thought Crystal Gardens where he tells the person, "This ring is key to all knowledge and power of our world. It will open all doors and areas to you." At this time ring is placed on finger and person goes through attunement procedures. After the new First Elder is attuned the old First Elder has him go to the center of the room and bend down, pressing ring into indentation in floor. The floor opens into a room containing inscriptions on the walls telling of past history. Also contains brainscan unit and computer terminal.
