Legends of the Day Walker

"The stalking death moves across the land. Pharaohs tremble and priests wail. It comes in the night from the places of the dead. Yet there is new terror for now it moves through the day as well." - inscription in the Temple of Khnum in the time of Khufu.

This is the first mention of a Day Walker. It has become the holy grail for vampyr everywhere, the search for what a Day Walker is and how to become one.

It is said the legendary Grandmother of the Vampyr, the first of them, was a Day Walker.
