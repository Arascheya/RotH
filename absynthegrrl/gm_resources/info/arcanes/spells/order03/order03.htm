Third Order spells

ABYSMAL ITCHING: Duration: Special, Range: 30m, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell allows the caster to conjure a burning pile of Poison Oak to the desired spot. Where ever the smoke is blown the effects will be felt. All within a 100m of the burning brush make a saving throw of .5 * CON or less. Those that make the CON roll are uneffected, those that don't the worse the miss the worse the reaction. Extreme reaction is a blistering of the bronchial tubes and throat and severe burning of the eyes.

CLAIRAUDIENCE: Duration: 1 melee per Order, Range: Special, Spell Type: Special.

This spell enables the arcane to concentrate upon some known locale and hear in his mind what ever noise he would hear if he was standing on the target point.

The arcane must either know the area or it must be an obvious area, ie behind the door, around the corner etc. This spell is always an active spell, even after passing the test between 3rd and 4th Orders.

CLAIRVOYANCE: Duration: 1 melee per Order, Range: Special, Spell Type: Special.

This spell enables the arcane to concentrate upon some known locale and see in his mind what ever he would see if standing at that point. This will not enable him to see in the dark or see invisible creatures and the like.

The arcane must either know the area or it must be an obvious area, ie behind the door, around the corner etc. This spell is always an active spell, even after passing the test between 3rd and 4th Orders.

COMMUNICATE WITH AVIANS: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 10m, Area of Effect: One Avian.

This spell allows the arcane to communicate with any non-intelligent avians. Remember that the avians do not have any intelligence so they will not understand long and complex questions.

COMMUNICATE WITH INSECTS: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 10m, Area of Effect: One Insect.

This spell allows the arcane to communicate with any non-intelligent insects. Remember that the insects do not have any intelligence so they will not understand long and complex questions.

COMMUNICATE WITH MAMMALS: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 10m, Area of Effect: One Mammal.

This spell allows the arcane to communicate with any non-intelligent mammals. Remember that the mammals do not have any intelligence so they will not understand long and complex questions.

COMMUNICATE WITH PLANTS: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 10m, Area of Effect: One Plant.

This spell allows the arcane to communicate with any non-intelligent plants. Remember that the plants do not have any intelligence so they will not understand long and complex questions.

COMMUNICATE WITH REPTILES: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 10m, Area of Effect: One Reptile.

This spell allows the arcane to communicate with any non-intelligent reptiles. Remember that the reptiles do not have any intelligence so they will not understand long and complex questions.

COMPREHEND SPOKEN LANGUAGES: Duration: 10 melee per Order, Range: Self, Area of Effect: 10m.

To use this spell it is necessary to have the First Order Spell Comprehend Languages at 75%. This spell allows the arcane to converse with beings who speak in an unknown language. All within the 10ms hear the arcane speaking their own language and the arcane hears all who speak in the area in his own tongue.

DISPEL MAGIC: Duration: Instant, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: Special, Spell Type: Active at all Orders.

This spell will dispel any spell with a duration (either Active or Passive). To determine if the Dispel Magic worked compare the Order of the arcane to the number of POW points in the Battle Magic with each Order of the arcane being worth 2 POW of Battle Magic. Each Order of the arcane is equal to one point of Rune Magic. Similar to the Battle Magic spell of the same name it is possible to put more POW points into this spell for the expressed purpose of going through any extra POW put into a spell.

This spell may also be cast against another spell user while he is preparing a spell causing the spell he is working on to dispel.

For dispelling magic on an enchanted item the arcane needs to know Enchant An Item at 40% or better. For more information on dispelling magic see Dispel Magic I at Order four.

ELEGANT EXPLOSIONS: Duration: 1 melee, Range: 15m plus 3m per Level, Area of Effect: Special.

For each Order of experience of the arcane, one explosion can be created. The explosion causes all within a 3m diameter area to take 1d6 damage. The explosions are accompanied by an elegant and colorful burst of fireworks which cause all within the area to attack at minus 5% due to confusion. This also causes all within the area to strike themselves or party members 30% of the time.

ERASE SCENT: Duration: 10 melee per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being for ever two Orders.

This spell allows the arcane to cause the normal scent of himself or another person to become totally neutralized. This makes it impossible to track him by scent.

FIREBALL: Duration: 2 strike ranks, Range: 33m plus 3m per Order, Area of Effect: 3m per hit die.

Produces an explosive burst of flame of intense heat doing 1d6 per Order of the caster. At 4th Level you can choose how many die of damage to put into the spell up to maximum hit die, until then it is 3d6 damage.

FIRE NEGATION: Duration: 5 melee plus 1 melee per Order, Range: Self, Area of Effect: Special, Spell Type: Active.

This spell creates an area 1m radius around the caster, that will destroy any fire that comes in contact with it.

FLUTTER: Duration: 1 melee, Range: Self.

This spell causes the arcane to rapidly shift back and forth between the ethereal plane and the prime material. This spell will only last one melee round, extension spells and such do not effect it. It is impossible to move when this spell is in effect, but healing magic maybe invoked.

When the arcane is attacked and a successful hit is rolled then a percentage is rolled. If the percentage is 01-50 the arcane is in the prime material and takes the damage.

It is possible to be trapped on the ethereal plane. If a solid object is placed where the arcane is coming back into the prime material and held there for the duration of the spell the arcane is trapped on the ethereal plane until the object is moved.

FLY: Duration: 10 melee plus 1 per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell gives the target being the magical power of flight. The being is able to move vertically and/or horizontally 6m per strike rank.

GUST OF WIND: Duration: 2 strike ranks, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell creates a strong puff of air which originates from the arcane and moves in the direction he is facing. The path of the wind is 3m wide by 3m per Order in length.

The force of this wind is sufficient to extinguish small unprotected flames. It will cause protected flames to dance madly and has a 5% chance per Order of the Arcane to extinguish even these flames. It will fan large fires outward 1 to 6 feet, and will force small flying creatures back 3 to 20 meters.

This gust will circulate the air and will dissipate noxious fumes and clouds as long as there is not a continuing source.

HILDAGARDE'S HEAVY HELPER: Duration: Instant, Range: 5m, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell causes a 10 X 10 X 10 foot cube of wet sand to appear. For each Order over the Order required to cast this spell add 1 foot to the dimensions.

HOLD PERSON: Duration: 10 melee plus 1 per Order, Range: 40m and in sight, Area of Effect: One Being.

This is a POW vs POW spell, and when the arcane overcomes the target's POW the target can not move until the spell either runs out or is dispelled.

INFRAVISION: Duration: 6 hours, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell gives the recipient the ability to see in the infrared spectrum with all of its bonuses and drawbacks. As in torches are bright to the point of painful and cold blooded creatures are ambient temperature so do not show up.

LIGHTNING BOLT: Duration: Instant, Range: Special, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell produces a 6" wide lightning bolt with a length of 10m plus 1m per Order of the arcane. This is an aimed spell, use the percentage chance of getting the spell off as the "to hit" percentage. Magical protection and leather armor will absorb damage, but metal armor only acts as a conductor. Damage for this spell is 1d6 per Order of the arcane. After the next Arcane Test the arcane can choose how many hit die he wants to put into the spell.

MASK MAGIC: Duration: 10 melee plus 3 melee per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: 1.3m diameter sphere.

This spell is cast on any small metal object. Any magical item within the sphere of influence will (for the duration of the spell) be undetectable by magical means. The masking effect is also undetectable.

There is a drawback to this spell, any magical items within the area of effect are also inoperative until removed from the influence or the expiration of the spell.

There can be many items enchanted with this spell in an area, over lapping each other.

MATERIAL: Duration: Instant and Permanent, Range: 5 zeem per Order, Area of Effect: One Cubic Foot per Level.

This spell gets the item specified from any source within range. If nothing is with in range nothing appears. Inanimate objects only.

This spell cost 2 POW points to search for the item and 3 POW points to bring the item to the arcane. If no item is within range only the search POW points are used.

MINUTE METEORS: Duration: Special, Range: 3m per Level, Area of Effect: One Target per Missile.

This spell enables the caster to cast small lightning bolts that inflict 1d4 points of damage on the target. The spell percentage is also the to hit percentage. These bolts follow piercing weapon rules for critical and impaling hits.

This ability continues until the arcane has fired off as many of the bolts as he has Orders of experience, or until he decides to forgo casting any more bolts.

The arcane must point at his desired target on the second strike rank after the spell is cast and a bolt will be discharged. This process is repeated every other strike rank until all the bolts are released or the arcane ends the spell. This spell will continue into the next melee round.

MORDEN'S BACKWARD BLAST: Duration 10 min. Range: 20m, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell causes the target to do everything in reverse. Talk, Move, etc. This is a POW vs POW spell.

MYSTIC ROPE: Duration: 10 minutes plus 10 minutes per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell creates a magical rope of up 100 feet in length which moves about on command. The rope has sufficient independent movement to make untying impossible for anyone other than the caster. It is immune to all non-magical forces.

Initial casting of the spell gives the arcane 20 feet of rope and for each additional power point put into the spell during casting the arcane gets 10 additional feet up to the 100 foot limit.

If the rope is damaged, it regenerates one foot for every melee round.

PEACEFUL CREATION: Duration: Special, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: Special.

The recipient of this spell can create instantaneously any one object that he knows how to make by normal means. The object lasts for 10 minutes per Order of the caster, or until an attempt is made to use the item for harm.

PHANTASMAL FORCE: Duration: Special, Range: 25m plus 3m per Order, Area of Effect: One complete non-moving illusion, Spell Type: Active.

This spell allows the arcane to create a single non-moving illusion that will stay as long as the arcane is concentrating on the illusion and is within the range of the spell.

After the next Arcane Test the spell becomes passive and has a duration of 1 day per Order as long as the arcane is within the range of the spell.

The effects of this spell will last until the viewer successfully disbelieves the illusion.

PIT OF FLAME: Duration: 10 minutes per Order, Range: Sight, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell conjures a pit 20 feet by 20 feet by 5 feet deep filled with flames. Those falling into it take damage equal to half the caster's Order in six sided die.

RALPH: Duration: Special, Range: 3m plus 1m/Order, Area of Effect: One Being.

Requires a successful POW vs CON, causing the victim to start vomiting. He will continue to vomit for 25-CON melee rounds.

REASSEMBLE: Duration: 10 melee/Order, Range: Special, Area of Effect: 1m/Order, Spell Type: Active.

This spell returns the desired object, selected by touching a piece of it, to its whole condition, minus any pieces outside of the area of effect.

The arcane needs to concentrate on the process and once the assembly is complete concentrate on maintaining it. When the spell runs out or the arcane stops concentrating the pieces fall into a pile.

RUNES OF FIRE: Duration: Special, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell allows the arcane to write messages on any object or in the air itself. The letter of the message burn with a fire that does 1d4 points of damage per Order of the arcane for each melee round of contact. The message may be up to 10 words per Order of the arcane in length.

This spell lasts for 10 melee rounds per Order. The spell can be set as a delayed spell and will trigger in similar fashion as Magic Mouth.

SEWAGE SEEING: Duration: 1 melee per order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being per Order.

This spell allows the being to hear, see, and breath in sewage.

SHOLNON'S SECRET SPY SPELL: Duration: 1 hr per Order, Range 1 Zeem per Order, Area of Effect: One Being.

After a successful POW vs POW the target's voice is always audible to the arcane at will.

SPIRIT BINDING: Duration: 10 melee, Range: self, Area of Effect: Self.

With the use of this spell the arcane can bind a free spirit for his own use into animals or into Power/Spirit Storage Crystals. On the death of the binder the spirit is free once more. See Binding of Spirits RQ2 for more information on Bound Spirits.

STEAM BOMB: Duration: Special, Range: Thrown Item, Area of Effect: 10m radius cloud.

This spell forms a small crystal sphere filled with steam. When the sphere is broken it forms a 10m radius cloud of super heated steam doing 2d4 damage initially plus 1d4 for each melee in the cloud.

The cloud totally obscures vision and anyone emerging from it will emerge in a random direction. The cloud will last 1 melee for every two Orders of the arcane. The crystal ball will last for 10 minutes at which time it will disappear.

STEPHEN LE STRANGE'S SPELL OF INSTANT IDLENESS: Duration: 10 melee per Order, Range: 20m plus 3m per Order, Area of Effect: 10m diameter plus 1m per Order, Spell Type: Active.

One POW vs POW roll is made and everyone in the area of effect compares to that roll, all those effected by the spell sit around watching the clouds, or dust motes go by, the birds the bees, ho hum it is a nice day to do nothing.

SPORLING'S WALL OF WHIMSEY: Duration: 5 melee per Order, Range: 20m, Area of Effect: 3m X 3m wall plus 1m to the dimensions per Order.

This spell creates a wall of blue light that causes one whimsical thing to happen to what ever living matter that penetrates it each time it is penetrated. SUGGESTION: Duration: 60 min per Order, Range: 10m, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell is a POW vs POW spell, and it allows the arcane give a simple one sentence command. This will not cause the victim to do something he would not normally do.

SUNLIGHT: Duration: 3 melee, Range: 20m, Area of Effect: 3m Diameter Circle.

With this spell the arcane calls down a circle of sunlight at the desired location. The light is identical to normal sunlight and has been observed to be effective against various darkness loving creatures and many forms of undead.

THE SPELL OF THE BEE HOLDER: Duration: Instant and Permanent, Range: 3m, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell teleports in a hive of killer bees 3m from the caster in the direction he indicates. The hive will appear about two feet off the floor and it will immediately drop to the floor, causing the bees to become very angry. The bees will begin attacking every creature in the room, beginning with those nearest them. The bee's attack at 75%, have the ability to sting 4 times before death, always go for the non-armored areas, and do 1d4 plus 2 point poison (the poison is cumulative). The hive has a population of 1d100 X 10 bees in it.

by James Schneider

THE SPELL OF THE HOT TOES: Duration: 10 minutes per Order, Range: 3m, Area of Effect: One Pair of Feet.

This spell will require POW vs POW. Once the arcane is successful the targets toes become painfully hot. He will be unable to attack and his parry and defense will be reduced by half. He will take one hit point of damage per foot.

The components for this spell are a tiny piece of Phosphorus and a two inch long thin stick.

by Marlys Schneider

TRANSFER TENSILE STRENGTH: Duration: 1 Day per Order of Arcane, Range: Sight, Area of Effect: One Item.

This spell will transfer the tensile strength from an item held in the arcane's hand to another item that must be within sight of the arcane.

Thus the arcane holding on to a piece of over cooked spaghetti can transfer the spaghetti's tensile strength to the sword swinging toward his head. If the item being effected is on a person then the person's POW must be overcome by the arcane. If the item to be effected has a spirit bound into it then the spirit's POW must be overcome.

UNSEEN ACCOUNTANT: Duration: Special, Range: Special, Area of Effect: One Accountant.

This spell take 1 Permanent POW point initially, then it is 1 Temporary POW point every time the accountant is summoned, and he will stay around for one hour or until told to leave.

By this spell the caster creates a force similar to the Unseen Servant who follows him around and will on request take treasure given to him and store it in an extra-dimensional space. The accountant can not be seen or touch only heard, and he is the only one who can get the treasure back. He can read an inventory at anytime.

The accountant 10% on every thing he banks or 1 magic item out of every 10. You can either give him the item or he will (DM roll which one) take the item. IRS Audits are extra.

The accountant can only be dispelled by someone who knows exactly where he is, usually while he is speaking. If he is dispelled all the treasure is lost.

WALK ON WATER: Duration: 10 minutes per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being per Order.

This spell allows the recipient to walk on calm water for the duration of the spell.

WARM: Duration: 10 min per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being

This spell causes a zone of comfortable temperature and humidity to form around the target (the target's idea of comfortable). The zone is two inches thick and is independent of the external environment.

by Marlys Schneider

WATER BREATHING: Duration: 30 minutes per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being.

Allows the recipient to be able to breath water for the duration of the spell. This spell is dispelled by breathing air.

The reverse of this spell can be cast on water breathing creatures to allow them to breath air.
