First Order Spells

AFFECT NORMAL FIRES: Duration: 5 melee per Order of caster, Range: 2m per Order, Area of Effect: Up to a 1m bonfire, Spell Type: Active.

This spell enables an Arcane to control the size of a normal fire, either reducing it or enlarging it. Fuel consumption, heat output, and light are always in proportion to the size of the fire. So big fires burn more fuel than small fires, as well as put out more light and heat.

ALARM: Duration: 40 minutes + 10 minutes per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: up to 20 square feet per Order.

To cast this spell the arcane must trace the boundary of the area he wants to effect. The effected area will react to the presents of any living creature larger than SIZ .5. As soon as the creature sets foot on the area, touches it, or sends a hostile spell across it, a loud ringing will begin which will be clearly heard with in a 60ft radius. Undead will trigger the spell if the animator sends them with hostile intent.

ANDREA'S RAMBLING CLEW: Duration: Special, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One ball of string.

When cast this spell will translate written or spoken clues into the motion of a rolling ball of string. The ball of string will roll towards the landmarks mentioned in the clue in a sequential order. When the ball is unwound the spell is gone and must be recast. Picking up the ball will also dispel the magic.

The ball can roll up hill on a shallow grade (30 degrees or less) but will be stopped by stair steps, thorns, mud etc. It will back track to find a way around steep places but it is not smart enough to keep from falling into rivers, rat holes etc. The ball will unwind at the same rate as a man walks.

The original clue gets a POW vs POW if the spell misses the string will incorrectly translate the clue.

AWFUL STING: Duration: Instant, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: One Living Being.

This spell casts a small blue-green bolt, which hums like a million enraged bees, at the target. The target is stung as if by 1000 bees, a feeling that last only a strike rank, but is felt throughout the entire body. POW * 1 or less to retain consciousness, consciousness is recovered 30-CON strike ranks.

BASIC WEB: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 10m, Area of Effect: 3m diameter sphere.

A gossamer webbing of fiberglass type strands entangle all within the target area. For breaking purposes the web has 40 strength points.

CHARM PERSON: Duration: Special, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: Being touched.

This spell will effect any intelligent being. The being will regard the caster as a trusted friend and ally to be heeded and protected. This is not a control spell, the being still thinks and acts on its own. But the words and actions of the caster are viewed with favor.

The duration of the spell depends on the intelligence of the being as well as the actions of the caster. The spell is broken if the caster attacks the being, there is also the possibility of the spell being broken if a member of the caster's party attacks the being.

A POW vs POW is needed for this spell to take effect. If POW vs. POW is failed the target has a 3% per point of INT to know that a charm spell was tried.

COMPREHEND LANGUAGES: Duration: 10 melee per Order of caster, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: It has to be a written or spoken language.

With this spell the Arcane is able to read a written language or understand the spoken language of a speaking creature. The Arcane has to touch the item or being to be able to understand it. As long as the arcane is touching the being speaking the language, he can speak the language.

After the duration of the spell the knowledge read or heard stays with the Arcane but not how to read or speak the language.

CREATE AIR: Duration: Special, Range: Touch.

This spell creates normal air for the caster at sea level pressure. For each Order of the caster enough air can be created to last the caster one hour.

DANCING LIGHTS: Duration: 2 melee per Order, Range 3m per Order, Area of Effect: Special, Spell Type: Special.

Four lights plus one per Order, the caster can choose how many lights he wants. The lights produced are the size and intensity of birthday cake candles. The lights move as the spell caster desires, with him having to concentrate on the movement, otherwise they stay still.

DETECT MAGIC: Duration: 1 melee, Range: Self, Area of Effect: Vision

This spell causes anything with a magical aura to glow for the caster only.

DRY SPELL: Duration: Instant, Range: 3m per Order, Area of Effect: .3 cubic meters per Order.

This spell can remove enough water from any one object to completely dry it out. The water will reappear somewhere else within the caster's range at a point specified. The reverse draws water from somewhere in range to thoroughly saturate one object. This spell may not be used against living plants or animals. This spell works only with water.

ENCHANTED WEAPON: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: One Weapon.

This spell will add 5% per Order of the arcane to the attack and parry of the weapon up to 15%. It will also add +1 per Order of the arcane to damage for the weapon up to +6. Like the Fireball spell each Order of adders costs one POW point, so a 5th Order arcane spends 5 POW points and gets +15% to attack and parry and +5 to damage.

ENLARGE: Duration: 10 melee per Order, Range: Touch, Area of effect: One creature or object

This spell causes a 20% enlargement for every point of power put into the spell. Maximum enlargement is 200% of the creature or object's original size. After attaining 70% proficiency with this spell, the caster can reverse the spell causing diminution at the same rates and costs as enlargement.

ERASE: Duration: Permanent, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One scroll or two facing pages.

As the name implies this spell erases written words on paper, metal or stone.

FEATHER FALL: Duration: 1 second per Order, Range: 3m per Order, Area of Effect: One being or object per Order.

When this spell is cast on the target it will reduce the rate of fall to a constant .6 meters per second.

FIND FAMILIAR: Duration: Special, Range: 1 Zeem per Order of caster.

This spell allows the Arcane to call a Familiar if any are available within the spells range. When this spell is cast the Arcane begins to chant and continues chanting until either a familiar has arrived or 1 Xer has past.

This spell may only be attempted at an Arcane Guild House or Arcane Testing Site. Due to the physical and emotional cost of this spell it is only allowed to be used once per year. The type of familiar answering the Arcane's call is random.

The familiar adds CON points to the Arcane with every two CON points the familiar has adding one point to the Arcane.

1 - 3 Order Arcanes

For Arcanes in this skill range the familiar functions as a Bound Spirit for both POW and INT considerations. It also allows the Arcane to see and hear with the Familiar's senses while the familiar is within a five kilometer range of the Arcane.

4 - 6 Order Arcanes

The familiar functions as a Bound Spirit but with no limit on vision or hearing senses.

7 and up Order Arcanes

The familiar functions as a Rune status character's Allied Spirit.

If the familiar is killed the Arcane looses 6 permanent POW points and the number of CON points added by the familiar multiplied by 1.5. The CON points are regained at the rate of 1 point per month. To gain another familiar the Arcane must purchase another use of the Find Familiar spell.

FLARE: Duration: Special, Range: 100m

With this spell, the arcane causes an intensely bright, yellow/white spark to zip from the tip of his middle finger. This spark moves at 100m per second to a distance of 100m and bursts into a brilliant, but utterly harmless, 2m ball of light. This light casts the equivalent of half day light in a 60m radius about the ball of light. This light will slowly drift downward at the rate of 1m per second. If it hits any solid object it will go out. During the initial firing only it can and does ricochet off any solid object.

FLOAT: Duration: 10 minutes per Order, Range: 20m per Order, Area of Effect: One Object up to 100lbs per Order, Spell Type: Active.

This spell produces a large bubble of air, that will take the shape the caster wants, under the target item. Allowing the target item to float on or near the surface of any liquid.

Sunken items can be raised as long as the arcane knows where they are and they are with in the limits of this spell.

FRESHEN AIR: Duration: 10 melee rounds per Order, Range: 3m, Area of Effect: 100 cubic feet per melee.

This spell will remove any an all particulate matter from the air (smoke, dust, fog, etc.). This spell creates a mild draft as it works. In addition to cleaning the air it lightly scents the area. It is available in evergreen, lemon, herbal, fresh sea air, and spice.

FRIENDS: Duration: 2 minutes, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: Self

Adds half again to Charisma for the duration of the spell.

HAWK VISION: Duration: 4 hours, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One set of eyes.

This spell gives the target vision similar to a 10x power telescope. This vision can be switched on and off by the user for the duration of the spell.

HASTE: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell will double the movement class of the recipient. This spell effects movement only.

HEALING: Duration: Permanent, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being, Spell Type: Active.

With this spell the arcane can either heal a specific wound or over all body damage. If the arcane touches a wound when casting this spell then it is the wound touched that is healed, or the arcane can touch a none hurt area and have the healing flow evenly through out the injured person's body.

The amount healed is 1d4+ 1 point per level of arcane, with the arcane being able to adjust the amount of healing done once he is past 4th level.

This spell heals one point of damage per strike rank and the arcane must be concentrating on the healing through out the spell casting and healing otherwise the spell is broken. If the spell is broken the healing stops at that point and the arcane looses all the POW required to cast the spell.

IDENTIFY: Duration: Instant, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One item.

The caster matched his power against the power of the item, if he is successful he will know one ability of the item. The cost of this spell is one power point per ability of item. Each ability requires a separate power vs. power. For example the flaming sword that does 3 to 18 points of damage would cost 1 point of power to find out it flames, 1 more point of power would tell the caster that the minimum is 3 points of damage, the next point would tell the caster 4, then another would tell him 5, up until he reached 18 twice in a row. Thus to fully identify this sword would cost the caster 19 points of power provided he made all of the power vs. power rolls. Once a power vs. power roll is missed the caster must improve his spell use percentage for this spell, at which point he starts all over again.

LIGHT: Duration: 1 hour or until dispelled, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell must be cast on something, a piece of wood, a tip of a finger or any other object will serve. This spell will light an area of 10m radius.

The Reverse of this spell is called Dark and has the same properties as the Light spell.

LOCK JAW: Duration: 1 min. per Order, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: 1 being per 3 Orders.

Successful POW vs POW causes the targets mouth to shut in an uncontrolled rigid spasm. Spell is reversible.

MAGIC MISSILE: Range: 100m

This spell produces magical bolts that do 2D4+2 points of damage. The caster has to hit with them, the base hit percent is 15 to which is added the caster's weapon attach bonus. This spell give one missile to start with and an additional missile every other Order (1st Order 1 missile, 3rd Order 2 missiles etc).

Missiles can come out at only one per strike rank, however the arcane can delay their coming as long as he wishes. If he carries some over to the next melee round it will cost him an additional point of power.

MENDING: Duration: Permanent, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One item.

With this spell the caster can repair broken non-magical items. Each casting of this spell repairs 20 points of damage. This spell does not create matter so all the pieces have to be there and they have to be fitted together correctly. Note: magical items maybe repaired but then their magical properties are forever lost.

MESSAGE: Duration: 1 melee round per every two Orders, Range: Sight.

When this spell is cast the arcane can whisper a message and secretly or openly point at a target who will then hear the message. The message must fit the spell duration, and if there is time remaining the target may whisper a reply. There must be an unobstructed line of sight between the arcane and his target.

MUSCLE SPASM: Duration: 1 melee, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: One Muscle

Successful POW vs POW causes the designated muscle to go into an uncontrollable spasm.

NATURE CALL: Duration: until relieved, Range 10m, Area of Effect: one being with bladder.

A successful POW vs POW causes the victim to have to go relieve his bladder, NOW!

NYSTUL'S MAGIC AURA: Duration: 1 day per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One item.

This spell places an aura of magic on the touched item. This item will then detect as magic even though it has no other magical effects.

PASS WITHOUT A TRACE: Duration: 15 minutes, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being/Order.

This spell allows the recipient to move over any type of terrain without leaving foot print or scent. Thus tracking a person with this spell on is impossible.

PROTECTION FROM DEMONKIND: Duration: 10 melee, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One living being.

This spell acts as 5 points of armor against demon tainted beings. Adds two points to effective power against demon POW vs POW attacks, and adds 5% defense when in combat with demonkind.

PUSH: Duration: Instant, Range: 7m.

This spell inflicts a sharp poke on the target, if the caster over comes the targets power. This will cause no damage to the target but will distract him.

RELEASING BELLS: Duration: Instant, Range: 60m, Area of Effect: One Creature.

This spell causes the sound of Chinese Bells to ring out, the vibration of which release any form of non-magical bonds holding a single creature or being.

This spell will release knotted, ropes, chains and shackles, leather straps, gags, wooden stocks, bridles, girths etc.

REVERSE FLOW: Duration: 10 melee rounds per Order, Range: Touch.

This spell can cause any liquid to flow in the opposite direction from that normally expected. Thus a stream might flow up hill, a water fall reverse, or wine pour upwards into a glass and spill if the glass were turned upright.

SHIELD: Duration: 15 minutes, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One living being.

Gives 3 points protection per Order of caster up to 9 points. Gives 1 point Counter Magic per Order. Costs 1 power point per Order of caster, caster can decide to spend less power.

This spell is incompatible with Rune Magic Shield and Absorption spells.

SHOCKING GRASP: Duration: Until discharged, Range: Touch.

This spell generates an electric charge doing damage of 1D8 plus 1 point per Order of caster. The arcane need only touch the targets body or an electrical conductor which touches the target. Armor does not count against this spell but magical protections do.

SLEEP: Duration: 25 minus targets CON in hours, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: One living being per Order.

If caster over comes targets power, the target falls into a deep sleep from which he can be awakened only by strenuous effort until the spells duration has past.

A POW vs POW is rolled and the power of all the creatures to be effected is matched against the one roll. Say that the caster is fourth Order and has a power of 15, the player rolls a 43%, the power of the beings is 14, 12, 17, and 20. The beings with the 12 and 14 go to sleep the other two do not.

This spell only works on creatures that are capable of sleeping.

SMOKY SPHERE: Duration: 1 melee, Range: Thrown Item, Area of Effect: 3m radius.

This spell produces a small crystal sphere filled with swirling grey smoke. When thrown it bursts on impact creating a 3m radius of harsh, irritating smoke, similar to tear gas. It adds 30% difficulty to spells with verbal components and causes coughing and choking for 21-CON melee rounds.

The crystal sphere will last 10 minutes at which point if it has not been thrown it will disappear.

SPIDER CLIMB: Duration: 1 melee round per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One living being.

This spell allows a person to walk up walls and across ceilings like a spider. Being's hands and feet must be bare and empty. This spell has a weight limit of 400 pounds.

SUMMON MOUNT: Duration: Special, Range: 1 zeem per Order, Area of Effect: Special.

The use of this spell issues a call for any riding animal to come to the arcane. The call lasts until a mount arrives or 1 hour per Order which ever comes first.

The mount will remain with the arcane and willingly serve for one day per Order. If this spell is recast before the day is out it will be extended another day. At the end of this spell the mount will return to its original owner.

THE ROSY MIST OF REASON: Duration: Special, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: 20m diameter.

This spell produces a cloud of rose colored mist that causes all who fail a INT*2 or less to sit and talk things over with their opponents. Those making INT*2 are not effected. Unintelligent creatures have 10% chance of leaving, 20% chance of being indecisive, and 70% of attacking anyway. All in area are effected including the caster. The mist lasts 1 minute plus 1 minute for each additional power point put into the spell. Effects last as long as the mist.

TALL WALKER: Duration: 5 melee plus 3 melee per point of power, Range: Self.

This spell creates 1m tall stilts under the feet of the caster. They will move with the arcane so that things such as acid pools etc. can be safely waded through. The stilts themselves are undestructible. The stilts maybe lengthened .5m per point of power. All additional power points have to be put in at the time of the spell's casting.

UMBRELLA: Duration: 10 melee plus 1 per Order, Range: Caster, Area of Effect: Special

This spell creates an umbrella shaped force field over the caster. One foot in diameter per Order of caster, which acts as 15 points of armor against blows from above. The force field will be destroyed when it absorbs 120 points of damage.

UNSEEN SERVANT: Duration: 30 melee plus 1 melee per Order, Range: 1 zeem.

This spell creates a force which can carry up to 20 pounds. This force will fetch and carry for the arcane that summoned it. Only the arcane that summoned it can hear, see, or give it commands. The arcane can dispel it at anytime or it will be dispelled if it receives 6 points of magical damage.

WIZARD'S GLUE: Duration: Permanent, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: 10cm by 10cm per Order with a maximum of 1m by 1m.

Provides the caster with glue having 20 points of STR per point of POW put into the spell. With a maximum number of POW points equal to the caster's Order.
