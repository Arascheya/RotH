Fourth Order spells

ACID CLOUD: Duration: Special, Range: Thrown Item, Area of Effect: 10m radius cloud.

This spell forms a small crystal sphere filled with yellowish brown mist. When the sphere is broken it forms a 10m radius cloud of acid doing 4d4 damage initially plus 1d4 for each melee in the cloud.

The cloud will last 1 melee for every two Orders of the arcane. The crystal ball will last for 10 minutes at which time it will disappear.

BE CIVILIZED: Duration: 1 hour + 10 min for every additional POW point put into the spell, Range: 20m, Area of Effect: One Being per order of arcane.

This spell is reversible. Causes all effected beings to become ultra civilized, polite, and a proper gentleman/lady (John Steed). POW vs POW is required.

BODY BAG: Duration: 3 months, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One container.

When this spell is cast on a container, any body placed inside the container will be preserved for the duration of the spell. This allows transport of the deceased to a location where a resurrection maybe attempted.

BOG: Duration: 10 melee per order, Range: 15m, Area of Effect: 10m diameter area.

This spell turns any horizontal (outdoor non-stone) surface into a soft bog that acts like quicksand. Anyone walking on it sinks at 1 inch per melee per SIZ point, and 1 inch for each additional 50 pounds of weight being carried.

When the spell ends, the area returns to normal, except for bubbles occupied by anyone who sank in. This spell is reversible.

CHARM INSECT: Duration: Special, Range: Self, Area of Effect: 10m radius, Spell Type: Always Active.

This spell will charm any non-intelligent insects in a 10m area around the caster. The caster can prevent them from harming himself or his companions, or send them off in a specified direction. The effects of this spell will last as long as the caster concentrates on it.

CHARM MAMMAL: Duration: Special, Range: Self, Area of Effect: 10m radius, Spell Type: Always Active.

This spell works the same as the Charm Insect spell but for non-intelligent mammals.

CHARM REPTILE: Duration: Special, Range: Self, Area of Effect: 10m radius, Spell Type: Always Active.

This spell works the same as the Charm Insect spell but for non-intelligent reptiles.

COLD: Duration: 10 minutes, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell produces a magical fire, which burns without consuming fuel. A torch lit with Cold Fire will yield light equivalent to a normal torch, but instead of heat, it radiates intense cold. Wood, metal, stone, or even icicles maybe lit with Cold Fire, but this will not ignite other materials by contact.

Normal fires maybe extinguished by Cold Fire or, as a popular party item, drinks may be cooled by the use of this spell. This effect is intensely hazardous to all fire based creatures, causing 1d6 damage per order of arcane if cast on the creature, or 1d4 per order if the creature is struck with a torch with it on it.

COMMAND: Duration: One command, Range: 10m, Area of Effect: One Being

This spell allows the arcane to command the victim to do something, as long as the arcane succeeded in his POW vs POW. The command must be spoken in a language that the victim understands and can only be a sentence of 7 words or less, and the sentence must be active. Such as "Bring me that crystal ball." or "Go, run away." You can not order the victim to do anything that would go against his moral code.

CONDUCTOR: Duration: 1 hour per Order, Range: 10m, Area of Effect: One Continuous Item.

This spell causes any normally non-conductive material to conduct electricity as well as water. If cast on a conductive material, it doubles the conductivity.

CONFUSION: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: One Being per Order.

The arcane designates which creatures he is trying to confuse. Then the POW vs POW is resolved by him making one roll and matching each designated target against that roll.

The victims of this spell will respond by 1-23 wander away from the area, 24-74 stand confused, 74-95 attack nearest creature, 96-00 attack the arcane. At the end of 10 melee rounds the victim returns to normal.

CONTROL OF FIRE: Duration: 10 melee, Range: Self, Area of Effect: Special, Spell Type: Always Active.

This spell is for arcanes who specialize in Fire, Heat, Light, or Defensive type spells only.

This spell allows the Arcane to direct fire type spells thrown at him back at the caster. As long as the arcane concentrates on the Control of Fire spell incoming fire base spells will always rebound to the caster.

CREATURE SUMMONING I: Duration: 2 melee plus 1 melee per level, Area of Effect: One Creature.

With this spell the arcane summons one creature that is naturally SIZ 6 or smaller and is within a 10 zeem range at the time of the spell being cast. The summoned creature will do the arcane's bidding for the duration of the spell if the arcane overcomes the creature's POW. Otherwise the creature is not helpful and will attack the arcane unless the proper safety precautions have been taken.

DELAY LINE: Duration:Special, Range: 10m radius, Area of Effect: One Spell.

This spell causes a spell cast by another arcane to be delayed from 1 to 6 melee rounds as desired. This spell must be cast first and will effect the next spell cast in a 10m radius of the caster.

When the delayed spell completes its action it will act in the same manner as if it had not been delayed.

DREAMWEAVER: Duration: Special, Range: 40m, Area of Effect: One Being + One Being for each additional 2 points of POW.

With this spell the arcane can convey almost any type of information to the recipient. This information will be in the form of a dream, that the recipient will remember upon awakening. This spell will go into effect when the recipient goes to sleep if he is not already asleep.

The arcane can attempt to implant a suggestion through the dream if a successful POW vs POW is made.

ELECTRIC ARC: Area of Effect: the spell Lightning Bolt

This spell allows the arcane to break a lightning bolt into multiple smaller bolts. Each bolt will do 1d6 + 1 point per order with only one bolt per target doing damage to that target. This spell must be cast immediately prior to casting Lightning.

A lightning bolt will give its number of hit die of smaller bolts.

ENCHANT ITEM: Casting Time: See GM.

This spell creates the framework pattern that is combined with another spell to bind it into an item. Preliminary defining patterns must be placed on all parts of the item to be enchanted (all pieces of the set of armor, both boots, etc.) during its construction phase (as near as possible to the beginning of construction as possible, usually after the components have been gathered but before item assembly). Once construction is complete, the rest of the Enchant Item Spell pattern is woven together with the other spell and is placed on the item.

This produces an item with a magic spell in it that requires external Power to activate the spell.

When used with Mend, magical items can be repaired.

EXTEND: Duration: Special, Range: Special, Area of Effect: One Spell.

This spell extends either the range of a spell by 50% or the duration of a spell to either 30 minutes or half again as long whichever is greater. This spell is cast first and the spell to be effected has to be cast within a melee round.

This spell only effects 1st through 3rd Order spells.

FEAR: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 30m Area of Effect: 20m diameter.

POW vs POW with only one roll being made and everyone in the target area matches their POW to that roll. Causing all who are affected to loose faith in himself and his party to win the fight, find the treasure, etc. He will defend himself at full but attack at half value, and will not use offensive magic. With this spell the victim can be attacked without dispelling the spell. If the victim makes and INT * 2 or less he realizes that he has been hit with this spell. Battle Magic Fanaticize 8 or Dispel Magic 8 will negate this spell.

FEIGN DEATH: Duration: 6 melee +1 melee per order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell places the being into a cataleptic state which is impossible to distinguish from death. The being can hear but no feeling or sight is possible. In addition, paralysis, poison, or POW drains will not effect the being while under this spell. Poison injected or otherwise introduced into the body will become effective at the end of the spell.

The spell caster is able to end the effects at any time. It requires a full melee round from the ending of the spell for the effected being to be able to move.

FINDING RITUAL: Duration: Special, Range: Self.

This spell requires 5 minutes of constant work to complete and 7 POW points. Once completed the arcane can track down and find any person, place or thing that he has physically come into contact with within the last year. For each additional POW point added to this spell he can add another year.

The arcane can sense what direction the desired thing is from him and can tell if it is near or far. This ability will last for 6 weeks with an additional 6 weeks added for each additional POW point put into the spell at the time of casting.

If the arcane fails to find the person, place, or thing he is looking for in the time he is allotted he may never use this spell to look for that item again.

IF he uses any other magic during the duration of this spell the spell is broken. The arcane then has to redo the ritual and expend another 7 POW points. Whether the spell is broken or not the allotted time still runs.

FIRE CHARM: Duration: 2 melee per order, Range: 60m but in line of sight, Area of Effect: One Fire, Spell Type: Active.

With this spell the arcane causes a gossamer veil of multi hued flame to circle the fire at a 2m distance. Any creature observing the fire or the dancing circle of flame must resist a POW vs POW or be charmed into remaining motionless and gazing into the flames for the duration of the spell.

While so charmed creatures are subject to suggestions of twelve or fewer words. The charm is broken by any attacks against the charmed creature or if a solid object blocks the creatures view of the fire.

FIRE TRAP: Duration: Until Dispelled, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell sets a magical fire trap on the specified item. When the trap is triggered, a 1d4 per level of caster fireball is released into a 2m radius surrounding the item. The item becomes immune to the fire blast.

The caster may open the item at anytime without setting off the trap.

FUMBLE: Duration: Instant, Range: 160m, Area of Effect: One Being

This spell is aimed against an opponent. A successful POW vs POW requires the victim to roll on the Arduin Fumble Table AG1-61.

HALLUCINATORY TERRAIN: Duration: Permanent, Range: 60m, Area of Effect: 50m by 50m area per Order of Arcane.

This spell lays an illusion over the existing terrain, so that a cliff becomes a rolling hillside, or a rolling hillside becomes a cliff, etc.

The illusion lasts until it is dispelled. If the victim does a successful POW + INT vs the caster's POW + INT then the victim can see through the illusion to the real terrain below, however this take complete concentration. If the victim stumbles into the illusion he gets plus 2 to his POW + INT score to try and see through the illusion.

ICE SHAPE: Duration: 10 minutes per Order, Range: 3m per Order, Area of Effect: One cubic meter per Order.

Given enough water, the arcane can, by stroking gestures freeze it into ice of any shape he likes. It will keep 10 minutes per Order before it begins to thaw unless it is attacked by heat. The reverse thaws ice.

ICE STORM: Duration: 1 melee per Order of Arcane, Range: 4m per Order, Area of Effect: 12m, Spell Type: Active.

When this spell is cast, the arcane causes either large hail stones to pound down (3d10 per Order of Arcane), or driving sleet and freezing rain that will blind creatures and coat everything in ice (DEX * 3 to remain standing).

This spell also lowers the air temperature in the area to 10 degree F for the duration of the spell, the area will slowly warm after the spell is over.

IMPROVED INVISIBILITY: Duration: 10 melee, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being, Spell Type: Passive

This spell makes a living being or an inanimate object invisible. This spell is negated to the end of the melee if the being attacks, or is attacked.

INSTANT BAROQUE: Duration: Permanent, Range: 3m per Order, Area of Effect: One Item.

This spell causes any long, straight piece of material to twist into decorative curlicues. It has been used with good effect on swords, iron bars, pillars, etc. It shortens the item to one-third of its original length. The reverse straightens out curlicues in decorative materials.

INSTANT GOTHIC: Duration: Permanent, Range: 3m per Order, Area of Effect: 3 cubic meters per Order.

This spell causes tall vertical pieces of material, or stacks of material to bend towards each other in an attempt to form gothic arches. If the pieces are fastened at their tops there is not effect. If they are further apart than they are high, they will bend towards each other until the tops are at 45 degree angles to the bottoms.

LAATKA'S ACID SPELL: Duration: 2 melee per order, Range: 40m, Area of Effect: 10m diameter.

All within the area of effect must make a CON*3 or less or they will be stoned for the duration of the spell. Beings must be in the area of effect to be effected. There is a 10% chance of a bad trip (stoned is in reference to LSD type acid of the 60's).

LIQUID FORM: Duration: 4 Turns, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell allows the caster to change one being into liquid form. The person transformed retains bodily control and may flow in the direction desired. This includes up mild slopes of not greater than 1 in 12. The being altered can not be harmed by impact or edged weapons, but is vulnerable to heat and cold. He may return to normal form when ever desired.

MASSMORPH: Duration: Until dispelled, Range: 3m per Order, Area of Effect: Special.

This can only be used on willing beings. With this spell the arcane can make 150 SIZ points per Order of Arcane of willing beings appear to be trees of any sort. Further more these beings can be passed through or touched without breaking the illusion. Any being damaged will bleed thus revealing the illusion as an illusion, POW + INT of caster vs POW + INT of being trying to see through the illusion.

MEMORY ALTERATION: Duration: Special, Range: 60m, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell allows the arcane to actively alter a specific event either changing or replacing the memory of it. A Heal Mind, Restoration, Wish, or Divine Intervention is required to restore the correct memories.

MIND LINK: Duration: 15 min, Range: 160m, Area of Effect: One Being per casting of spell.

This spell allows the participants to communicate telepathically and use each others magic abilities, such as power and knowledge of spells.

Notes:

A character must allow himself to be included in the link.

Only arcanes can share spell knowledge.

Moral effecting spells cast against one member of a link attack all beings link with the target with the same attack roll. This flow goes only to being in direct Mind Link with the target. If a rune type or arcane is effected by an attack on someone in the link his allied spirit or familiar is not effected.

Beings in link defend against magical attacks with their own POW. POW can not be shared for defense purposes.

Any participant may leave the link at anytime.

No permanent spell knowledge can be traded.

If being A is linked with being B and being A is linked with being C beings B and C are not linked.

MIST OF BLACK MISERY: Duration: 10 min, Range: 20m, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell wraps the victim in a clinging black mist, that completely covers them. This mist causes complete sensory deprivation. Sanity rolls are needed for the victim.

MULTISPELL: Duration: 15 min, Area of Effect: Self.

This spell allows the casting of two 1st-3rd Order spells in a melee round at 4th Order, three spells at 5th Order and four spells at 6th Order, for the duration of the spell. Both spells take effect on strike rank 12.

PAIN BLAST: Duration: Special, Range: 40m, Area of Effect: 10m diameter.

This spell causes all creatures in the area of effect to experience excruciating pain. The pains lasts for 25-CON melee, and after the pain stops the victim is in shock for 30-CON minutes unless 1*CON or less on a d100. This also requires a Sanity roll.

PHANTOM SHIELD MAIDENS: Duration: Special.

This spell creates two phantom beings carrying large shields that can parry for the arcane. The parry percentage is 80% with a 40% parry for missile weapons. When the shields have taken 32 points of damage the phantom disappears. Damage accumulates the same as weapons damage ie 4 points carry over.

POLYMORPH: Duration: 10 min per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell allows the caster to partially change one creature into another creature, the change only gives the shape and method of travel.

After the test between sixth and seventh Order has been successfully passed the arcane can cause a complete change to occur. However a system shock roll is needed. As long as the recipient of the spell is willing and knows what he is being changed into success is 95 or below. If the being being changed is not willing and or does not know that he is being changed the roll is CON * 2 or less.

The recipient has to make an intelligence roll to keep from losing himself in the change. If the recipient is expecting the change INT + POW * 3 or less, if not expecting it INT + POW or less. If the roll is failed the recipient now thinks of himself as if he had always been the new creature minus 10 Sanity points immediately.

The first time any being is changed into another there is a Sanity roll. Success adds 1d3 to Sanity, failure is -2d4 points.

At this point the spell may be applied to self as well. The Sanity save is 80% or less since the caster understands what is occuring.

REMOVE CURSE: Duration: Instant, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being.

As the name implies. The arcane must be successful in a [POW + Order + any permanent POW point sacrificed] vs [POW + Order + any permanent POW point sacrificed] of the being doing the cursing.

SHATTERRAY: Duration: Instant, Range: 40m, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell causes a beam of force to radiate out of the caster's palm. Anything struck with this beam take 5d6 concussion damage. For every order above Sixth Order an additional 1d6 of damage is done. For each additional 1d6 in damage it costs an additional POW point.

SPELL SHIELD: Duration: 15 min, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell stops all Third Order Arcane and 3 Point Rune Spells, and acts as 8 points of Counter Magic against Battle Magic Spells.

Incompatible with Shield.

SUNLIGHT SERENADE: Duration: 10 melee per order, Range: 20m, Area of Effect: 10m diameter +1m per order bast fourth, Spell Type: Active.

This spell creates bright golden sunlight and peaceful music in the area of effect. A successful POW vs POW causes undead to discorporate. If the POW vs POW fails the undead still leave the area. All light sensitive type refuse to enter the area.

TONAL ATTACK: Duration: 20 melee per Order, Range: 40m, Area of Effect: One Being plus one additional being for each order over fourth.

This spell produces random disruptive tones in the ears of the target, that the working of magic impossible. A successful POW vs POW is needed for this spell to work.

TRANSPARENCY: Duration: 1 melee per Order, Range: 3m, Area of Effect: One Item.

This spell causes normally opaque materials to become transparent equal to high quality glass. Thus it may be seen through but is not invisible.

Living creatures so enchanted are plus 10% to attack and plus 10% to defense.

The reverse of this spell causes normally transparent materials to become opaque.

WAILING WHEEL OF FIRE: Duration: 10 Melee, Range: 40m, Area of Effect: Special, Spell Type: Always Active.

This spell creates a whirling, wailing pin wheel of fire that showers sparks in all directions (watch out for forest fires). All hit take 4d6 fire damage and a POW vs POW or run in fear.

WALL OF DARKNESS: Duration: 10 min per Order of Arcane, Range: 20m, Area of Effect: 3m + 1m per Order by 3m + 1m per Order by 1m thick wall.

This spell creates a wall of darkness that is impervious to all forms of vision and light. The arcane can see through it similar to a one-way mirror or a Lightwall.

WALL OF FIRE: Duration: 10 min per Order of Arcane, Range: 20m, Area of Effect: 3m + 1m per Order by 3m + 1m per Order by 1m thick wall.

This spell creates a wall of fire and flame at the designated location. The wall does not radiate heat in any way so to take damage a creature has to enter into the wall. Entering into the wall causes 1d6 per Order per 2 strike ranks in the fire. Cold based creatures take double damage.

WALL OF GLUE: Duration: 10 min per Order of Arcane, Range: 20m, Area of Effect: 3m + 1m per Order by 3m + 1m per Order by 1 inch thick wall.

This spell creates a wall of tacky glue at the designated location. Any creature trying to pass through or over it is stuck. The strength of the glue is 40 STR points plus 10 STR points per Order of the arcane.

WALL OF WATER: Duration: 10 min per Order of Arcane, Range: 20m, Area of Effect: 3m + 1m per Order by 3m + 1m per Order by 1m thick wall.

This spell creates a wall of water at the designated location. Any creature passing through it causes 1d6 per Order of the arcane. Fire base creatures take double damage.

WALL OF ICE: Duration: 10 min per Order of Arcane, Range 20m, Area of Effect: 3m + 1m per Order by 3m + 1m per Order by 1m thick wall.

This spell creates a wall of ice with a super cooled water core at the designated location. The wall does not radiate cold in any way. Breaking through the wall causes 1d6 per Order of Arcane for all in a 3m area around the wall. Those that are within 1m take double damage from the super cooled water core. Heat and Fire base creatures take double damage.

WARDING TONES: Duration: 1 melee per Order, Range: 120m, Area of Effect: 10m radius.

This spell creates loud tones which effectively block all sonic attacks against all beings in the area of effect. This included such things as Siren Songs, Banshee Wails, All Drums, and the first use of a Horn of Blasting.

WATER SHAPE: Duration: 10 melee per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell causes water to assume any desired shape and hardness. Such artifacts may have hardness up to that of iron. Such items still appear to be water.

This spell is reversible.

WISHFUL THINKING: Duration: 10 melee per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: Special.

Cast on a being anyone attempting to spy on the subject must over come the POW of the caster or they will hear and see just exactly what they want.

Cast on a written message it will last until dispelled. Anyone reading the message, except the person to whom it is addressed, will read what they want to read.

WITHERED MANHOOD: Duration: Special, Range: 5m, Area of Effect: 1 male being.

Successful POW vs POW causes the target to become totally incapable of any sexual activity requiring the use of male genitalia. This lasts for 1d6 days for each point of power put into the spell. There is no way of removing this spell except time.

WIZARD EYE: Duration: 5 melee per Order, Range: Special, Area of Effect: Special, Spell Type: Always Active.

This spell creates a non-detectable eye which sees through. It travels at twice man speed. It can see with infravision for 3m otherwise with normal human sight. The eye can enter any place as long as there is a crack it can travel through, it can not travel through air tight doors.
