Disperse

Basic Scatter: Order: 10th; Casting Time: See GM.

This spell takes a group of individuals and scatters them in random directions and to a distance of 1 to 1000 meters from the center of the group..

Enhanced Disperse: Order: 16th

This spell scatters the members of the group throughout the current Realm. Distance and direction are random.

Complete Dispersal: Order: 25th

This spell scatters the group throghout the entire multi-verse. Direction, distance, and Realm are completely random.
