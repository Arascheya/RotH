Power Tapping Portal

Tapping portal spell - opens the energy tap that powers all spells not relying only on the Arcane's power - this spell is not really complete - it leaves attach points for other spell pieces to be attached - using this spell by itself leaves an ever increasing energy build going until the Arcane shuts it down - left too long it begins to create temporal and spacial distortions - left uncancelled it can spiral into a self sustaining, ever increasing rip in space-time
