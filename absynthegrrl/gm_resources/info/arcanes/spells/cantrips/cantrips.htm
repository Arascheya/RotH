Cantrips Descriptions

ALTER COLOR: Duration: 1 day, Range: 1m per 10% competency, Area of Effect: Special.

This cantrip will alter the coloring of any organic non-living material. Material component is a bit of dye of the intended color.

ANIMATE CLEANING IMPLEMENT: Duration: 10 minutes, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Item.

This spell animates a mop, broom, brush, rag, bucket, dustpan, etc. causing them to automatically do their designated task. The casting apprentice needs to be in the room the items are working in. The items will take verbal commands such as "You missed a spot in front of the stove." There is a limit of INT/3 items activated at one time.

BOING: Duration: Instant, Range: Special, Are of Effect: Self.

Causes the caster to immediately jump or "hop" 1' per STR point along a horizontal plane. He will climb to a height of two thirds the leap distance at the mid point of his path. For example a character with a strength of 18 would jump 18 feet away his feet being 12 feet off the ground at the mid point of the jump.

Landings are up to the hopper, as no special aid or help is given to him by the spell. The full distance is always covered and can be done from a standing start with legs unflexed.

CHANGE: Duration: Permanent, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Item.

The spell will change the shape of an inanimate item but not the substance. Thus a square 1" X 1" X 12" piece of wood can be changed into a round 1" diameter 12" long wooden rod. This spell is used to train the apprentice to handle polymorph type spells.

CHILL/WARM: Duration: Instant, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Item.

This spell instantly cools the touched item by 40 degrees F. at which time it will start to slowly warm. However it will not freeze an item.

The reverse of this spell will warm an item 40 degrees F. at which time it will start to slowly cool. This will not boil a liquid though.

DUST MAGNET: Duration: 10 minutes, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Dust Rag or Feather Duster.

This spell will cause the rag or duster to act like a magnet to all dust within 1/2 inch of it. This may be used with Animate Cleaning Implement.

EXTERMINATE: Duration: Instant, Range: 3m, Area of Effect: One Cubic Foot.

This spell will kill all small insects (under 3" in length) in a one cubic foot area. For bigger insects the apprentice has to have the insect in his field of vision and target the insect by pointing at it. Maximum sized insect killed is 12" in length.

FRESHEN: Duration: Special, Range: Special, Area of Effect: One Room.

This spell will cause a small freshening spring breeze to circulate through the room the caster is standing in. This is the same as airing a room out in the spring after a long winter.

GIGGLE: Duration: 3 Strike Ranks, Range: 3m, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell is a POW vs POW spell, but the apprentice does not get a power gain roll when this spell is used. This is only so that an apprentice can get a feel of how a POW vs POW spell is used.

INTRO TO SUMMONING: Duration: Instant, Range: 10m, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell allows the apprentice to experiment with summoning spells on houseflies. Usually only one fly will appear, appearing within 1m of the caster at a designated spot. There has to be a fly within 10m of the caster.

LEVITATION 075: Duration: 2 melee, Range: 5m, Area of Effect: One Item of Under 2 ounces in Weight.

This is a very weak levitate spell that is used to teach apprentices how to control spells that need concentration. It is not unheard of for a master to spill a can of nails, tie his apprentice to a chair and expect to have the nails back in the can before the apprentice may eat again.

NUMB: Duration: 10 melee, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Hit Location.

This spell will numb up to 6 hit points of damage to one area. This is not a heal spell, it just numbs the pain away.

This spell was made by a young mage who had had a master that beat him often as an apprentice. This spell is not normally taught by masters but some how most apprentices seem to learn this spell.

PURIFY LIQUID: Duration: Permanent, Range: .1m, Area of Effect: One Gallon.

With this spell the apprentice can purify used oil, dirty water, or any other liquid. Needed for this spell is two containers, and a short length of hose.

The container with the impure liquid is placed higher than the empty container and one end of the hose is lowered into the liquid and the other is put into the empty jar. The purified liquid will run through the hose leaving all the contaminants in the original jar. The apprentice needs to prime the hose with ten drops of the desired pure liquid.

SEW: Duration: Special, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One seam.

This spell causes a magical needle to appear in the caster's hand. The caster then runs the needle along the area to be stitched, the needle then threads itself with the thread supplied by the caster. A pair of scissors appears and cuts the thread to the correct length. This stitching will last as long as normal stitches would, providing that the thread is the right thread for the job.

The reverse of this spell will cause the scissors to appear and cut the seam it has been rub on.

SUMMONING 101: Duration: Instant, Range 30m, Area of Effect: One mouse.

This spell is a continuation to the Intro to Summoning Spell, the apprentice must have Intro at 75% before he will be taught this spell.

With this spell the caster causes a mouse to appear within 1m of him at a designated spot. There has to be a mouse within 30m of the caster for this spell to work.

WEED KILLER: Duration: Special, Range: .33m, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell is normally used to kill weeds in gardens, this spell will do 10 hit points of damage to weeds, the average weed requires .25 hit point to be killed. This spell lasts until dispelled or until 10 hit points worth of weeds are killed.

The spell is used by pointing a finger at the undesired weed and saying the word "Ortho".
