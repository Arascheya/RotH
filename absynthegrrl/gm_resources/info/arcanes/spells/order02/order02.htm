Second Order spells

ANTI-WEB AURA: Duration: 10 melee, Range: Self Only.

This spell does not allow any webs, magical or otherwise, to stick to the arcane. This spell is incompatible with Shield.

AUDIBLE GLAAMAR: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 30m + 3m/Order, Area of Effect: Range of Hearing, Spell Type: Active.

This spell causes a volume of sound to arise, at what ever distance the caster desires, and seeming to recede, close, or remain at a fixed place as desired. This spell will reproduce 50 size points plus 10 size points per Order, meaning that the caster can make the noise of one size 50 dragon, 200 small rats, 4 men, etc.

BINDING: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell halves the movement of those effected by it. It can never reduce movement to zero. BLIND SPOT: Duration: 5 min per Order, Range: 45m, Area of Effect: One Being, Spell Type: Active.

The arcane must point at his intended victim and if the victim's POW is overcome the arcane becomes TOTALLY invisible to the victim. Not even True Sight, magical items, mirrors, etc will reveal the arcane to the victim.

CONTINUAL LIGHT: Duration: until dispelled, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell is similar to the Light spell, except it will light an area of 20m radius. This spell cancels the Dark spell without losing any of its power. A Continual Dark spell coming in contact with a Continual Light spell causes both spells to fail.

The reverse of this spells is Continual Dark, it has the same properties as the Continual Light spell.

DETECT CULT AND STATUS: Duration: Instant, Range 80m, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell is directed on one being and a POW vs POW is needed. If the arcane overcomes the target's POW then the arcane knows the Cult and Status of the being. DETECT DEMON/DEMON INFLUENCE: Duration: 1 melee, Range: Self, Area of Effect: 40m.

Detects any creature possessing demon taint or being controlled by a demon. This spell is blocked by 3m of rock or metal. Usable by the arcane only.

DETECT ILLUSION: Duration: 1 melee, Range: Self, Area of Effect: 40m

Causes any illusions glow violet for the arcane only.

DETECT INVISIBILITY: Duration: 1 melee, Range: Self, Area of Effect: 40m.

Detects any invisible or out of phase creature or object. This spell is blocked by 3m of rock or metal. Usable by the arcane only. There is a component of a squeeze ball filled with corn starch for this spell. While the arcane is vocalizing the spell he makes hand gestures with the squeeze ball blowing dust out in 360 degrees around him. The corn starch will adhere to the invisible creature or item and will glow to all who are in the room.

DETECT LIFE: Duration: 1 melee, Range: Self, Area of Effect:40m.

Detects life forms SIZ 3 and up, breaking them into two categories; SIZ 3 to 21 and SIZ 22 and larger. This spell is stopped by 3m of solid rock or metal.

DROP: Duration: Instant, Range: 6m plus 3m per Order, Area of Effect: One Being.

Will cause the victim to drop what ever he is holding. A successful POW vs POW is needed.

DRY SPELL II: Duration: Instant, Range: 3m per Order, Area of Effect: .3 cubic meters per Order.

This spell can remove enough liquid from any one object to completely dry it out. The liquid will reappear somewhere else within the caster's range at a point specified. This spell may not be used against living plants or animals.

DUNKLEMEYER'S SPELL OF THE TRANTELLA: Duration: 5 melee/Order, Range: 20m, Area of Effect: 10m diameter.

All hit begin a wild uncontrolled whirling jumping dance, they can not stop and whirl in random directions. This is a POW vs POW spell, the arcane makes one roll only which is matched against all in the area of effect.

ENHANCED WEB: Duration: 20 melee, Range: 20m, Area of Effect: 6m diameter sphere.

This spell is similar to the Basic Web spell except for breaking purposes it has a STR of 80.

ESP: Duration: 10 melee per Order, Range: 2m per Order up to 30m. Area of Effect: Special, Spell Type: Active

With this spell the arcane is able to probe the surface thoughts of one sentient creature per melee round. Caster must make a successful POW vs. POW to obtain these thoughts.

EYES OF FIRE: Duration: 10 melee per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell has two forms. The first form cause the eyes to glow one solid color, caster's choice of color. Pupils, whites, and irises can not be seen, all parts of the eye glow.

The second form of the spell cause the eyes to appear to have fire burning in the pupils. All parts of the eye appear normal except for the small fires. The flames will be what ever color the caster wants.

This spell does not in anyway effect the user's vision. This spell may have a permanency spell cast on it.

FANATICIZE: Duration: 10 melee, Range 50m, Area of Effect: Two Beings per Order.

This spell is similar to the Battle Magic spell Fanaticism. One POW vs POW is rolled and all targetted intelligent beings' POW is matched to the roll those losing are effected.

Victims of this spell get get 1.5 times their attack percentages, they will not parry or cast any spells and it halves the Defense Bonus. To dispel this spell a four point Battle Magic Dispel Magic or an Arcane Dispel Magic is needed.

FORGET: Duration: Permanent, Range: 10m, Area of Effect: 6m radius.

This spell causes all creatures within the area of effect to forget the events of the previous one minute of time. For every three Orders experience of the caster another minute is forgotten. This spell will not negate charms, suggestions, geases, or similar spells.

A full Wish will restore the lost memories, Divine Intervention will also bring back the lost time.

ILLUSION OF NO-CHANGE: Duration: Special, Range: 3m per Order, Area of Effect: 3 cubic meters per Order.

This creates the illusion that some real thing has not changed, moved, etc.

INSTANT ODORS: Duration: Special, Range: Special, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell causes a very strong odor to emanate from the arcane at the rate of .3m radius per Strike Rank for 1 melee round plus 1 Strike Rank per Order. Duration there after is up to the winds and air currents.

Note, however, that the odor must be one that the arcane has access to while he is learning the spell. When the spell is first learned the arcane can learn five different odors, for each additional week the arcane puts into learning this spell he gets five additional odors. The arcane can put in additional weeks at any time in his career.

INVISIBILITY: Duration: 10 melee, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being, Spell Type: Active.

This spell makes a living being invisible. This spell is negated if the character attacks, is attacked, or if the caster's concentration is broken.

KNOCK: Duration: Instant, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: Object Touched.

This spell will open non magical locks, for magical locks enough POW must be put into the spell to over come the magic of the lock spell (see Countermagic in RQ).

LAATKA'S LIGHTENING SPELL: Duration: 20 melee per Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: Object Touched.

This spell causes the item touched to lose 90% of its weight (but not mass). Think about it.

LEOMUND'S TRAP: Duration: Permanent, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: Object Touched.

This spell makes an item appear trapped when it is not.

LEVITATE: Duration: 10 Melee per Level, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell will allow the recipient to either move 7m vertical or 13m horizontal. This spell will lift 200 pounds. For each additional point of POW put in at the casting of the spell the recipient can either move an additional 7m vertical, 13m horizontal, or 200lb.

LOCATE OBJECT: Duration: 1 melee round/Level, Range: 20m + 5m/Level, Spell Type: Active.

For the duration of the spell or as long as the spell is being concentrated on the Arcane will know the direction of the object being looked for if the object is with in range. This spell can not be used to locate living beings. The Arcane can do nothing but a slow walk while concentrating on this spell.

MAGICAL CONSCIENCE: Duration: Permanent, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Item.

This spell is cast on an object and is permanent unless dispelled. If the object is removed from its proper location by an other than its lawful owner or other authorized person, it will form a magic mouth and begin to shout loudly and continuously "Thief, Help robbery, I belong to, etc. Any other items within audible range will take up the cry.

MAGIC EYE: Duration: Special, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Item.

This spell is cast on an object and then, when the trigger event happens it will cause a magic eye to appear on the object and remain for 10 melee per Order of arcane. While the eye lasts, the caster can see all that it sees. If more than one eye turns on at once, the arcane can choose which to see through, and switch about as he likes.

MAGIC MOUTH: Duration: Special, Range: Special, Area of Effect: One Object.

When this spell is cast, the Arcane impowers the chosen object with an enchanted mouth which suddenly appears and speaks the message stored by the Arcane at the occurrence of a specified event. The message can be of 25 words or less in a language known by the Arcane. It can not speak spells.

MARBLES: Duration: 10 melee per Order, Range: 3m per Order, Area of Effect: 1 square meter.

By using this spell the arcane can create enough marbles to cover a one square meter area per Order. Anyone stepping on an area covered by these marbles must roll DEX*2 or less each melee or fall down.

The reverse vanishes round pebbles, small eggs, bearings, etc.

MIRROR IMAGE: Duration: 2 melee/Order, Range: Self.

This spell allows the arcane to create 1-4 exact duplicates of himself. These images come into being around the arcane and mimic his every move. It is impossible for attackers to know for certain which is the arcane and which is an image. Any image hit disappears and does not return.

MOON RUNES: Duration: One Year, Range: Touch.

These are silvery, shining letters, drawn with the finger on any reasonably solid surface. They may glow constantly or be set to shine under certain conditions. Likewise, they maybe set to fade under certain conditions or after a specific amount of time up to one year.

OPPENHAZER'S MISTAKE: Duration: 1d100 melee, Area of Effect: 1m diameter area.

Many centuries ago, somewhere in the Multiverse, the illustrious magician Wannamaker Oppenhazer attempted to invent a conjuration that would cause water to condense out of the air above the mage's head so that he might shower in the wilds and keep clean. He wanted to call this magic "Oppenhazer's Helpful Bath" but what he came up with has, to this day, been called his "Mistake".

This spell will cause a miniature snow and hail storm to form right above the arcane's head. This storm, once fully formed, is stationary and very cold (about 30 degrees F). It will deposit 1d12 inches of snow and hail over the duration of the spell. This spell in and of itself does no damage.

PRESERVE VEGETABLE MATTER: Duration: 1 month, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One small bunch of herbs.

This spell will preserve the potency of plants for one month (17 Weeks). The plants have to be fresh for this spell to work, picked within the last hour. The spell will preserve 6 doses worth of herbs for each casting

PROTECTION FROM NORMAL MISSILES: Duration: 10 melee, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell protects the recipient from hurled or projected missiles of the non-magical variety. This spell is incompatible with Shield.

PYROTECHNICS: Duration: Special, Range: 100m, Area of Effect: Special.

With this spell the arcane can create a fireworks display with the following restrictions. One burst of light (arcanes choice of colors) per Order. The arcane can either fire off all of his light bursts at once or have them one after another, or a combination.

Example: a Fifth Order Arcane can either have five bursts of light at the same strike rank or he can have one on strike rank 5 and from that burst of light a "rocket" is fired in a random direction from which the second burst comes on strike rank 7. This process can continue until all five bursts are gone or the arcane can have a combination of the two effects.

The arcane choices how far away the first burst will appear and all bursts after that will be that distance from the last burst. Color, distance, and burst pattern must be decided while the spell is being worked up, after the spell goes off the arcane can not change anything. These bursts also cause the smell of gun powder and smoke, but no damage.

RAY OF ENFEEBLEMENT: Duration: 1 melee/Order, Range: 3m + 1m/Order, Area of Effect: one creature.

By this spell the arcane weakens the STR of an opponent and all attacks and skills based on STR. The arcane must make a successful POW vs. STR. This spell removes 4 STR points plus 1 per Order so at 2nd Order the arcane removes 6 points of STR if successful at a cost of 1 POW point per Order. This spell cannot take a creature to zero STR points it will automatically leave 1 point of STR even if the arcane could have removed all. The arcane loses the "extra" POW points that were put in.

ROPE TRICK: Duration: 20 melee/Order, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Rope.

When cast upon a piece of rope from 2-10m in length, one end of the rope rises into the air until the whole is hanging perpendicular, as if attached at the upper end. The upper end is, in fact, fastened in an extra dimensional space. The arcane and up to five others can climb up the rope and into this place of safety. The rope can be pulled up after as long as less than six people are in the space. If the people are not out by the time the spell runs out they will fall to the ground.

SCARE: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: One Being.

When the arcane overcomes the victim's POW, the victim loses faith in the ability of himself and his party to succeed at what they are doing. The victim will still defend at full capability but attacks at half value. The victim will not use offense magic. If the victim makes a 2*INT or less he will recognize that the spell has been cast on him.

This spell is reversible making the being it is cast on a fanatical fighter. His attacks will be half again as good, he will not parry, and his defense will be half. The victim of the reverse does not care if a spell has been cast on him, and will not cast ANY spells himself.

This spell and its reverse can be countered with a dispel magic of the appropriate strength, time, or an opposite spell being cast on the victim.

SHATTER: Range: 20m, Area of Effect: One Object.

This spell causes non-magical objects of crystal, glass, ceramic, or porcelain to shatter. The arcane can shatter 10 pounds per Order.

STAFFORD'S SWING: Duration: Special, Range: 20m.

When the arcane first learns this spell he rolls 1d100 to determining the base number of melee rounds this spell lasts. To this figure is added 1 melee round per Order of the arcane.

This spell creates a rope of magical energy up to 20m in length. This rope will be anchored immovable to the spot indicated during the casting and will end in the arcane's outstretched hands. This line will anchor anywhere: on a wall, in the air, in water, etc.

The rope is indestructible to all physical forces. However, even one point of magical force will cut it; i.e. a Blade Sharpened weapon, a Fireball, a Lightning Bolt, etc.

STINKING CLOUD: Duration: 1 melee/Order, Range: 10m, Area of Effect: 3m radius.

This spell causes a billowing mass of fog to appear at the target point. Any creature caught within this cloud makes a resistance roll based on their own POW vs. their own CON. If their POW wins they are only effected while they are in the cloud. If their CON wins they are effect while in the cloud and for 3 + d6 minutes.

STRENGTH: Duration: 10 melee, Range: 80m, Area of Effect: One Being.

This spell adds half again to the being's STR, up to the being's species maximum STR.

SLIPPERY SPELL: Duration: 10 melee/Order, Range: 20m, Area of Effect: 3m squared (9m area) plus a 2m area per Order after this.

Causes a zero coefficient friction area. This is a field that rests on the surface it is cast on.

TENSER'S FLOATING DISC: Duration: 30 melee rounds plus 1 melee round per Order, Range: 6m.

With this spell the arcane creates a 1m circular plane of null gravity. This disk will hold 100lb per Order of the arcane and floats about 1m above ground at all times and has to remain within 6m of the arcane or the spell ceases to be. Only one of these can be in effect at a time.

TRANSFER STICKINESS: Duration: 1 year per Order, Range: 3m per Order, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell transfers the stickiness from one substance to another. The spell will effect one pint per Order or one square meter per Order of surface. Both substances must be within range of the spell.

WINE TO POISON: Duration: 5 melee, Range: Touch, Area of Effect: One Container of Wine.

The arcane must touch the wine itself, the wine is then a poison of a potency equal to the arcane's power at the time of casting. After 5 melee the wine is just wine, and an arcane of 7th Order or greater is needed to detect that the wine was magically tampered with.

WIND: Duration: 1 melee round, Range: Special, Area of Effect: Special.

This spell produces either a gust of wind for one strike rank or a steady breeze for a melee round. The gust of wind can bowl man sized creatures over. The average of the SIZ and DEX of the being in the path of the wind verses the POW of the arcane casting the spell. If the being is overcome it is knocked off its feet, if it is not overcome then it is just staggering.

The steady breeze causes small flames to dance about, it fans flames and fires of larger size making them hotter, it will hold back moving clouds of vapor for the duration of the wind, and it will move stagnant air and poisonous gasses in the direction it is blowing.
