City of Knowledge - Info

RAZAKS- Legendary high demons said to be only demons capable of defeating Ancients in battle

CONFLICT OF THE PROPHECY-final battle between forces of demon taint and others, unclear if it includes Razaks and Ancients

CAVERNS OF HISTORY- labyrinth like maze of caves and passages said to contain a library of the Ancients, possibly under the Gardens of Lazaar

GARDENS OF LAZAAR- mythical gardens of the Ancients thought to be accessible by some means beyond the Aryamons through a pass near the city of Rumpsville

CENTIPEDES AND SILVER WORMS- shows you a small piece of circuit board

SHRINE OF FERTILITY- could be any temple to the fertility goddess Tamera or perhaps the main temple beyond the Aryamons

MEN OF TIN- nothing

MOUNTAIN OF FIRE- only heard of as generic term for volcano

CHAMBER OF COLD RADIANCE- seen periodically in Ancient sites usually found beyond a door with a bound fire rune on it. the chamber glows with a cool bluish light

SILVERED LEAD SUIT- picture of a silver colored garment that completely covers wearer

HALL OF CONFUSING CHAMBERS- thought to be a trap or maze in Caverns of History complex

SPHINX WEBS- some sort of Ancient majik for teleportation found between two granite sphinxes facing each other

FIERY CHARIOT- the chariot ridden by the Ancient in charge of the sun, it gives off light and heat as it moves

BASE OF THE BLUE MOON- nothing

CENTIPEDE OF PURPOSE- mentioned in the History of the Time Before, a black rectangular insect of hard, smooth substance with 16 gold legs

SCARAB OF POWER- ring with a dull grey stone shaped like a scarab beetle also mentioned in the History of the Time Before

SWIRLING STONE OF KNOWLEDGE- clear seven sided stone with moving swirls of purple within also mentioned in the History of the Time Before

ONEMIND- demon king worshipped by Razak demons, said by them to be all powerful. worship consists of trying to remove as much individuality from self as possible

GREY CLOUD- last Razak demon attack launched at Ancients

MAJIK MAY ENTER BUT NEVER LEAVE- only allows majik of any form to pass through in one direction, often placed on a single room, only way around is to be able to dispel spell

SPIRALS OF HELL- runic spirals that transport anything that successfully walks it to another point, extremely hard and dangerous to walk as chances of success are fairly small

TECH TALENT- skills with Ancient majik
