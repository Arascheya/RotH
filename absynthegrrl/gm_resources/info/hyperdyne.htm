Hyperdyne Energy

Hyperdyne was founded by Nikola Tesla, Lord Willem, and the chief engineer as a way to experiment with power generation. In the beginning this is to provide motive force for the channel tunnel trains, but it rapidly expands from there.

Hyperdyne uses various esoteric generation methods. The least weird is the nuclear plants. After that are fusion reaction systems and at the leading edge of the spectrum are planetary core taps and matter/antinmatter systems.
