DevelStar Resource Developers Group

Outer Systems resource development company. This is one of the two opportunistic development companies that handle most Outer Systems resource gathering. Rival of OutSystems Resource Development Company.

DevelStar is not quite as rapacious as OutSystems but about as poor when it comes to working conditions for their employees. There is a constant corporate espionage campaign between the two companies. There are several other development corporations, but these are the biggest and baddest of the bunch.
