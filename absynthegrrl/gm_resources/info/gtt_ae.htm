GTT - Global Telecom and Technology

This is a conglomerate of previously existing smaller telecom companies. There are still independent providers, often leasing circuits through GTT, however their numbers continue to shrink.

GTT has expanded into entertainment equipment and entertainment IP supply. Rumors exist of their using embedded messaging to alter world perceptions. These rumors have not yet been substantiated. A related rumor indicates this influence technology may be contracted by anyone with sufficient capital.
