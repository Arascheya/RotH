DSC Starship - Drunken Sailer Constructors

The name started as a joke because early DSC ships were somewhat less than perfect in terms of dimensions and squareness. All these imperfections were cosmetic. A DSC ship could withstand an amasing amount of punishment before failing, making them a favortite among the Fringe Systems and Independent Frieghter crowds. Also, because of the imperfections, they were willing to sell at lower margins than many of the Core Systems manufacturers.

DSC and YES are the linked extensions of ITC from the old home world.
