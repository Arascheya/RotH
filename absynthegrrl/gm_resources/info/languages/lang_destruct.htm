Language of the Destructor - Proto-Tongue

A very old language form used mostly in spells of Destruction and Soul Manipulation. The following are some of the words from that tongue, followed by the final section of the Ritual to Bind a Hell Gate and create a Keeper.

Calling it the language of the Destructor is misleading. This is actually Proto-Tongue, the first language of the multi-verse, spoken by both the Creator and Destructor as they coalesced.

Rite of Binding - Final Section

inzaktormas alks zhilkahnash riahmzikna ziblanzkna

akhblas mahklahzkna ith nilikhnamaz riahmzikna

nilikhnamaz rihachzisas nimiktahl

rihachzis nibrachaum akhblas inzaktorm

nilikhnamaz kaklakazklus niri hachzis

klaraahzkna maliashzhk hachzis

klaraahzkna maliashzhk rakelispthaz

kla raziblanzkna kilikchanzahk

Translation

souls are rendered to you Destructor

this blood and power to you

power to Portals grant

to Portal bind this soul

power concentrate into Portal

my will bends Portal

my will bends soul of Keeper

will of Destructor over all
