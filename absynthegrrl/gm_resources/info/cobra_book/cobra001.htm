Book of Zeltrop

Volume 1 - Pre Elentaryn

11 Xer ALW

It has been decided that a diary will be kept so that future generations will understand what has gone before and what the basis is for our policies. I, Morietal, have been selected to perform this duty.

It was also decided to restart our calendar on the xer the war ended. It has been 10 xers since the final missiles and bombs hit. This area is large and due to the prevailing winds has been kept remarkably clear of radiation. We are hoping that there are other areas as fortunate as we are here. Hopefully we will be able to get in touch with other areas, if they do exist, very soon. A couple of small private aircrafts survived and enough of a fuel supply to be able to at least check the surrounding 200 zeems or so. Not only for radiation but also survivors and supplies of food, clothing and live stock. We are going to be very desperate if none of the animal life survived.

We have set up at the large civilian medical facilities a few zeems away from the military command post.

14 Xer ALW

We are going to bring all the survivors we can find here. It is hoped that in this way we may unify the entire planet through the need to survive and a lasting peace will have a better chance. We have been lucky in finding of enough fuel to reconnaissance this entire side of the mountains, as well as food and parts for the planes and radiation meters.

We have decided to restructure our government in the following manner. Each of the major uncontaminated areas will have a representative on a governing council. There will also be one person to act as an independent representative of the planet as a whole, keeping the entire planet wide picture in focus. This person will be accessible to all people and will be available in case one of the regional representatives refuses to hear any particular issue.

We have unearthed the medical facilities that were part of the command complex here. Thank everything that the only missile to get through was a dud and did not explode. As soon as we get a power source up and running we will be able to see what type of genetic damage has been done. My worst fears are that too many of us will be sterile or only able to produce abominations. The General was so against some of the medical research that went on by the military, not so much the goals but the ways the information was extracted. I will see that is not done as long as I live.

I don't know what I would do without Kareena. She is so skilled at soothing the ruffled feathers and strained nerves. A real peacemaker, not to mention our only surviving medical person.

18 Xer ALW

Searching the area around the medical facility at the command head quarters has turned up a tunnel leading off roughly in this direction. Following it to its end we have found the command computer. It had shut itself down into a wait state to conserve its batteries. Now we have another reason for wanting a power source, with this computer we can do our search so much faster. Of course once we get power we still have to break into the thing. All of the security cleared computer techs were killed.

23 Xer ALW

We have completed the search on this side of the mountains. The news is worse than I imagined. On the average only 10 out of 10,000 persons survived. I am devastated as this area should not have had that great a death toll. From the evidence 1 out of 100 killed themselves and/or their families within 3 hours of The War starting. We are all demoralized. However some good news is that we have found a small group of doctors, surgeons, and Research Physicians having their vacation on an island in a remote lake in the surrounding mountains. I do not know whom was more glad to see who. They were running low on food and drinkable water.

27 Xer ALW

The ratio of men to women is 2 to 1. If there has to be an imbalance then it would be by far better for the survival of the race if it ran more women to men.

We are working too hard just to get enough clean food and water, without searching the whole planet for survivors and melding them in with us here. But I feel that it is too important not to. I have the strong feeling/knowledge that if we are unified now when all that was has been destroyed that we can weld together a lasting peace.

The Last War was fought with nuclear type weapons. So we know that the land itself will be totally destroyed in some areas, and will stay that way for many zyrodyls. So far only 20-25 percent of the land we have surveyed has been totally saturated. An other 50% will be usable in 1000 zyrodyls or so. With hard research we maybe able to come up with something to speed up the process.

30 Xer ALW

It may sound petty but we decided that as long as we were making our society and when time is measured from that we would remake our calendars. So from this xer forward there will be 85 xers to a zerad, 7 zerads to a zyrodyl. So to xer is the 30th xer of the 1st zerad of zyrodyl 1.

35 Xer, 1 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

We have found some bigger ships that are being made usable. With the small ships for survey and the large ships to bring in survivors when we find them this should save time and fuel.

My biggest problem is how to get to the other continents. I will just have to sleep on it, going to sleep with a problem on my mind tends to bring an answer by morning. I have not had this happen so often before. I wonder if it is the slight radiation that all of us have been exposed to, or something else.

47 Xer, 1 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

The survey of this continent is finished. We will start on the islands immediately off shore and just keep spreading out until the entire planet is surveyed or we run out of fuel. With the islands we will fly over them and if there are people then we will send a boat. If there is safe food we will land and get it. As we get more organized we will move volunteers to other safe areas to start growing food. But first we have to get seed for this to work.

49 Xer, 1 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

We have established the civilian hospital and the caverns under it as the seat of the new government as so far it is the largest clear area we have found. We are going to have the area outside the caverns made into gardens. They will be named "The Gardens of Lazaar" after The General of the combined armies of the Allied Free Countries. For even thought he was a military man he worked for peace and the good of the people where ever he could.

We have realized that technology was not to blame for the war and have pledged to live in peace in the hopes that another one will never occur. Our main technological goal, at the moment, is medical research. The main goal after taking care of the injured and sick is gene codes for birth defects. It is hoped that if the gene carrying the defect could be isolated and studied it can be eliminated. Either by some technique not now known or just by not breeding if the gene damage is of a certain type or severity.

53 Xer, 1 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

Great news! We found a small village on one of the larger islands that is intact and has seed and fertilizer for growing crops. They also have living healthy meat animals. They are willing to join our cause. This is the first whole town of people we have found. Due to the winds and the fact that there wasn't a primary target near them, they are all healthier than we are. They have fruit trees as well, and vegetables. We were able to eat fresh food for the first time since war was declared. I had forgotten how good it was. Gardens and crops just moved up in priority. None of us realized how hungry we were for fresh food.

There are a couple small islands near our new friends that are growing wild fruit trees. I am overrun with volunteers to go and take care of them and see about putting in a couple crops. We are so fortunate that we are as close to the equator as we are. The weather has not been a major problem for us, but for others cold weather may just well be. We are going to start in surveying the hemisphere that is in winter. None of us feel comfortable continuing in the survey pattern we had set up now that weather has been pointed out to us.

71 Xer, 1 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

We have a working hydro power generator. We now can do so much more. Communications, medical and one of the teenagers from our island community was working on an electrical flyer. With a little help ironing out some of the bugs, he has a viable design. We were running out of fuel, plus none of us like the pollutants from the current engines.

For the first time I feel like we might just make it. Our first baby was born, alive and well. Granted she was conceived before the War but she is the first one to be born alive. She is going to be spoiled rotten by everyone. I hope that she does not get upset when the first baby to be conceived after the War is born.

5 Xer, 2 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

We have been so busy trying to survive that we are losing track of the importance of sapient life. So the council's first decision is to have a Winter Solstice Celebration. We will celebrate the return of sensibility, and that we are alive. The announcement only went out to xer but everyone is really getting into a celebrating spirit.

30 Xer, 2 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

The solstice party went off well. The morale of the entire population has gone up. The majority of the people are still in a kind of shock. As long as you tell them what to do they are ok. Luckily some of the first ones found are showing signs of coming out of it, so there is hope for the rest.

On the planet wide search we are starting to run into weather problems in the hemisphere that is in winter. The electric aircraft works, which really helps. We have started building more. So far only one man and a radiation counter sized but it leaves the other craft available for transporting stuff. As soon as the design engineers are satisfied with the test model we will build bigger ones. The hold up is that it is more of a light powered glider than anything.

48 Xer, 2 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

Good news Kareena~ is pregnant with our child. She is the first woman to become so. I wish we had a way of reading genes. I am afraid for our child, but I can but wait. I now realize something else we have lost, we have lost all contact with the deity.

62 Xer, 2 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

I had not mentioned to anyone about the loss of contact with the deity, but to xer in council it was brought up. A committee has been appointed to try and find any information on the worship of the deity. Even the people from the other continents said that the worship of the deity had lost favor and appeal generations before the war. We have even lost its name. This also could have contributed to the war.

85 Xer, 2 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

I have not wanted to enter into this diary for the last few xers. Kareena~ had an accident at the hospital. A patient mad with grief and pain started to attack people. Kareena~ was one of them. The doctors don't know yet if she will survive the blow to the head. She miscarried. The only good news out of this is that when Dr. Eksivail examined the fetus it was healthy and showed no sign of deformity. What happened to the patient that caused this the doctors will not tell me. Maybe they are right that it is better not to know.

Some leads have been found to the problem of the missing deity, but it is not very hopeful as so many records were destroyed. We will continue searching, but with the accident at the hospital I find myself wanting to, I think the term was, pray to something bigger than I am for help.

1 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

Last night I had a very vivid dream about where to find our deity. It may seem crazy but I told the committee about it and they are going to go and look for the place of my dream.

4 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

The committee came back to xer and said they found a chamber just like in my dream, with everything my dream had in it. Complete with an old holy man who had preserved the faith and artifacts in the countries held by the Dictator Jardiral. We are hoping to find some thing that will tell us if this man and his artifacts are for real, at his insistence. So we are going to dig up some carbon dating equipment and such.

7 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

We have gotten all the people out of the harsh weather areas in the northern hemisphere. The search goes on to the southern hemisphere before winter sets in down there. Luckily there is not much axial tilt and we have an almost perfect circular orbit, so winters are not as severe as they could be.

The ratios are looking better, 1 male to every 3 females, it gives us a better survival chance since there are fewer survivors than were estimated. People are forming group families instead of the monogamous relationships that were the preference before the war, I think that it is mainly to share the children, we have so few.

Kareena~ came out of her coma to xer, she even recognized me and said my name. The doctors said that this is a very good sign. I went and talked to the old holy man afterward. Even if he is a fake he brought comfort to me. He also gave me a book on how to let my conscious mind hear the subconscious mind.

When I have the time I will have to look in the tunnels and caves under the caverns we are in. But if what the old man and the book said are true then this ring is the cause of the ideas that come to me during the night. The old man said that the reason the ideas come at night are because that is when my sub conscious mind and my conscious mind are the closest. But that with work I could learn to be more accessible to it in a meditative state.

9 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

We are really making so much progress it is really hard to believe. Of course we all are putting in such long hours, but that does not explain it all. Just too many things have gone right the first time. I think our deity is still with us. According to the old Holy Man the deity is called Ertan (pronounced Air+tan). The search for the carbon dating equipment has gone well and we should have some results later to xer or tomorrow.

Kareena~ was released from the hospital to xer. She must still take it very slow and build back her strength. But Dr. Eksivail thought that she would do better out of the hospital and in an area where she feels safe and away from the reminder of the attack on her. She will sleep a lot, I have been told, as the body repairs itself. We tend to have forgotten that the body can fix itself most of the time if we just leave it alone long enough.

14 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

Carbon dating proves that the artifacts and the books are as old as the old man says they are. Most of us believe him anyway. Maybe because we want to believe.

I wish the general had lived long enough to tell me more about this ring he gave me. It has a seven sided stone of a strange bluish purple crystal that has motes of light swirling in it. His last words about it were that he had found it "down below". I don't know where he could have been referring too.

If only we could get into the information stored in the war computer, we could shorten the time of land survey. The computer would know where our missiles were headed and what incoming ones got through. Also I would really like to be able to convert it to something useful. The medical people could really use the number crunching and storage capabilities of a large machine. I will have to put the problem to the council, maybe we can allot more time to cracking into the hunk of junk.

16 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

The council has decided to assign our 3 most computer literate people full time to work on getting into either the information stored in the machine or to converting it to something we can use.

25 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

To xer, a small group of children were playing in the caverns, and found a secret door at the end of the passage way second from the left. Being bored they decided to explore rather than get an adult, and they got lost. We had the entire population out looking for them. After the door was a stair down into, what is now being called, the Tunnels of Confusion. We found all but one boy in these tunnels. It was a miracle we found them at all. It ended up with two people standing at every intersection down there. We could not even follow the tracks because of how fine the dust is. A track just does not hold in that powder. After getting some sleep I will go back down with some balls of string. One person tried to map the area but could not get it right. Almost drove the fellow nuts.

On our way back with the children we noticed that the walls of the tunnels and caverns after the secret door are smooth as glass. The walls of the cavern we have been using are natural rough cave type walls. These were constructed by someone with a technology that we do not possess. I will have to tell the council my thoughts on this. Maybe visitors from outer space is not such a far fetched idea after all.

26 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

I found the little boy just before noon to xer. He had somehow gotten through the Tunnels of Confusion to another hidden door. Had I not been running my hand along the walls and attaching the string every few feet I would not have found the panel that opened the door. Apparently he had found the door open but it had closed after he had gone through and could not find or reach the panel to open the door again.

The workmanship is fantastic. Even knowing where the door is I cannot find the cracks or the panel. I looked around for the panel on the other side and it took awhile to find it. I don't know whether to tell everyone about this or explore it on my own. I have mixed feelings. I wonder if this is the "down below" the General was talking about? It is possible that these caverns hook up with where the computer is. Tactically it would be a good place to locate it, under a major civilian hospital.

27 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

In council to xer we talked about the find of the hidden door and what could be beyond it. I told them of my thoughts of the construction of the caverns and of there possibly being a way through to the computer. We are sending a team of volunteers to explore the area. In all of recorded history none of us has ever heard of any other beings on our world. It is felt that there probably are not any living beings down there.

I will go with them, my curiosity would not allow me not to. Kareena~ has decided to go with me. Dr. Eksivail thinks that it is a good idea. He also told me that Kareena~ is in no danger from the man who attacked her as he died shortly before she regained consciousness.

29 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

We leave xer after tomorrow on our exploration. We are planning on being gone 3 xers to a week. We will have enough food and water for an additional 2 xers. It is hoped that we will find a way into the area that is easier than the Tunnels of Confusion.

36 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

There is more to the underground area than I thought there was. There are natural underground caverns with large pools and lakes with streams connecting them. On the same level as the Tunnels of Confusion, but past them, are lots of rooms that look like they were meant for living in (this has possibilities as we are running out of space). We did not explore that area completely as we were looking for a way to get to the computer, and felt it was lower than that level.

There may be even more. We could not check all the walls for little panels, and we found another long stairway leading down. However I did see an indentation that my ring might fit into. I do not know why I did not try it out when we were down there. Except that I had the overwhelming feeling that there was something there that would not be good to share at this time. I think I will look up our resident Holy man and talk with him some time soon.

We found a way to the computer, through another panel door. A very surprised Doriend, the lead technician, greeted us. We also found the computer's back up power plant, the lines between them were down so that it could not be activated. With this shorter way to the computer, Doriend is quite happy. Now instead of 3 xers travel from the Gardens to the computer it will only take one xer. Now we can also set up a com link so that we can communicate without all the hassles of trying to get a signal through and around so much rock.

I will mention to the Council that we could house people in the lower areas, if there are people willing to at least sleep there. Some of our party had to return to the surface because of adverse reactions to being underground and closed in.

42 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

Things are running real smoothly. We have gotten the orientation of newly found people down to a routine that works the best. We are not expecting to find many more. All that is left is the isolated hermit type or the small group on vacation. With the hydroelectric and the nuclear generators we are broadcasting messages over all the airwaves. So that if anyone is still out there they will not feel like they are alone. We will set up spots that we will check every week where people can come to if they want to join us.

What is left is the hard part of getting crops in and harvested. Hardly any of us have ever done anything like it. Most of us did not even have a house plant.

65 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl AWL

Doriend called on the com link this morning so excited that we could barely understand him. Apparently they have gained access into the computer and are dumping all the information onto paper. We felt that this would be the safest way, just so that a safety trap is not accidentally triggered and we loose all the information. Also this way we can purge the entire system and start from scratch. We may not blame technology for our plight but we sure don't trust a machine that was built and programmed to destroy. The more I think about it the more I hate the "military" mind set, even though I was in the military. Hopefully we will never get to the point where that type of thinking is encouraged and bred for.

The council has put all personnel not involved in necessary survival tasks on the job of wading through all the print out. Doriend says that it will take xers just to get it all printed off. None of us read as fast as a printer prints, I fear that this could get boring fast. We are breaking it into sections by category and field, so that the waders are at least reading through stuff that they start to understand. As I was in the military, as aid to General Lazaar, and read most of the military codes, I get the "Top Secret Projects" file. This will take some time but should prove interesting.

67 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

I decoded the titles of the projects first. There is one that sounds, very interesting, its code name is Tharth. Which using standard decoding decodes into Zeltrona, the name of the planet on the other side of the sun from us. I am hoping that it will have information on Zeltrona. Zeltrona is a very strange planet, it is directly opposite from us and in our orbital path. Zeltrop and Zeltrona are like the ends of a baton twirling around the hand/sun, hopefully never to meet.

80 Xer, 3 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

It took awhile but I finally got the Tharth file decoded. It appears that our military started a colony on Zeltrona without the civilians’ knowledge or consent. If my calculations are correct the colony was started about 5 zyrodyls before the Last War. I can only hope that they are self-supporting at this time or that they die quickly.

The council has decided to see if we can get a message to them and let them know what has happened here. From the file we have the coordinates of the relay satellite. Hopefully, in all of this information from the computer there is the frequency and codes needed to establish contact. It was the only thing not in the file and no reference was made to how many people are over there.

1 Xer, 4 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

I find it hard to believe that four zerads have already gone by and that it has only been four zerads. We did so much in such a little time. We are really working together so well, all things considered I am shocked.

A message was sent to the relay satellite yester xer, the earliest we can hear back is 7th of this zerad. That is if they respond immediately without taking any time to think on an answer. We are all uneasy, the more we find out about Project Tharth the less we like it. It has been realized that we here on Zeltrop do not have the ability to send any spacecraft over to Zeltrona to pick up the people there. What if there is too small a gene pool to survive but too many people for spacecrafts and fuel they have. What will we all do? The reading of the military information seems to be going so slow, now that we are looking for something specific.

7 Xer, 4 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

It is late and so far we have not received a reply to our message. I figure that we probably will hear something in three to five xers. There is a person at the radio around the clock, until we can rig up something to automatically record any incoming messages.

10 Xer, 4 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

Here is the message we received from Zeltrona/Tharth:

To: Zeltropian Council

From: Commander of the Tharth Project

We received your message. We do not have enough ships to return all personnel to Zeltrop. We do not have enough fuel here to make more than a one way trip with all our ships. It has been decided that we will all stay here until such a time as we all can return home.

How soon can you start making fuel? We realize that this will not be for a few zyrodyls as you will have to rebuild most of the factories and such. Did any of the fuel dumps and rockets survive?

How are we going to be able to explain to them that we will never start manufacturing that fuel again? It created too much toxic by product. Not to mention the majority of the raw material sites are too radiation saturated to get near for at least 200 zyrodyls. The council is trying to decide how to word our reply.

14 Xer, 4 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

The council has been meeting for extended sessions these last few xers, but no headway has been made on the message to the Tharth personnel. We will have to send something soon. I am in favor of laying it all out for them no matter the energy cost.

16 Xer, 4 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

The message we have decided on is as follows:

To: Commander Tharth Project

From: Zeltropian Council

Stand by to record following message

We regret to inform you that there are no rockets or fuel dumps left, and that the mining fields are radioactive beyond safe working levels. The total of attainable non radioactive fuel is enough to get one ship to you, that is if we had a ship left to send.

It appears that your stay on Zeltrona could be very long, if not permanent. We don't see how we can get space travel in any time under 20 zyrodyls in reality probably closer to 50.

At the end of this transmission are the global stats on how things are now, and the bones of our new government. If you have any ideas on how to get a space program started, please feel free to tell us.

We could possibly send an unmanned craft with imperishable supplies. It would not have a very good chance of getting there as we are short everything needed to build and launch a rocket.

There is also the problem of families being separated. We have a few people who think their loved ones are on Zeltrona. As we do not have a roster of your personnel it is impossible to know. We are for trading lists of survivors, and then deciding what to do.

The rest of the transmission was technical data on the planetary condition and guide lines for our government.

I fear that our message could cause the Tharth Mission to explode. Hopefully they all keep their heads on straight.

30 Xer, 4 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

We have not heard from Tharth at all. I fear the worst, as they did not go through the cleansing fire that we here on Zeltrop did. All we can do now is pray and move getting space travel to a higher priority.

We have though gotten an automatic listener for the radio. It records on a loop tape, and when it detects in coming messages it sounds an alert.

Life goes on as normally as can be expected. I am going to take a couple weeks off and explore the tunnels. The other night Kareena~ said that she would like to get away from everyone just the two of us. She is still easily depressed over the loss of the baby, and the doctors say that she should wait a zerad or two longer before trying for another. She is not the only one impatience with the wait for a child to hold.

I have talked to the Holy man but he knows no more than what was in the book he lent me. Maybe I should invite him along, I will talk it over with Kareena~. I am torn between exploring the caverns and exploring Kareena~'s and my relationship.

45 Xer, 4 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

A message came in from Tharth. Like I suspected, some people did not take our news very well. But the majority were of sounder and calmer minds, the commander suggests that we consider them sending a craft to here and us sending it back. We have fuel for one trip over, and he thinks he knows how to send us some more.

We now have a list of the Tharth personnel, and it was suggested that we trade match-ups. Trying for the largest possible gene pool for both planets. We had been registering survivors already, so we were able to rather quickly run a cross match for relatives. There was only one match up, a woman here has her husband and her brother living there. They are the only survivors of their families. Does she go there, or do they come here, or do we just leave it as is, or do we trade sister for brother.

50 Xer, 4 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

The council decided that since we have the larger gene pool, that Alanora should go to Tharth if she so desires, as well as anyone else who absolutely can not be happy here. In our return message we are suggesting that anyone there who really wants to can come here.

Gene pool wise that is the best we can do. Supply wise if Tharth can get fuel and ships to us, we can trade help back and forth. Already our young electric genius figures he can come up with a solar powered craft that could run between the two planets. His is stopped by the energy needed to launch off of the planet's surface. If we could come up with a fuel that was not so toxic to refine and use, we could run shuttles up to the solar ship. Well as the saying goes, I'll sleep on it.

It is now up to Tharth as to what we do.

60 Xer, 4 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

Kareena~, the Holy man and myself are going to go and explore the tunnels beyond the area we are using. We started using the area beyond the Tunnels of Confusion once we figured out the quick way through. We are also using some of the rooms at the bottom of the stairs that lead down to the computer level.

There is nothing pressing happening at this time. We are planning on being gone two weeks but don't send search parties until three weeks pass. We will leave tomorrow early.

74 Xer, 4 Zerad, 1 Zyrodyl ALW

Kareena~ wanted to try to map the Tunnels of Confusion to try out a mapping theory of hers. It worked, she just recorded the intersections not the distance. It is a viable method, she used it to map the rest of our travels. I will just insert our trip diary here and tomorrow I will catch up with what has been going on up here on the surface.

Xer 1: After testing Kareena~'s mapping strategy we continued on down to the Computer Level. We mapped out all the rooms in the large and small clusters at the bottom of the stairs. Finding that the underground "pool" is really a very large underground lake. With lighting the area could be breathtakingly beautiful. The next trip we will bring a small boat or canoe down and explore the lake and measure it. The water is warmer than we expected, so it is possible that there is a hot spring feeding into this water.

We decided not to follow the tunnel around the left side of the lake but to follow the path on the right to the computer. After the short stairs, we came upon two more groupings of rooms and another long circular stairwell down with a water fall flowing down its core. A very impressive sight. We retraced our steps to a small beach on the "river" from the lake to the waterfall. We will camp here for the night and in the morning continue mapping the Computer Level. If we have time on this trip we will go down the Waterfall Stairs.

Xer 2: We can see an island in the lake from the bridge that crosses the river leading to the waterfall. We mapped another grouping of rooms and crossed over section of lake again. Mapped another section of rooms and came to the passage we postponed exploring to map the route to the computer. This will shorten the way to the computer.

....Back on the main path, as it were, we cross the lake twice more, come out on a ledge above the water with a beach across from us. There is a waterfall near the beach which leads us to believe that we have been on that beach.

Once the Space Station is operational it will be practical to explore the moons. We are lucky in that a space station was planned before the War, and all we had to do was modify the existing plans.

The Thought Crystals are a living crystal that makes a symbiotic relationship with us. The movement of the motes of light show whether the crystal is attuned or not. It is also possible to read the emotional and mental state of a person attuned to a crystal by the movement of the motes. The crystal dies when the person does. There is mention that a dead crystal has strange properties, but that portion of the information is not readable for some reason. It is possible to use the attuned crystals as keys for locks and such, if we still had security clearances this crystal would make the best pass ever.

Once the crystal is attuned, it is painful to remove it from physical contact with the body. The farther the crystal is removed the worse the pain. Apparently the crystal once attuned will die if it is removed from its partner, and the partner feels the crystal's pain of dying. There does not appear to be any side effects other than the previously mentioned problem. So far all the benefits have not been categorized.

With the medical equipment that has been built from the information with the Thought Crystal Garden, it is possible to make quicker and more accurate diagnosis, not only of mental health but physical as well. One tends to heal faster and better if one is attuned to a crystal. Children born to mothers attuned to crystals are healthier. They even seem more mentally quick, but this could be that there is a stronger bond between mother and child.

Now that we are on top of everything and things are going so well, we are starting to relax and enjoy life.

The Space Station is assembled and is being brought on line. It is going real well and is on schedule.

We want to ship to Tharth some of the refined metals, and the equipment and information so they can refine their own.

Medically, we are doing better than well, the information from the Thought Crystal Gardens has been more than we could imagine. Given the pace the medical staff is working, in a zyrodyl or two we will be able to correct most of the minor defects from the radiation and before.

Food wise we are still short, but there have been mentions in the information from the Thought Crystal Gardens of being able to grow food in a vat! If we find out how this is done we will have to have a planet wide meeting to discuss it. Without us preying on the herd beasts they would over populate the planet, most if not all the natural predators were extinct before the War.

1 Sarahabra, 3 ALW

Two zyrodyls we have survived. I never really thought we would make it this far. I am speechless when I think of all we have done. If it had not been for the people stranded on Tharth, I do not think we could have done it. Somehow the thought of giving up and leaving them kept most of us going and striving harder.

The Shuttle is on its way to Tharth with more supplies.

Food is still the most important thing. Shelter is still rather crude and not all that comfortable, but as things get better we start making things more comfortable.

Privacy is a big factor, after living in the caves for almost two zyrodyls with almost 100,000 people we are all looking for a place of our own away from everyone else.

Rael is growing faster than I would like her to, it seems like she was just a baby yester xer and to xer she is a little girl, tomorrow she will be a young lady, the next xer she will be an adult on her own. Maybe it is the father in me not wanting to let go. She will be a zyrodyl old in 2 weeks, she is running around and talking up a storm. She loves us to read to her, she knows some of her favorite stories by heart. Next zyrodyl she will start school.
