Kvass - from extract

Ingredients:

1 cup sugar
1/2 tsp active dry yeast
3 liters warm water (105-110F)
6 tbsp kvass extract

Directions:

Combine all ingredients ina large container and mix well. Loosely cover and place in a warm place. (Same conditions as if proofing bread - approx 75-80F). Leave it alone for 18to 20 hours. Carefully pour off the kvass into another pitcher or large bottle, such as a growler, discarding sediment at the bottom. Refrigerate for remainder of the day.
