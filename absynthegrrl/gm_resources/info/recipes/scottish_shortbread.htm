Scottish Shortbread

from a friend of Sarah Higgins

Ingredients:

1 pound butter, room temperature
1 cup white, granulated sugar
5 cups (sifted measure) flour

Directions:

Cream butter and sugar with mixer, add flour and knead all together by hand. Press into rimmed baking sheet (I used an 11x7), prick dough all over with fork, bake at 350 for 10 minutes, then reduce heat to 300 and bake 40 minutes more. Wait two minutes, then cut into desired shapes. Let cool completely in pan.
