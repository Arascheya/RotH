Cottage Cheese Pancakes

Ingredients:

1 cup cottage cheese
6 large eggs
1/2 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:

Place all ingredients into a blender. Blend or process for at least a minute. Can also use an immersion blender. The batter will be very thin. Cook until set, then turn over and finish.
