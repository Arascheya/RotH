Green chilis and rice

Ingredients:

4 cups cooked rice
1/4 cup butter
1 cup chopped onion - sauted
1 cup sour cream
1 cup cottage cheese
1 bay leaf
2 cans green chilis
1 cup cheddar cheese

Directions:

Mix everything together. Put half rice mix in dish, top with half cheese, repeat.

Bake for 375 degrees until bubbly, 20-25 minutes.
