Russian Tea Cakes

Ingredients:

1 cup butter, softened
1/2cup powdered sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
3/4cup chopped nuts
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:

Cream butter, add sugar and vanilla and whip well. Add flour, salt, nuts and mix until dough holds together. form small balls on cookie sheet and bake in 400 degree ovenuntil bottoms are just brown and cookie is dry on top and holds together. Roll in additional powdered sugar while still hot.
