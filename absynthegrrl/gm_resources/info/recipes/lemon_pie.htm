Mom's Lemon Pie

Ingredients - Filling:

1 1/4 cup water
3/4 cup fresh lemon juice
6 large egg yolks
5 tablespoons cornstarch
2 tablespoons grated lemon peel
dash salt
2 tablespoons butter

Ingredients - Meringue

7 large egg whites
1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
1 2/3 cups powdered sugar

Directions - Filling:

Whisk first seven ingredientss in heavy sauce pan. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until filling thickens and just boils, about 20 minutes. Remove from heat and whisk in butter. Spread into prepared pie crust

Directions - Meringue

Beat egg whites at low spead until foamy. Beat in cream of tartar and one tablespoon sugar. Gradually beat in the rest of the sugar at medium spead until glossy peaks form, about 8 minutes. Spread meringue over filling, covering completely, sealing meringue to edges of crust and mounding in the middle.

Directions - Baking:

Bake pie for 30 minutes at 300 degrees. Reduce temperature to 275 and bake until meringue is golden brown and set when pie is shaken slightly, about 20 minutes longer. Transfer to rack and cool completely.
