Sour Cream Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

1/3 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 cup thick sour cream
2 squares bakers chocolate, melted
1 tsp vanilla
1/8 tsp salt
2 cups flour
1 tsp soda
1 egg

Directions:

Cream butter and sugar. Add the rest of the ingredients and beat for three minutes. Pour into two layer cake pans that have been fitted with wax paper. Bake twenty-five minutes in a moderately slow oven, 350 deg.
