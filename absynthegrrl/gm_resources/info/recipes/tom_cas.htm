Tomato and Bean Casserole

Ingredients:

1 tsp chili powder, or to taste
6 cups drained beans - any type, can be mixed
salt and pepper to taste
minced garlic
2 Tblsp minced fresh savory, marjoram, or oregano leaves, or 20 frsh basil leaves - torn
4 med/lrg tomatos - sliced
2 larg onions - chopped
1 cup gratd cheddar or jack cheese

Directions:

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Stir chili powder and garlic into beans. layer beans, onion, tomatos, etc. in casserole. Finish with cheese. Bake until hot and bubbly. Begin to check after 20 minutes
