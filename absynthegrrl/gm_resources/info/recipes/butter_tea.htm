Butter Tea - Meriksas Style

Ingredients:

5 cups water
1 tablespoon black tea leaves
¼ the teaspoon salt
2 tablespoon butter
½ cup milk

Directions:

Boil the water in a tea pan and then turn down the fire. Add a heaping tablespoon of tea leaves to the boiling water and boil again for a couple of minutes.

Pour your tea, salt, butter, and milk into any big container, cover it with lid and shake it for 2-3 minutes. A blender also works very well and the more you shake or blend, the more it will add to the taste.

Serve the tea right away, since po cha is best when it’s very hot. Recipe serves four people.

Notes:

Vashi finds this recipe inferior to her mother's. Her mom would only use yak butter, smoked black tea, a bit of salt, and a tiny pinch of cardamom. She would double the amount of tea leaves shown in the Meriksas "gourmet" recipe.
