Tapioca Pudding

Ingredients:

1/2 cup tapioca - soaked overnight

1 quart milk

3/4 cup sugar

3 beaten eggs

3/4 cup sugar

3/4 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:

Soak tapioca overnight. Bring milk and 3/4 cup sugar to boil. Add tapioca and cook until tapioca is clear, about 30 minutes. Combine beaten eggs, other 3/4 cup sugar, and vanilla and add to mixture. Bring back to a boil, cool and eat. Makes about 2 quarts pudding.
