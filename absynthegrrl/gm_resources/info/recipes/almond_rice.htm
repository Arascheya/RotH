Rice Crispy Treats - Almond Butter style

Ingredients:

1/2 cup almond butter
1/3 cup honey
4 cupsrice cereal
1 tsp vailla

Directions:

Heat almond butter, vanilla, and honey in a pan until the almond butter is liquid and everything is mixed. Add rice cereal and mix together. Add coconut, nuts,or other adjuncts as desired.Sometime a bit of melted butter also is good.

Press into a buttered glass pand and spread out.

Melt semi-sweet chocolate chips to drizzle or pour over the top. Determine amount by how much chocolate you want on top. Melt in microwave, using 20 second intervals, until chocolate is fluid and pourable.
