Onion Rings

Ingredients:

1 large onion cut into rings
1 1/4 cup all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 egg
1 cup milk, or as needed
3/4 cup dry bread crumbs (optional)
seasoned salt to taste

Directions:

Heat oil in deep fryer to at least 365. Separate onion slices into individual rings. Mix ingrediants together in a shallow bowl. Dredge onion rings in flour, then di in batter. Let drain, roll in bread crumbs (if desired), fry in small batches until golden brown.
