Old Fashioned Sweet Potato Pie

Ingredients:

2 cups cooked, mashed, sweet potatoes
1 cup sugar
1-1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup melted butter
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla OR 2 tablespoons Bourbon
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1 cup milk

Directions:

preheat oven to 350 deg.

Mix 1 cup sugar and all but hte milk together. Once well mixed add milk and incorporate. Bake 40 to 50minutes and cool to room temperature before adding meringue.

For meringue - beat 3 egg whites with remaining 1/4 cup sugar until stiff but not dry. Spread on pie and spinkle with a touch of granulated sugar. Bake 5 to 10 minutes or until golden brown.
