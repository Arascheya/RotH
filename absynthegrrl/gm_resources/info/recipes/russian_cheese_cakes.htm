Sirniki - Russian Cheese Cakes- variant 1

Ingredients:

1/2 lb farmers cheese
1 egg
~1 cup flour+ (I used whole wheat pastry flour with good results but traditionally all-purpose white flour is used)
1-2T butter for frying (my mom often does a 50/50 combo of butter and canola oil as well)

Directions:

Mix cheese, egg and some of the flour. The amount of flour will depend on how “wet” your cheese is. You don’t want your dough to be too dry.

Divide into little cakes

Coverin flour

Melt butter in medium high heat skillet

Fry for about five minutes on each side, until golden brown

Serve with sweetened condensed milk. Another favorite is cherry preserves mixed with sour cream.

See Sirniki for additional details.

Sirniki - Russian Cheese Cakes- variant 2

Ingredients:

cottage cheese 100 gr
one egg yolk
handful of raisins
powdered sugar 10 gr
pinch of salt
vanilla sugar 10 gr
flour 30 gr
vegetable oil for frying

Directions:

Combine cottage cheese with sugar (both powdered and vanilla), egg yolk, prewashed raisins, salt and 20 grams of flour. Roll small balls about 1 cm thick. Dip each one into flour and fry on preheated pan greased with vegetable oil until each side is golden brown.

Portion size is two pieces. Place on a serving plate. Sour cream, raspberry jam or honey are surved separately in a gravy-boat.
