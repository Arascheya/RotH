Inyati Aerospace

Inyati Aerospace is a creator of most air based transportation systems. Recently they have branched out into space platforms and frames.

They have a subsidiary company, Inyati Space Services, which provides almost slave labor for all their projects. Destitute or poverty level people can indenture themselves with Space Services and work toward debt repayment. Space Services often works with CCB in these cases. This arrangement is not beneficial for the workers.
