Mega Corp Ascendency

This is the usage label applied to the time between the late 1890s and late 1990s when the Mega-Corps began to coalesce and form. The time is marked by rapid declines in the powers of traditional governments, including the democratic experiment of the United States. The Mega-Corps began forming their own para-military security forces. By the end of the 1950s these forces rivaled many nation-state military groups.

By the mid 1970s the Mega-Corps had the governance powers over the rest of the populations. Some were benevolent and some were not. Those desiring to live in peace and prosperity soon found the only avenue was to sigh on with on of the Corps.

Arcology living began on the Isle of Wight in the 1890s. This facility was a group of gated housing tracts set up and maintained by ITC for its employees working at the construction and test facilities located on the island. By the late 1980s arcologies were the norm for Corp personnel. They offered increased security and luxurious living situations.
