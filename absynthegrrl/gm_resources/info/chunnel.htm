Channel Tunnel

The channel tunnel is a CIWL project.

Propulsion through tunnel will be by early electric motor. again could be improved using Babbage Engine computations and the same types of improved manufacture processes that allowed the Engines to be built. THe chief engineer can describe electric motors to the point they can be fabricated - can also describe generators which can be driven from steam boilers. This will be enough to power the helper engines for the chunnel If they search news archives, such as they are, they will find previous articles about Wellingham, Babbage Engines, and the Tunnel project.

Tunnel route is from Folkestone, UK to Calaise, France - follows chunnel route but is suspended /resting on sea bottom instead of drilled. Tunnel material and framework is composite materials from Sphere by the Engineer
