SCX-STN 1036.0255/1PRI - 26 MARAHASAL 1699 ALW

Report 2 observations of energy anomaly:

Periodically appearing within the energy sphere are shadowy, moving shapes. One of the shapes moved toward us and stepped out of the energy field. It appeared to be a robed man who looked around and asked, "Can you tell me the way to Caliban?"

The following is a transcript of the ensuing conversation:

Us: "Where?"

Him: "Caliban, the mansion and estate of the grand mage Thazradum."

Us: "I don't know what you are talking about."

Him: "Hmm, I must have the wrong gate. Is this by chance a new one?"

With that he stepped back into the energy field.
