SCX-STN 1036.250/1PRI - 20 MARAHASAL 1699 ALW

Report 1 on energy anomaly:

A large bubble of energy was formed in the main gravitic compression chamber during attempts to increase hull metal density to 2.5 times normal. This bubble formation was accompanied by a loud "pop" and a "wavering" of everything near the compression chamber. Nothing appears to remain of the original hull metal mass. The bubble remained even after full emergency shutdown procedures ran. We will continue monitoring and measurements in an attempt to identify this phenomenon.
