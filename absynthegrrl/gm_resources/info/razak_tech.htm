Razak Technology

Razak battle technology was originally designed by the Drkzyrkth. It was based on their emerging nano-technologies and included the most advanced developments to that point. The primary components of the suite are a set of command and control rings for the command staff and a self-replicating viral style nanotech enhancer package. The enhancers reduce dependence on external medical staff through improved healing, poison and disease resistance. The nanites also create a communication system between anyone "infected".
