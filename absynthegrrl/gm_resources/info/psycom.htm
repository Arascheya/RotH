PsyCom

Developers of military technology, implants, and enhancements for use by nation-state and corporate armed forces. Rumors periodically link PsyCom to strange virii in net-space and in localized outbreaks in real-space. To date none of these have been proven.

Internally PsyCom employees are using equipment that is rarely released to the public.

PsyCom disappears at the time of the last great diaspora, as do many other Mega Corps., victims of the rapid flight from the homeworld.

PsyCom is revived in the time of Valen as PsyberTech Systems. Someone comes across an obscure reference to the name and cyber implants when they are setting up a company to begin that type of research. See PsyberTech Systems as well for info about Valen's time. It is the company that Sarah Draybin's parents work for and the one that gives Valen her implants.

PsyCom is destroyed during the Cyber Wars. Valen's adoptive parents are killed along with everyone else at the facility. Once in a while rumors surface of a secret data backup location elsewhere. So far none of the rumors has pointed to anything real though. Valen keeps hoping, although the hope fades a little more each year.
