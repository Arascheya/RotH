Yamasa Walhedra

YW specializes in bleeding edge military equipment and pharmaceuticals. They are the main provider of military enhancement drugs and implants. Using these technologies they have become the leading Corp in the race back into space. The enhancements they can give their workers offset the cheap labor Inyati Aerospace has at its disposal.

YW has an arcology in Seattle. It is located on the northern section of West Seattle.

YW wants to become the leading colonization and space development group. When the Hut arrives they are locked in the beginnings of a bitter struggle against Inyati Aerospace for the position.
