Sisters of the Web

The Sisters of the Web is a group that worships and supports Ishra. It is headed by the Crone's Council, a council composed of the first nine members of the sisterhood. All of them are Death Womb Seductresses. Shar is the Eldest, followed by Nayya. They are the two who can confer the blessings of the Sisterhood and of Ishra on new members.

The Crone's Council is composed of Nayya, Shareya, Lisette, Tansy, Thalasa, plus four others. Nayya is the most senior member, followed by Shareya.
