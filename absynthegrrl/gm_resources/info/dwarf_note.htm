Dead Dwarf Note - used in Desert of Desolation Scenarios

[written in Lotus Empire tongue]

I have deciphered part of what seems to be a holy book. The portion reads as follows:

"It is our priesthood rite that whatsoever Ashiam accepts she will take from the altar and that which she wishes the priests to have, she leaves on the altar. This profited the priests greatly, except that all offerings left in the East Inner Temple would disappear and never be found again."

"Several priests, upset to see their assets disappearing, searched the East Inner Temple carefully, and they, too, disappeared. The East Inner Temple became a most hallowed spot, so that all who wished to give offerings came to that temple only. The priesthood then became unprofitable and our doctrine had to be changed-tourism turned the trick."

I have found enough information to know that there are three gems that should provide the way into the Tomb of Tesh-Mar. One is a blue-white opal five inches in diameter, one is a star ruby five inches in diameter, and the third is a clear polished gem of pure quartz crystal five inches in diameter. All three are somewhere near the Oasis of the White Palm or in the Haunted valley nearby.

My travels have gained control of me, I find I can no longer stay long in any one spot. I cannot even retain concentration on studies or training for more than a week. It seems I am doomed to wander for...???
