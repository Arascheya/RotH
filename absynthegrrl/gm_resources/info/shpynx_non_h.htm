Granite Sphynx

My brothers and I are guides along the path to knowledge of the Time Before. Many of us are out and about and we point to our brethren in the Caverns of History, beneath the Gardens of Lazaar.

Great are the dangers and strong must be your resolve to complete the path, but greater still are the rewards.

Follow the crawlways to the left as you face the orange entry until you find my Rose Quartz brother. He shall point you on your way.

Seek the tavern of my name, near the Palace of Pleasures. There shall be found keys to the speech of my brother. Three of my brothers reside nearby, two in the light, one in the earth. Two of them speak only our language while one speaks with this tongue. From here on most of the brotherhood speak in our tongue only.

Granite Sphynx beneath Sphynx Inn

There are many ways to find the path of knowledge now. Many of our brotherhood identify these gates. My brothers above guard the University Gate, the Red Moon Gate at the High Temple of the Moons is guarded by other granite brothers, the Room of Children Gate is watched by rainbow marble, and the long Blue Moon Path can be started here.

I hunger for the Green Serpent. My brothers above hunger for the Sapphire Eye.
