Miskaton Update - post to campaign wiki

Since Miskaton is basically a grouping of islands in the center of the convergence of two rivers, there needed to be access to the land routes near it. To solve this, the small satellite towns of West Port, East Port, and North Port were formed. These each sit on one of the river banks and have direct freight and ferry service to major sections of Miskaton.

North Port was set in the lowlands north of the city, and for several generations, it flourished. Just after the Hell Gate Wars there were severe floods which raised the permanent level of the river enough to leave the North Port areas flooded. It also triggered another round of rebuilding in Miskaton itself. North Port was abandoned in favor of Arisalia just up the Expr River.
