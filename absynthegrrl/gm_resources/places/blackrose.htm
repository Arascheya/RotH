Black Rose Chocolatier

The Black Rose is run by a Tiefling named Irisa. She sells chocolates, pastries, and black roses. The roses are imported from the dark forests of the Discord. The chocolate has been rated the finest in Yew Nork. The shop includes a small area of tables in the front so the patrons may consume their confections on-site. In the back is the production area. Many ingredients come from Arakov Market and the Consortium of Trade in the Mirror Souk.

The Black Rose replaces Babbo in the building next to Ezra's Magic Shop.
