Museum of Natural History, Yew Nork City

This is supplementary information for the Evacuees world version of the American Museum of Natural history, located just off Central Park. The wikipedia page is close enough except for any exceptions listed below.

History

Additions to history section:

Excavation for the foundations of the original building cracked into the tunnel system surrounding a Hell Gate. This resulted in the death of several workmen before the tunnels were sealed with the foundation structure. There was a secret agreement between the primary contractor and the first museum director to embed an access method into the foundation. This information was lost until recently when a low level researcher found it while preparing for the museum birthday bash.
