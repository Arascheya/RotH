Far Star Bar

The Far Star is a hole in the wall place located down near the independent section of the docks. It caters to the independent starship crews and station support personnel. It is not a high class place at all sso if you are looking for a nice quiet place to relax with a drink, this is not it.

It is run by a fire tempered redhead named Lise. She serves four grades of beer, several varieties of hard stuff, and a few good hard liquors that she personally likes. There are no mixers and no fancy drinks at this place. The music tend to be loud and danceable, selected from a battered music station in the corner.

Rules for employees:

Don’t date the customers

Don’t bring your dates in - must appear available at all times but never be available.

Keep it within legal - can be to the bleeding edge, but never over the line.
