Miskatonic University

Miskatonic University is the School of Majik, located in Miskaton, in the Valley of Exper. It is a multi-verse renowned school, attended by the cream of the arcane crop from everywhere that majik is practiced.

There is a World Gate link in the basement of the headmistress house to allow students from other realms to attend the school. It is a general purpose Gate, meaning, each student arriving through the Gate is majikally and technologically identified and tagged so the Gate will return them only to their point of origin, not to any other realm.

Data packets are available on any world that acknowledges access to Centrys, and on some that do not through more shady means.

Miskatonic University

Miskatonic University is a multi-versal renowned institution for the learning of higher majik. All exceptional students of the Arcane Arts are welcome to apply. We have a thorough but fair screening process that guarantees the best and brightest in all our classes and seminars.

Located in the scenic Expr Valley, nestled in the heart of the thriving metropolis of Miskaton, the student is never far from cultural and social opportunities. All serious students are invited to talk to our placement and recruiting councilors. Our primary offices are located on the Street of Dark Blades, Mistress Ward, Centrys. Please bring two personal references and be prepared to demonstrate majikal and intellectual prowess at the time of application.

We look forward to your application.
