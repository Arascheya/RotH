Miskaton

Miskaton - town where a major Arcane school is located. It is a Venice like city similar to Glantrii/Merovingen.

Thi city is ruled by mayor who is selected by the Merchanters households in an election based process by the heads of the families.

City police and investigators are actually fair and fairly adept. Merchanters maintain their own small armies in the guize of household guards, They are often used as covert action groups. If they are caught they are NOT protected.

Law enforcement is a bit lax at the lower social levels, there is a limited amount of corruption, usually in little pockets. Coruption is punished harshly when it is found.
