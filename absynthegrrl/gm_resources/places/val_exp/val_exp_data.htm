Ancient’s Way

Ancient’s Way - a segment of the tube train system that is now used to trade from the high valley to the Lotus Empire, High Valley, and the Isle of Research. It has entry points near the hidden valley side of the valley and near the west side. it is depressurized and open at several points, it is kept minimally clean and way stations have been carved into it in places where the old escape hatches were placed, it is somewhat maintained by the caravans that use it and the way station operators that depend on it for livelihood.

One of the access ways leads to the high mountain where the frozen monastery is located. It is here that the hut people will enter, unless they get the hut figured out. If they activate the gate controller inside the hut they will transport themselves and it to the Z equivalent location, plus getting all kinds of warnings about dangerous feedback and resonances from the interaction of the two fields. It will cause them massive amounts of pain since it will really mess with the hut control systems.
