Adam and Eve - a Public House and Inn

The Adam and Eve serves one of the best tables of food in Death City. It is also a favorite place among the Temple Heath Yards staff and the Central Station people.

It is located on the lands once belonging to the Cathedral of All Faiths that stands east of the river. It is just up from the river walk which leads to the rail bridge and Central Station.
