Death's Head Tattoo

Jasmine Franciss runs this tattoo and piercing shop. It is located in Goth City, on the main floor of the building where Dans Macabre is in the basement and Rainy's apartment is on one of the upper floors. Jasmine is a skilled piercer. She has three tattoo artists working at the shop with her, Darren, Doug, and Marianne. Doug is also a capable piercer. They come highly recommended by the Cathouse and Dans communities.
