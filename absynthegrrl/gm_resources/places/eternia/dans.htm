Dans Macabre

Goth club in the Goth City. It is located in the basement of the building Rainy lives in. It is a bit smaller than the Devil's Cathouse, however it has adequate stage space for guest bands. Love's Bitch will appear here once in a while in its second incarnation.

Dans is mostly a dance floor, some seating, and a bar. It looks similar to the Inferno, something Ennui will notice right away.

Dans is across the street from the City Cathedral, a juxtaposition that was not without planning.
