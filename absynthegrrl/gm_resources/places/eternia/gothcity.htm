Goth City

Goth City is the temporary identifier for the large gothic city between Death City and Portal Station. This city houses Dans Macabre, Eternal Metal Works and various other locations. Goth City is a large tangle of tall buildings, winding streets, and dark, dim areas. Think something like a cross between New York City and Gotham City. The architectural styles surge and collide against one another in random patterns. There is a large park in the center of the city. It is surrounded by towering buildings and narrow streets.

Travel is by underground rail, walking, bicycles, and rail feeds from the rest of Eternia.

There is a cathedral, City Cathedral, near the park. It is dwarfed by the buildings around it. Rainy's apartment has a spectacular view of the cathedral.

Eternal Metal Works sits outside the city central core, in an industrialized area nearby. Ron commutes to work by bicycle from his home in the fringes of the city. Rainy commutes from her downtown apartment by the underground.
