Lotus Empire Geography

Much of the Lotus Empire is desert of one form or another. Some areas get a little rain, others get none. In general, the land is harsh, forbidding, and difficult. Survival requires a great deal of effort outside the major cities or the areas along major waterways.

The harshest zone if the Galhai Wastes, an area to the north of the capital city of Jakara. The Galhai Wastes encompass various local areas, including the Kharatoum Habaya.
