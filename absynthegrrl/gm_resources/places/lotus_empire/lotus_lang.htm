Lotus Empire - Language Basics

Lotus Empire terms

Mish = seed

Tahal = carrier

Ilat = bonded

Ilat t’han = bondsperson

Ilat t’hani = bondspersons male

Ilat t’hana = bondspersons female

Ilat mish tahal = bonded seed carrier = used to indicate male(s) bonded to a woman

Desert Terms

Habaya - a desolate stretch of desert where little to no water falls. Habayas usually suffer from high winds, dangerous sand and dust storms and are plagued by Habyanisha, desert skimmers.

Erg - large area of sand and dunes - wikipedia info

Hamada - largely barren rocky plateaus - wikipedia info
