Dark Forces Small Temple - Outer Zone

Temple Image

These are found on the periphery of the first Dark Forces uprising areas. Mostly this is in the areas around the current Lotus Empire.

The inner section rises above all the other areas of the temple. The main building consists of several towers, the highest of which is 100' tall. These towers are ornately carved on the outside. Statues of leering, grotesque creatures sit at the corners of the towers. Window frames are carved like open mouths. The fountains spout water from the mouths of twisted beasts. Each tower is topped by a waving, bright-colored pennant.
