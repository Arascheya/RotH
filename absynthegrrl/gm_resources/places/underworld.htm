The Underworld

The Underworld is located in the basement of the One Front Street building. The entrance is shon as the stairs to the right of the front entrance. The first floor hosts an art gallery and the second floor is a dance studio and performance space.

The Underworld caters to the Goth crowd, with a heavy, dark, hell-scape feel. The drinks are strong, the music loud, and the patrons friendly.

The building is owned by the Cassaveras Group. There is a tunnel entrance in the back of the bar.
