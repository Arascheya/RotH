Fire Beetle stuffed Ice Worm

We use only the youngest and most tender Ice Worms and the fiercest Fire Beetles to prepare this delightful dish. It is a delightful blending of hot and cold, delicate and spicy, with a hint of the sublime. Served in a rich sauce of Blackheart Brandy, roasted garlic, and exotic spices, this is a treat for even the most jaded of palates.

Consider ordering a complete carcass for your party of twenty or more. Smaller portions are available, however, the presentation of the dish suffers from the plating process.
