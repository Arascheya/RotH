Fire Beetle stuffed Ice Worm

Both better and worse than it sounds. The ice worm flesh tends to resemble calamari in texture, but it has a mix of almost lobster and pheasant flavors. The fire beetle flesh is dark, spicy, and sharp, kind of a mix of vinegar and flavorful habanero peppers. The sauce is blackheart brandy, roasted garlic, fire beetle extract, habanero pepers, and other spices.
