Tharth and Zeltrop

These were engineered worlds, created by the Creation Team in a dynamically stabilized pattern of one world on each side of the sun, positioned in the same orbital path, and held there by a dynamic control system embedded in each planet. Power is derived from planetary core heat and this is replenished by a solar energy to core heat exchange process. The control system and tapping system can be accessed through the Gigabolt mountains.

This was one of the last projects of the Creation Team before they disappeared from the galactic environs. As a result some unexpected or unresolved issues remained. One was a viral form that would slowly destroy higher brain functions in any organism on the worlds. The original experimental life-forms established by the CT died out from this issue. It also nearly destroyed the Karanal survivors after they arrived through the World Gate.
