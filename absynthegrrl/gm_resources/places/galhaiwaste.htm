Galhai Waste

The Galhai Waste was formed by the battle between Tesch Mar and Nyigroth. The Hand recovered the Hand and Eye of Etesa after the battle and began his program to release the demons and Etesa from imprisonment after this acquisition. The Hand lives in the Waste and is the Undying One refered to in the Akhalt legends. Integrate some of the Salt Sea and Al Qadim ruins into this area. Need to plant the Akhalt legend somewhere where the players can come across it.

The Galhai Wastes replace the Great Anvil in the Lotus Empire maps.
