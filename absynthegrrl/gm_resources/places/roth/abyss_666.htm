Level 666 of the Abyss - Succubi

This realm contains the succubi. It is an old Level, with a rich history of rise, fall, and rise again. It is a place of desert, oceans, and towering cities. Some lie in ruins, some are fully functioning, and some are remants of old glories.

The realm was ruled by Iskandera, until a coup forced her to flee. This coup brought on a period of decline as the members of the coup fought among themselves once Iskandera was defeated.

Arascheya ni Iskandera's two daughters took back the realm after the creation of the Asylum Tactical Teams in Sphere. This was the first instance of the Asylum Tactical Teams being used for non-Asylum reasons.
