Cleopatra VII

Late 69 BC to 12 Aug 30 BC - see wikipedia for more data

Last Pharaoh of Egypt.

Cleopatra had the misfortune to land in the middle of Roman politics. She was also the richest woman in the ancient world and a powerful woman in a time when the emerging civilizations were anti-woman.

Ange surved as regent during Cleopatra's travels, both to Rome and to Tarsus. At those times she was Pharaoh in all but name. Ange spent long intervals in iluminating poison trances trying to find a way out of the coming decline. In the end, all possibilities cost countless Eqyptian lives and led to worse outcomes. The one that played out was the best of a bad lot.

Besides fnuctioning as regent, Ange was also a close confidant, friend, and executor of shadow tasks.
