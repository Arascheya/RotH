Natasha

Natasha died from radiation exposure on a world where several large scale disasters had prompted rescues from the effected areas. She was a scientist at the facility when it was destroyed by terrorist forces.

Kali is sent to pick her up after an Extraction Army mission. Can introduce some of the army through Natasha and her interactions after pick-up.

Natasha will choose to live in the large Victorian style city instead of Death City itself.
