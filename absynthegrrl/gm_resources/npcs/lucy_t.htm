Alucinia (Lucy) Tomarasdan

Alucinia gives the impression of having always lived in Stratmore Upon Comcrash, however her past is far more than she typically lets on. As a girl she followed Irena Katarya, and her friends, after they came through Stratmore. Irena had suffered a cyberware crash and was in the village for almost two years before she recovered enough to leave. Lucy and Darina both followed along, losing track of Irena when they arrived at Gana in the Lotus Empire.

She didn't see Irena again until the final Hell Gate Wars battle. By that time Alucinia was commander of the personal forces of the Empress of the Lotus Empire. After the Hell Gate Wars she renounced her command and took to living on the streets, hoping to drink and debauch her memories away. She was saved by Ahkmed from death in a back alley and nursed back to health and sanity by Darina and him.

Irena

Irena came to the village in a damaged state, capable of very simple, repetitive actions, but not much more. She was taken in by Marissa, Darina's mother, where she was treated as another daughter. The second spring after her arrival she was nearby when crazy old Serena came into town to trade with a small caravan. Serena was examining a few old wands when Irena picked one up before anyone thought to stop her. It discharged an electrical trap of some form and knocked Irena down. Once she woke up she began to rapidly heal, progressing through the Tai Chi moves that always were the first sign of cyber brain rebuild and moving on to be fully back by late summer. Once she was back restlessness forced her to begin moving.
