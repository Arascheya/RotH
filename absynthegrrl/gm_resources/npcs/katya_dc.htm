Katya

Appears in Death City stories after the current breaking of the Truce is resolved. Petrov and his team find her on a recovery world under wreckage, appearing less injured than her position and clothing would indicate. She becomes a bartender at the Devil's Cathouse, usually working the upper bar. She is a very good dancer, although she rarely dances with others around. She is quiet, shy, and does not remember anything before being rescued by Petrov.

Katya is eastern european looking and waifish, with an eye of horus tattoo over her left eye and 3 blood red tears below her right one.

consider - Petrov sees enough similarity in something that he mistakes Katya for Kali at first. He will stand by her in many situations because of this

consider - Katya, Merris, and Petrov hooking up in a weird triangle relationship of some form. Katya has the same energy signature and way of moving as Kali but without the baggage of 35 years in the realms.
