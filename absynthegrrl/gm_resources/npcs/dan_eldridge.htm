DannyEldridge

Danny Eldridge was a field medic for the armed forces in his home world. He was recovered after dying in a botched covert op. Kali recruited him shortly after his arrival for her team.

Danny always wanted to be able to save everyone, to do more, to always win against death. In Death City he has his skills and magic to assist him. His success rate is quite a bit higher as a result.

Danny becomes the chief medic once the large team is assembled. Until then he is the primary with Jeff helping out from time to time. Particularly if it looks to be a very dangerous run.
