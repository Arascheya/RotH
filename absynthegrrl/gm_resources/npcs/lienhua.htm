Lien Hua Petrie

Lien Hua is of Chinese descent. Her family are herbalists and merchants, residing in the Limehouse disctrict. She is an accomplished herbalist and botanist, able to create and identify many odd and powerful concoctions. She has taught some of these to her husband for use at the London Hospital.

Lien Hua is an extremely self-possessed woman. She carries herself as if she were a princess or woman of station, even in a cluture that does not acknowledge this.

Lien Hua is married to John Petrie. She is a key player in a health initiative for women and children in the Chinese community, particularly the residents of the Limehouse area. Lien Hua is a source of information for Lestrade when the yard gets called in to Limehouse events. They live near the Wapping Stairs and the Prospect of Whitby pub.

Information on her name:
Meaning: Lotus flower
Origin: Chinese
Gender: Girl
Pronunciation:(lee en hwah)
Form of: Itself (Lien-hua)
