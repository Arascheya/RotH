Emily Susan Caulfield

Alt. Earth data

Born 1834 to Sarah Elaine and St. George Francis Caulfield, Emily was a semi-wild child for a member of the upper classes. She had an illegitimate daughter, Emma Marie, who was spirited away by a spinster aunt before Emily maried.

Original Earth data - starting point

Emily Susan Caulfield is the daughter of St. George Francis Caulfield.1 She married Henry Lowther, 3rd Earl of Lonsdale, son of Henry Cecil Lowther and Lucy Eleanor Sherard, on 31 July 1852. She died on 15 July 1917. From 31 July 1852, her married name became Lowther.
