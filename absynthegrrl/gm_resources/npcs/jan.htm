Jan Swordbreaker

Personality: cool, aloof with human males. She is loyal to her sword companions.

She is an able fighter.

Suffers from claustrophobia, but has it mostly under control

Her preferred fighting style is Florentine

She was raped when younger, a friend of hers was raped and killed at the same time. It happened near the borders of the amazon realms and the allied kingdom.

She is involved in hunting for Shandi, Sister Quest, Dan’s rescue - he is rescued from the Obsidian Citadel, ends up on the Ghost Ship with Milo - becoming an associate of Belit.

She comes from a tribe of amazons, allied to a minor kingdom in mutual protection and trade. The pact between the two groups was generations old. Two magical swords were forged at the time of the signing of the pact, sealing the pledges. One sword for the ruler of the kingdom, the other for the queen of the amazons. If ever the oaths between the two groups were broken, the swords would break also.

Jan is the daughter of the present queen. During an attack by a large invasion force, the queen, Jan’s grandmother, was struck down. Jan picked up her sword and stood over her grandmother’s body. The final blows of the battle were between Jan and the leader of the invaders. As Jan went to strike the killing blow her sword shattered. Jan’s mother finished the battle.

Jan was exiled before it was discovered that the regent for the kingdom had hired the invaders to conquer the amazon nation by proxy.

She started life on one of Arther's worlds and was transported to Karanal when the party was attacked by a band of brigands. Her best friend, a centaur named Rapscallion, was killed in the attack.
