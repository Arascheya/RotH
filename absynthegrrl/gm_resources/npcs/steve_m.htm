Steve Marwatt

Steve was Mary and Ara's lover and the father of Mary's children. He has been a constant companion in their lives since they were all in their early teens. They have provided a mostly unified front against the religious and backward culture of their home world.

Steve married Mary after finishing school and they inherited a farmstead from Mary's folks. Ara moved in with them as soon as the farm was set up and has lived with them ever since. They have made their poly amorous, three way life style work and have been happy for most of their time together.
