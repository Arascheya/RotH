Sharyl - Stormy

Personality: kindly, grandmotherly over hull metal core. She does not suffer fools lightly. She is also known as Stormy because of the short temper she had when she was younger.

Sharyl is the logical one in the pair that is her and Kayara. She is the one to point out flaws or unexpected outcomes in Kayara's schemes. Kayara balances out Sharyl's tendency to get stuck in the details and lost in the logic. They work in a non-adversarial relationship, each complimenting the other.

Abilities: use all arcane spells, head of planet wide training system, in on all tests with Kayara and Elentaryn

Ideas: life with gypsies, life at tavern, life as arcane trainee, and mistress of school

Notes:

Sharyl now runs the Arcane school in the Valley of Experiment. She tends to hang out at the Failed Mage Tavern where she tends bar. She is also the owner and manager of the facility. She will still be seen periodically at Stormy’s however Kayara has mostly taken over that facility now. She tends to get bored with the bureaucracy of the School and retreats to running the bar at the Failed Mage whenever she can get away.

She and Kayara have become lovers over the last years as Elentaryn has become more withdrawn. She has responded to her incredible life-span with optimism and a sense of curiosity, similar to Kayara. This has been what has kept her from falling into the isolation and slow despair that is eating at Elentaryn.

Shandi is their child.
