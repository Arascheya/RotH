Yasmine Raisul

The picture was taken on her 18th birthday at the Yamasa Walhedra Archology, Seattle

She was born in Tangier Morrocco and was moved to Chiba City when her mother was recruited by Yamasa Walhedra. Yasmine was ten at the time. Her uncle moved to London when she moved to Chiba City.

They were relocated to the Special Projects Arcology when Yasmine was seventeen.

Yasmine is multi-lingual from these various moves, speaking fluent Arabic, Tarifit (a Berber dialect), Japanese, and English.

Yasmine finds archology life to be boring and common place. She has begun slipping out and hanging at the Coffee Messiah and her uncle's restaurant next to it.
