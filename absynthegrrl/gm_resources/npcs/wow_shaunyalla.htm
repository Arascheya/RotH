Shaunyalla - Undead - Warlock - Horde

Starts in Tirisfal Glades

She was the identical twin of Irislaynya. They retain most of their memories from before and are still lovers, as they were in life. The few who might remember them would refer to the as the incest twins. Teschyan befriends her while in the Undercity. Before the plague, they were twin sisters living in the area around Brill. They were a bit twisted even then, prefering each other as lovers and eschewing contact with the boys of Brill and the outlying farms. They had planned to go to Lordaeron before the plague ravaged the land and sent them into mindless servitude. Now that they are free, they are once again together. Magic and sex are the only two things that make them feel alive.
