Robert Travers - Watcher's Council

age: 28

occupation: Watcher

personality: a bit adventurous, a bit of a loner. believes the council and the slayer should work in more of a partnership rather than an authority/worker way. finds Van Helsing to be an insufferable bore.

Imagine a character mid way between Giles and an English butler when playing this character.

info:

Drusilla - sired and rendered insane by Angelus about 80 years ago. about 70 years ago she killed Angelus - a serious infraction in vampyr social structures.

Angelus - particularly nasty and cruel vampyr sired in Ireland about 120 years ago

Van Helsing - senior Watcher and the Watcher of Princess Maria, the currently acting slayer. Tends toward arrogance and hubris. Believes himself to be one of the final authorities on just about everything. He supports the group of watchers that feel the slayer should be subservient to the council of watchers. Absolutely incensed that a woman, Lady Valen, dared to tell him off and about medical things on which he fancies himself an expert.
