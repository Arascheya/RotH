Sweeny Todd (Benjamin Barker)

Barber on Fleet St., shop is called Sweeny Todd's Tonsorial Emporium. His shop and apartments are above Mrs. Lovett's on Fleet Street. He is a sour, bitter man, angry from mistreatment by a judge who lusted after his wife. Todd was sent into exile when he was still Benjamin Barker and his wife was raped and killed. His daughter is a prisoner of the judge, however, he has not yet managed to free her.

At the time of the Hut in London, he has not yet begun his murderous activities. In this time line he might not. If her were to become friends with Angelique he could have her solve the problem without resorting to violence himself.
