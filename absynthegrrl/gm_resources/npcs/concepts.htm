River-like

/* a River kind of day */

dancing and swirling around in my head, more real than real. unbidden they swim to the surface at the worst of times. when i should be doing something else. concentrating on other things. more alive than life when i should be focused, thin shadows when i have time for them. perverse that way. why haunt me when i'm busy instead of still?

am i ever really still though? head bouncing around, shadows, darkness, light, faces...

sane but not - behind the eyes weird blurs

shadows and fire and ash and wind - what was it? what is it? survived, but barely - sane but not...

heavy sounds, need driving heavy sounds to pull the focus out...

scurrying, scurrying here and there, set on meaningless tasks to fill the emptiness of time

craziness spawns frenetic action

craziness spawns inactive puzzlement

what does it mean? in the end? nothing?...fills space/time

brain fuzzed, numbness and bewilderment, stare at the screen and see symbols

lives so full they are empty - not empty with fullness

crazy other-space, light-headed muzziness, fuzzy clarity, clear murkiness

fugue state disconnected connects - mind/body interupts

child/woman looks through hazed eyes - come to see the unreal real or the real unreal she's never sure

close/distant for so long...

never trust the connects - fragile, burns

connect/disconnect/connect in waves brain slides sideways on reality and time, then rights itself in a jerk, then tilts again, a helicopter in heavy winds

coherence - so many need cohereance, an anchor in the storms, i like coherence it is my friend, mostly, until it goes out at night and finds its way back drunk and disorderly...then it is a rope and a noose and a stone...and not my friend at all as it rails against the chaos that should be a friend too..

life support - need to remember life support, think, breathe, think, focus?

should be able to step through the walls into otherwhere/otherwhen but it doesn't work right

why doesn't it work? is real real that solid that other real is lost?

/* focused River kind of day */

seeking understanding of the real real and the other real walking the line of them

real real is hard and tedious - wall mart, jobs, school, mortgages, and expectations, and flourescent lights

other real is just as real - it is the realms of Valen and Shandi, Death City and the Empire, the Hut and the game worlds, candle light and forest sunshine

both are as real as the other

i am confused by those who live only in real real

somewhere in the past the real real was more real than other real but not any more

the two blend best where there is grass and green and growing things

real real is far more intrusive than other real

the balance point is the shaky bit

not enough other real - depression

not enough real real - endangered survival

then the balance with those who think only the real real is real - that is hardest of all

hardest of all is to trust my definition of balance over another's

the contradictions in another - grounded only in the real real even though they spin around their own center of oddness - how can that be?

if there is a center to spin around how do they not see the other real?

to know music without the other real is just strange...

to enjoy movie, video, and written distractions so much and still miss the other real is strange

maybe the link was broken in childhood instead of forged...

so confusing really - whatever 'broke' in my head was 'fixed' in others

i don't think they understand...but then i don't understand either...


i like my demons, mostly

sometimes they make me crazy and not fun,

but i still like them

i could numb them,

get drugs to put them to sleep,

but it seems too costly

better to burn and choke on them,

maybe even do myself harm than to deny they exist and pretend to like normal


sometimes the balance breaks and i go mad,

better madness than normal,

better anything than normal...

anything to feel, to have existance beyond expectation and boring repetion


why live only in the real real?

so many live only in the real real

without the other real would there be art?

would there be music or creativity or even hope?


isn't that ugly bane of existance religion an off-shoot of the desire for the other real?

Veteran - psychic wars

someone who has survived a massive psychic war but shows extreme internal damage, mostly in the form of large personality pieces missing. A scan would reveal many sections of the brain shut down. A psychic probe would show images of burnt out equipment or buildings, or whatever they would relate massive psychic damage to. The veteran functions, just not really well. The veteran’s sleep is plagued with nightmares and this causes anyone observing to notice massive shakes and tremors passing through the veteran at these times.
