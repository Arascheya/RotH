Lisette (Lis)

Lisette appears hard and cold from a distance, but is warm, friendly, and accessible once you get to know her. She is fiercely loyal to her friends and a deadly enemy. Her cold side allows her to be an excellent Thieves Guild leader and her warmth makes her popular as a tavern keeper. Members of the guild often hold legitimate positions in the Cavern of Dark Delights, however, not every employee is a Thieves Guild member. Those in the know have recognition signs for getting word to the guild when needed.

Lisette arrived with Nayya when she fled the forces of the East Oceanic Trading Company attempting to kill her. She is one of Nayya's oldest friends. She has become the head of the Thieves Guild in Jakara, the capital city of the Lotus Empire.She is also the owner and manager of the Cavern of Dark Delights.

Nayya and Shareya rescued Lisette soon after they formed their alliance. The core decision group has remained Nayya, Shareya, and Liksette ever since.
