Irishkah

Irishkah is the Tiefling proprietess of The Asylum and a descendent of Arascheya Iskandera. Arascheya and her lovers, Mary and Steve, founded The Asylum long in the past of Sphere.

Irishkah is a steady and kind boss and an able administrator. She has brought The Asylum back to prominence and prosperity after several generations of less capable management. She is always available to talk with clients, inmates, staff and Flowers as needed.

Irishkah is not afraid to use her special operatives to promote and protect Asylum interests and personnel. If another party comes and asks for assistance, she will detail operatives to their cause if it is in the intests of The Asylum. The cost of loaned operatives is extremely high and operatives are always instructed to walk from the third party if they are asked to work against Asylum interests. There has been one case where other operatives were sent in to extract loaners from a third party mess. The highly visible retribution has discouraged any additional instances of abuse of Asylum operatives.
