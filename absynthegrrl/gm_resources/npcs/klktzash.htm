Klktzash - Zash - Type V Demon

Zash is the Hut librarian. She was summoned by Margla early in the Hut's history and has adapted to the freedoms and opportunities presented. When Margla set up her release Zash refused to leave the Hut with the other creatures. Prefering instead to remain and take her chances with the new owners.

She is a consumate librarian now, knowing where to find many of the more obscure items and information sources in the vast library of the Hut. Research chance of 75% for common knowledge kinds of questions and 50% for more obscure references. She would be able to quickly adapt to a computerized system if it were installed as an additional library resource.

She is friendly and easy going unless her books are threatened. She has established a policy where any book in the library may not be removed from the Hut. In concert with Margla she devised programs to automatically detect and return to the library any book entering the Interface Chamber
