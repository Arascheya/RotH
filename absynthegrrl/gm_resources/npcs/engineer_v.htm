Engineer

The engineer is a tech savy being from one of the Realms. It was hanging in Centrys by accident after it opened a Door by mistake. It looks vaguely Shadow-like and is a brilliant engineer. It was in trouble with the Watch and looking for a way out when Lord Wellingham's group appeared. It agreed to echange tech for escape and has remained in London ever since.

It is beginning to become restless and longs to return to its own place and time.
