Nadjah Polina

Nadjah is the lead forensics tech on the Ghost Precinct team. She is friendly, warm, and open. No one can figure out what got her posted to the Ghost Precinct.

Truth is she wanted it and asked for it, pressuring everyone until they authorized the transfer. She is very happy investigating the weird and unexplained.

Nadjah studied ballet as a child. She injured herself when she was sixteen and could no longer dance. She envies Shan her dance, untl she finds out the cost of being the strange.
