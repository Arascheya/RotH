Henry Prevost Babbage

Henry was born 16 September 1824 and is one of the sons of Charles Babbage. Henry was the only child of Charles to take an active interest in his father's tinkerings. After his father's death he assembled various of his father's designs with the help of Anne Wellingham. This brought him to the attention of Willem, leading to his position as the chief hardware design enginneer for ICM.

Henry served in India before leaving military service to pursue a career in engineering with ITC.
