Ibrahim bin Raisul - Sultan of Kharatoum

Sultan of Kharatoum at the time of the battle between Nyigroth and Tesch Mar. He was killed in the battle when Kharatoum was destroyed. His twin daughters were captured by Nyigroth as an attempt to locate the Moon Stones. The capture did not work.

Ibrahim was a decent man and tried to keep Kharatoum a good place for his populace.
