Details:

She is the daughter of Kayara and Sharyl through genetic manipulation by Sharyl. Kayara and Sharyl have allowed Elentaryn to believe he is her father to bolster his ego. Shandi does not yet know, although she had some suspicions before she ran. Kayara will tell her when she returns with the Hut, assuming that they do.

The Family was charged with her education and they tutored her along with Aalynn - teaching her all the stuff they had found during their adventures. They were not allowed to send her on protected missions like Aalynn went on though. She learned dance from her mother Kayara and some tech stuff from Elentaryn, although she wasn’t really drawn to it.

When she reached 18 she ran. Hoping to find adventure she hopped a tube train and headed in a random direction. She ended up in Aztol's garden in Coslembra.

Aztol implanted something and had a demon bound to it. He then used his brain scanner to load fear and distrust of anything requiring technology or magic. He also placed hard blocks against her abilities to think coherently. He blended her genetic structure with that of several demons and the creatures from Level Seven to make her a hybrid. It was his intent that this would give her the strength, speed, instincts, and reactions necessary to keep her alive. He wanted her father to have to deal with a situation he most likely could not fix without killing his own daughter. He implanted a small messaging device that was set to emerge and display a holograph of himself delivering a message to her father about Shandi.

Ishra managed to slide enough of her conciousness into Shandi during the brainscan to act as a goad. This keeps reminding Shandi that Ishra is bound and needs to be freed.

All the tampering has left her in a state of almost stupor unless she is receiving doses of stimulants or in a situation of intense danger. She has a physical craving for either of the above. She is only able to really sleep after a dose or two of some of the stronger opium derivatives. Her favorite is laudanum like tinctures or a small pipe full of opium. Turkish coffee is about the weakest stimulant that will work for her. She will consume massive quantities of this during the average day. It is extremely rare to find her without a mug of the stuff at hand. If she really needs to be awake she will chew coca leaves or sometimes inject raw adrenaline. She doesn’t do this very often because of the availability of it. If she goes more than a few weeks without stimulants her body starts to consume itself. This is another legacy of Aztol.

If attacked or in battle she tends to be very Callisto like, with a happy grin and pure madness present in her eyes. She tends to say things like, "You hurt me, why did you hurt me?" or, "How dare you try and hurt me..." as she is rapidly dealing with the attack. Several of the groups she’s been with don’t know which is worse, watching her take apart her opponents, or the absolute joy she seems to take in it. When the battle is over they have to deal with a very morose, depressed person who slides rapidly back into a kind of stupor. Most have found the best way to help her is to let her slip back into it and stay there for at least an hour or two before attempting to awaken her. Unlike Callisto she doesn’t have the same sort of vicious cruelty or revenge motivation.

Her speech patterns tend to be broken and jerky, as if it is somewhat difficult to string words together into a coherent whole. When she tries to formulate thought strings there are still residuals that work to scatter her thoughts around into incoherence. When Kara and Valen teach her signing she is able to communicate more clearly. It is less broken and jerky, although complex thoughts are still difficult. The signing is a mix of Eritsani and Imperial common forms from the times of Valen.

Aztol enlisted the aid of Kaltereth who was in his employ to set up the magical components of his effects on her mind. Kaltereth slipped Shandi out to Vilyanu just before she escaped herself. She wanted to place Shandi somewhere where Aztol would not be likely to find her. Kaltereth escaped from him shortly after he cemented his hold over Ishra and the spider ship.

In Vilyanu Shandi was befriended by a group of adventurers new to the city. During their adventures they attempted to remove her curse. They were partially successful, unlocking her ability to see majik patterns and a little of her ability to communicate. They freed the demon that was holding the blocks in place, but they could not find a way to remove the blocks that wouldn't either kill her or render her catatonic.

She was gated to Marwatt's Reach during a magical fire-fight. The rest of the party is still wondering where she went. Here she met Kyira and became a part of Ghost Wind's crew. They ended up on River Wind and then in the Hut.

Angelique has connected with her in strange ways and it has helped her remain more awake. Their relationship also gives her a bit more focus. She is learning to apply the ability to see majik. She could well become a formidable pattern based mage. The linking between the items they each own has been enhanced by the effects of both of their personalities. Together they will be able to See in new and clearer ways.
