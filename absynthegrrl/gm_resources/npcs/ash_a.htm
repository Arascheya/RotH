Ash Aragon

Ash Aragon is a member of the Ghost Precinct.

Raw Notes:

hates humans from childhood trauma - lived in an area of town run by mods and thugs, watched the day to day ugliness and it seeped into her soul
her family was landed Tzani, from eastern europe. Her parents longed for the old ways as they struggled to avoid the thugs and survive. moving was not possible - too little money and nowhere to go
considers herself Tzani - has no qualms about killing humans

considers most humans gawakt (non-persons)
"it was gawakt, outsider, non-person. Killing gawakt does not matter."
considers ghost precinct people, thus non-gawakt

assigned to ghost precint after several incidents of casual violence to suspects - was vindicated because suspects were guilty, which is why she was not suspended or imprisoned
respects Talia and Shan after they demonstrate levels of dangerous competence at the library vaults
likes Leia Cassini because she is as broken as Ash, only differently
wants to learn the tactical skills she sees in Talia and Shan
very pale eyes, which tend to look cold and empty most of the time, warm up with Talia, Shan, and Leia
