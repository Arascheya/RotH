Kyira Dzahmahth

Kyira is a succubus that accompanies Irena in the Hut. Irena came across her in a brothel Tzell on her search for Margla Fleshfeaster. They became almost instant friends. Once Irena acquired the Hut and figured out how to use it, she returned to offer Kyira an escapre from the brothel.

Kyira loves women but must feed on men to sustain herself. She willingly allies herself with Irena when she finds out the Hut will sustain her without her needing to feed. She becomes the librarian out of boredom and a desire to learn, something her demonic background made difficult.
