Darien

Darien has a gentlemanly cowboy approach in his demeanor. His language is filled with Howdy's and Ma'am and sir kind of things. This was an anachronism in the Inferno, but a very pleasant sort. Quiet and reserved, Darien doesn't say much about himself. He came from a broken home and the bad parts of town. Over time he pulled himself out and doesn't speak of it much at all. He had a sister who was badly abused and raped by his father's brother. No one believed her so she disappeared one night. She has never been found.

Darien is from Ennui's Realm and was killed in one of the first attacks on Death City by the proxy gods.
