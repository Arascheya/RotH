Ayesha

Ayesha was in the employ of Valen and her regime. Her job involved her with oversite of various resource development companies throughout the Empire. In the course of her work she found various odditites in how OutSystems conducted business.

Ayesha is the predatory one - Kyira was born a predator and loathes parts of it, Ayesha became one through experience and intent. Ayesha kills without remorse and without much thought when in a hostile situation. Of the two, Kyira is the one with a highly developed sense of morality around killing. Need to show this through the action around them more than directly.

Ayesha was trained in infiltration and covert ops by the same school as Valen. Valen knew her slightly when they were in school together, however Ayesha doesn't recognize Valen as having been Sarah Draybin.
