Amarna

Operates Passions, a chocolate and coffee shop. This is the shop near the Goth clothing store and the dance shop in Death City.

Several months after the arrival of Kali and the Silent Woman, the Slient Woman will start roasting coffee and making chocolates at Passions. She will create a strangely exotic roaster and perfect the Tezkyakla "smoked coffee". Once the elaborate machine is working, Amarna will put a glass window between the shop and the roastery and chocolate prep area to show it off.
