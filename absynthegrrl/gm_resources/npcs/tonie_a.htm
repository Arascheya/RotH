Tonie Ayrthane

Sister of Dorotea Ayrthane and mother of Nadya Ayrthane. Tonie was the lead researcher on a powerful artifact shortly before the magic wars that devestated her world. The matriarch of the city had her researching ways to use the artifact in defense of the city and had insisted it be used too soon. The results were catastrophic, destroying large parts of the city and unleashing unexpected and bizarre magical side effects.
