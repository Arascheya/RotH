Anta

Anta is one of the two twins. She is the magical engineer on the Antigoni.

She was born on one of the poor outer edge cities. Her mother died when she was young and a local beggar chief took her and her sister in. They became beggars for his group.

Anta and Emly escaped from him by signing on with the Antigoni.

Emly and Anta are twins. They bicker constantly among themselves but are fiercely loyal to each other. For personalities think a mix of Ed from Cowboy Bebop and Ana and Uni Puma from Dominion: Tank Police.

Anta is intensely loyal to the Antigoni and her crew.
