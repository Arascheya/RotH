L'Arys - Second Mate

L'Arys is the latest addition to the crew of the Antigoni. She was the last in a family of all girls. She joined seeking adventure. Her father did not oppose her because he had no marriage prospects for her. She is the most optimistic and cheerful of the crew. She has not experienced the same number of set backs and disasters as the rest of the crew.

L'Arys is asexual. Her only interests lie in finding friends she can trust and enjoy the company of.
