Ghani

Appearance:

A genetic anomily has given her slightly blue tinge to the whites of her eyes.

Notes:

Ghani is a daughter of Margla. She is younger than her sister Krystal. Margla would not be able to tell her who her father was if whe were still alive to be asked. Rumor has it she came from a daliance with a powerful ruler on a world Margla happened to visit once.

Ghani had planned to be a Flower at The Asylum until she was attacked by a client. After she discovered her heritage as a daughter of the Hut and her basic immortality, she became a special operations person for The Asylum in addition t her Flower duties. She has mostly restricted her clients to women after her near death at the hands of a deranged male client. She works closely with the Madam of the The Asylum as a special operative.

One of her closest friends at The Asylum is Irulan, another Flower. Irulan is skilled with poisons, toxins, and political intrigue, being able to assist Ghani in her various other duties. Irulan often helps out in the shelter section of The Asylum and has befriended many, in particular, a quiet, shy young girl who is in reality one of the guises of She Who Must Never Be Named.
