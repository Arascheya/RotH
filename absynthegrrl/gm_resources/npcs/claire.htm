Claire

Claire is about 5'3" tall with black hair and hazel eyes.

Claire works at Temple Heath and sometimes visits the Devil's Cathouse. She is small and slight. Her jobs are mostly engineering drawings, planning, and logistics. Sometimes she feels invaluable and sometimes she feels valueless. It all depends on how involved she is that particular day. Claire does the lighting effects and sound for Love's Bitch.

Claire forms a mild crush on Kali once she sees how she dances and her confident and in control mannerisms. What Claire fails to recognize is that Kali's attitudes are sometimes just shells over her own insecurities.

Claire is shy and retiring outside the Cathouse. Inside the Cathouse she is a different person. She has begun to show more confidence and personality as she has become comfortable in the scene. She enjoys the Temple Heath work but she shines at the club.
