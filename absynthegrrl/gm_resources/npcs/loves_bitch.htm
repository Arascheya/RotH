Love's Bitch

House band at the Devil's Cathouse.

There are 6 band members, Nightshade (Margaux) on drums, Belladonna (Juli) on keyboards, Ennui on bass and vocals, Nausea (Maggie) on lead guitar and vocals, Kali as stage dancer and lead and backing vocals, and Merris on violin and vocals. Their sound is similar to a mix of The Cruxshadows, Evanescence, Within Temptation, and Abney Park.

The original line-up for Love's Bitch was Nightshade on drums, Belladonna on keyboards, Ennui on bass, Nausea on lead guitar and vocals, and Le Mort on lead vocals. Le Mort was killed in a dark force attack on a rescue mission. This is the mission Kali, Sammi, and Jemima were sent to help. Le Mort always dressed as an elegant Vampire About Town when performing. He always argued for more seriousness when performing.

Add descriptions - Nightshade - faith clone - wears cat morph makeup, pink wife beater, and black leather pants when performing, Belladonna - cave-buffy clone - wears Bride of Frankenstein makeup and a white wedding dress when performing, Nausea - marla singer clone - wears Bride of Frankenstein makeup and an old pink looking wedding dress when performing, Ennui - herself - wears Boogie Woman costume with a blood red crop top under the torn tuxedo coat when performing, Merris - as herself - wears death's head makeup and black hooded cloak when performing - once again assuming a guise of an angel of death.

Merris is the latest member of the band. She recruited herself after seeing a show with Kali after the band returned without Le Mort. There is a section in notes dealing with this scene as well as the updated open the band creates after Merris and Kali start. Kali was added shortly after as the stage dancer and backing vocals. She had come with Merris and the others saw her dancing along. Kali wears makeup and costuming similar to Sally from Nightmare Before Christmas.

On stage the three "brides" ham it up with each other constantly in psuedo-lesbian erotic movements and actions. Le Mort was always put off by this. Merris rolls with it in her role as reaper. Nightshade gets pulled in as well whenever someone goes back to where she is or when she comes forward with percussion that isn't her drum kit. The band is playful and having a good time. They don't take themselves seriously at all.

Typical Show Opening

There should be a rock-operatic feel to the cycle of songs written about the "Dead Girl Living". If the songs were performed back to back it would tell the story of a disfunctional couple, Doctor Fronkonstien and her lover. The lover commits a form of suicide because she feels like the doctor is too much into her own ego. The doctor calls her lover back and they try again. In the end, the lover leaves again and Fronkonstien needs to figure out how to move on. This cycle has been written primarily by Kali with assitance from the others.

Kali is the rawest singer in the band and tends to sing lead on the songs requiring the most intense and raw style. Nausea has a more lyrical and fluid singing style.
