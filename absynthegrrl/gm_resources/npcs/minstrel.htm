Haunts the Spirit of the Minstrel Tavern in Dihliz, located in the Ruined Kingdoms area of the Lotus Empire.

Backstory is similar - he is from the Kharatoum Sultanate time frame and will remain until Kalyana is restored and comes to see him. He will confess his love for her and be released at that point.

Minstrel of Dawn - Danilithor Tyazen - ghost - loved Kalyana one of the emperor’s twin daughters, the one who became the Shade. He can feel she is still alive and will not die until knows what has happened with her. He seeks to find her. He was searching for her when brigands hired by Nyigroth slew him. He was mistaken for their real target, the assistant to Tesch Mar whom was suspected of knowing the where the Moon Stones were. His ghost has remained at the Grey Minstrel Inn waiting for any news about Kalyana. If an adventuring group mentions anything about her or the Moon Stones he will seek to talk with them. He will follow them if they cannot or will not communicate with him.

He is able to recognize Kalyana and Nyssa and the Moon Stones should he see them. He does not know about what has happened to Kalyana, only that she is still alive. He knows Nyssa was bound into a ship and presented to the Sultan and that the Sultan committed suicide on the ship once he knew. He does know the Moon Stones were used to imprison the Lizard Wizards in a pocket dimension and that they had something to do with the formation of the Sinking Plain. This he knows from conversations he has overheard at the Inn.

Also - was minstrel in court of ruler whose daughter was bound as the ghost ship. he will be able to recognize her and also her sister. He knows that the binding was performed by Nyigroth and the means of freeing her. He was killed by bandits hired by Nyigroth to insure his silence. He can only be set free by....
