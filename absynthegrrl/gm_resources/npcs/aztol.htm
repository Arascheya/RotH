Aztol

Personality:

impatient spoiled child, after World Gate controller incidents he is completely insane desiring only to exact vengeance on Elentaryn. He will respond from anger to any afront. He is sociopathic, believing he knows best and anything in his way should be destroyed.

Appearance:

6’ tall, medium build, with violet eyes, and blondish hair. His age appears to be 90 to 120 yrs

Stats:

str-12;int-18;wis-16;dex-12;con-12;char15

Notes:

New Campaign Notes

Aztol was the youngest cousin to the Tolaz family in the Valley of Experiment. The main Tolaz family was wiped out, when he was about twelve, because they were sacrificing people to the dark demons to gain power. A group of Arcanes and Merchanters organized the raid, using their various household contingents. The slaughter was frightening. None from the village survived the purge except Aztol.

During his explorations as a child he had come across an access point to the Ancient's Way that ran beneath the village. He used this to flee when the butchery began. He fled west and ended up on the Isle of Research. There he managed to persuade the Mad Techno into training him in the ancient ways.

When the mad Techno discovered Aztol's twisted, nasty view of humanity, he attempted to exile him, dying in the process. Aztol retained the Mad Techno's island for his base, however, many of the Mad Techno's creations were destroyed in their fight.

Elentaryn launched an attack against Aztol, destroying much of the island's facilities before forcing Aztol to flee. This would have been after the World Gate debacle.

Aztol captured Seal Team6 from the Gate Dimension after fleeing Zeltrop. He took control of the commander, using a stolen brain scan system. He killed any of the crew that would not help him and forced the controlled commander to train new recruits.

Tech:

Special Forces troops are equipped with equipment based on the US land warrior system. This includes built in imaging system (video, night vision, and majik detection), comm. system (voice and heads up data display), positioning system including friendly and suspected enemy locations, and telemetry links back to the Spider Ship. They are also equipped with smart weapons capable of "seeing" with IR and majik detection devices. These weapons link back to the helmet system allowing forces to use their weapons to see around corners and into other hostile places without exposing themselves.

Regular troops are equipped with comm. gear and positioning indicators so Special Forces can determine where they are in battle situations.

Currently possesses the Spider Ship of Ishra. He has Ishra held captive through the use of a brain scan unit and a Keeper type organism which allows him to retain control of her mind.

When Aztol fled his base of operations was secured by Kayara and Sharyl. They have transfered most of the equipment to Ghunayya, a former associate of Aztol. They have checked her out and believe she is honorable enough for them to deal with. So far she has not betrayed their trust. She is Milo's contact for equipment and information. Milo and the Ghost Ship are her far ranging eyes and ears.

Older Data

The local population believed that knowledge and technology were responsible for the current poor state of their world. He and 2 friends were out exploring when the secret police gathered up his family but happened to witness the death of his father and humiliation of his mother. They were exploring an ancient site of some form.

Early in his life he fell in love with a girl named Maranda in Altaarkrn. It didn’t last. As he aged he became more twisted by what he perceived to be failings in the world around him.

He swore to gather enough power and people to extract vengeance. He has somehow acquired a bit of knowledge about the access tile on Karanal and desires info about it, along with the Cobra Book history said to reside in the Gardens of Lazaar. He was forced to lead a rescue mission to get them back. He lost Krystal on that mission and then found out she had known about the Cobra Book he was searching for.
