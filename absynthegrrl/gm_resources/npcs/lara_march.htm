The rest of the family is very traditional and very religious, in the born-again, creepy, judgmental kind of way. Use the Christian radio station lines on her, from her brother, if they fit.

Lara will be the most sensitive to the Veil. She has always been a bit fei, escaping into books and fantasy to endure her home-life. This ledher into her own mind and down to the shattered core,the burned out city-scape with the huddled, burned figure that is her soul-image. In that wreckage she found the Core of Being. She can see the interconnections between the various planes and sheets of the multi-verse, seeing it in a three plus dimensional construct. For her, the visions are triggered by being near the Hell Gate. Once she passes through it, she has a more complete understanding, including the understanding that she can mold herself by her will. As she learns where the various gates are on the world,she can use them to get from place to place,becoming her own gate controller.

Lara and Dan are avid gamers and are following the Realms of the Hut development almost as closely as Talia.
