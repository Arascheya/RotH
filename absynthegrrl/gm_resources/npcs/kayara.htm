Kayara

Personality:

Kaya (Trickster): gypsy allegria, fun loving, Meli with brains. The Trickster appears as a young woman with an open, radiant smile and the glint of mischief in her eyes. Her hair is jet black, her skin medium-dark, and she is usually dressed in T'Sani young woman wear, long skirts, a skimpy but frilly top, and wearing lots of gaudy jewelry.

Yara: old gypsy seer, appears as OLD gypsy woman - she will appear in Tzell from time to time, usually coincides with the appearance of Vishina. She can also be used in any town on the rivers if the River Wind is docked there. She will sometimes travel with the River Wind to other towns.

Dancer: seductive, high cha, associates only with Stormy and old fisherman

Steele Gazelle/Vishina: seductive, high cha, Valley of Experiment and Dark Carnival variant of the Dancer persona, she dances at the Diamond Spider in Tzell two nights a week, every fifth week, three sets each night.

Vishina wears two rings. One ring has a small needle tipped with a substance called Skintwister which causes anyone injected to have violent convulsions for about five minutes. The other ring contains a powdered poison called Black Rapture which is lethal within 30 seconds of its ingestion. She will use the first ring on someone who playfully molests her; she reserves the second ring for the truly crude types. She also wears a long comb in her hair which she can use as a weapon.

Blind Seer of Jakara: She leans on a young urchin, probably a girl, but under the rags and dust it is hard to tell. The seer wears old robes, from a time when many wore more concealment and protection from the heat. Her hair is bone white, her eyes milky and filmed wth just the faintest tint of green fire in the depths. Her skin is leathery and seamed from too much wind and not enough water. She is a far cry from the water-fat populace of Jakara.

Appearance:

Ht: 5'7". Wt: 130 Ibs. Age: immortal. Hair: black, Skin: medium-dark Eyes: dark jade green, lit with an inner fire - this feature remains constant across all incarnations except the blind seer in the Lotus Empire areas where her eyes are milky white, with a very faint green tinge - can be seen on a really good spot roll. These are eyes like no other that the players have seen and the appearance of them will make a strong impression.

Notes

For new campaign - Kayara is aligned with Irena and the Hut in terms of balance keeping. She runs a travelling carnival, The Dark Carnival, similar to the Ravenloft one, and has enabled the World Gate system so she can use it. It isn't as easy or specific as the Hut, but it allows her to get around. Tyana set up the redirects and the smoke screen utilities so Elentaryn still believes the World Gates are sealed. Sharyl made a big deal of asking him to allow Miskatonic U. to bring students in through a slaved gate in the basements of the school.

As the Ancients aged each one aquired special abilities. Kayara can control the World Gates using only her mind. Sharyl is a natural majik user. Elentaryn has enhanced technical skills, mostly hardware based. All three can access the Core of being, the racial memory, going back to the beginnings of the race on Karanal.

Holo Message - used when she appears as the blind seer of the Galhai Wastes

Kayara has developed skills allowing her the capabilities of a Face Dancer of Rune Lord status. She is also the trickster. Nothing delights her more than to set a plan in motion to point adventurers toward some new goal or problem. She is even more delighted if it flies in the face of Elentaryn's carefully manipulated view of how things should progress.

She is one of Shandi's moms, the other is Sharyl, also known as Stormy. Elentaryn does not know this. As far as he is concerned he is Shandi's father.

YARA - another fragment if it’s needed

A neutral power has touched you. You stand at a crossroads in a time when many forces are striving for power. Very little is certain and the outcome is clouded by too many omens, portents, and seeings. One force seeks ‘Planetary Defense Command’, another seeks to not submit to an old master, a third desires dominion, another strives to be free of an old curse, revenge and power is sought by the crazed one, another seeks to close what is open and open what is closed and free that which is long bound, and one seeks harmony and balance.

Many things figure into the twists of these desires: ancient items known as the Well Artifacts, of which there are four, two have been found; an obscure tower dancing in shadows; 7 great orbs of power; a path of statues; a hut - larger within than without; a book bound in dragon hide with an odd name; and lastly a very special set of twins one who must be saved from magic by magic before she can help.
