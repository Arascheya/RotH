Belladonna - Juliqayan (Juli) Bethany Demryths

Bella plays keyboards for Love's Bitch, including the theatre organ once it is operational. Bella looks like cave-buffy and appears on stage as a Bride of Frankenstein makeup. She and Nausea are lovers. They are also very quiet and private people so the rest of the band knows very little about them.

Belladonna is one of the old people Merris released in Duluth. Once Merris joins the band Bella will slowly start to recognize her. Once she does she will thank Merris for her help. This is when Merris first truly believes she did good as the "Angel of Death".

Bella was an actress for most of her life. In her declining days she returned to her home in Duluth and remained active as a sponsor and guest artist for the local community theatre groups. She went into the hospital after a serious stroke.
