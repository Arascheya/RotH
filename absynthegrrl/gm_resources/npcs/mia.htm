Mia - previous player character

Mia comes from the region near the Isle of Experiment. She was a player character in the Dhar Ankes/Oriz scenario and one of the first iterations of the Hut. She was from a group of Shape Shifters - of the were-tiger clans.

Mia-

You awaken in a room with walls, floor, and ceiling made of a silver metal with a lime green tinge to them. The room is lit with bright white light from square panels in the ceiling.

You feel as though someone has placed your head in a vise and squeezed, but the feeling is not physical, more like you have had too many things happening all at once and your mind has shut down and is now just beginning to wake up again. You have vague recollections about incredible sights of pasts, futures, and alternate "nows". None of the images are clear enough to actually remember except one of you wearing gold chain around your neck with a web of woven platinum suspended from it. Inside the web of platinum is an egg shaped orb of green jade intricately etched with ornate traceries with gold beaten into them. You were using this necklace to search for something else, something important.

About twenty feet away from you you see a male human with curly light brown hair, going slightly grey, a close trimmed beard and he is wearing close fitting leggings/breeches of a blue colored coarse fabric and a woolen shirt with a checked pattern of bright colors. On the floor next to him is a strange wooden device, with strings, similar to a lute but with a different body shape.

As your head begins to clear you feel as if some part of yourself is missing but other parts are even more complete than they were before. As your head clears even more you realize part of the confusion is some of the feelings of confidence and security are coming from Aria and Landria. You feel better able to deal with yourself and others and your confidence is almost as great as when you left your tribe, before being caught by the slavers.
