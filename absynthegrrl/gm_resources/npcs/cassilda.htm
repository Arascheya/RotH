Cassilda (Muggles - the little servant demon) - bound High Priestess of Meera

Betrayed and imprisoned by The Hand early in his rise to power. She is the last of the Meera priesthood and the last to hold the knowledge of the spells of the cult. She was imprisoned in a blue glass bottle and left buried under Haven. To anyone finding the bottle she appears as a lesser demon capable of performing simple commands. Should any give her drink, food, and let her sleep with them she will revert back to her original form. Sleeping with Muggles does not require sex, just holding and cuddling. Once in this form she desires to find the holy relics of Meera and work to re-establish the cult. She has Face Dancer capabilities once she is freed.

Yes, my dear you shall be as a lowly little demon, ha ha ha!"

Thus I was until you found and released me. What he has done with my pendant I do not know but I must find it. Contained within it are some of the Rune Spells my cult knows.
