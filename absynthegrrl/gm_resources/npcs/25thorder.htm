Tesch Mar - He was thought to be one of the greatest mages ever to have lived. He died(?) in a fight with Nyigroth that caused the Great Wastes about 400 years ago. Created a fortress after coming across a book speaking of the magical constructs of Margla FleshFeaster. He bound the Lizard Kings into the pocket universe of Trezgath when they threatened to destroy the human cities of the desert plains. He used an artifact of great power and scattered the control crystals for it when he was done. These became known as the Tears of Kharatoum after the disasters visited upon his friend the Sultan of Kharatoum. His theories and abilities are legendary. Every so often an item he created is still found in long abandoned hordes.

Tzilrynor - Tzilrynor was known mostly for his research into soul capture and storing. He created the Soul Eater toward that purpose and managed to capture many Arcanes until he was stopped by She Who Must Never Be Named and Margla in the City of Doors. He had a tower or stronghold somewhere near the Dwarven First Home but it has never been found. It is here that he created Soul Eater.
