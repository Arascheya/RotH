Eleza Ekretherion

Eliza is a fighter. High spirited and brash she will claw her way through anything in her way. She comes from a dieing world where a Death City Recovery Team was sent.

Born on a world in conflict she was part of a last stand battle. During the final moments she was stunned by a blow from an opposition trouper. Kali found her under some rubble. When she awoke she first tried to gut Kali in her disorientation. Then she thought Kali was the goddess when she looked up. At last Kali convinced her she was only another person.

Eliza is a Shadow Walker as well. Eleza is restlessness and angry and is even more obvious than it is in Kali. Through her Kali begins to see what Merris was talking about.

For personality think a cross between Faith and Eliza.
