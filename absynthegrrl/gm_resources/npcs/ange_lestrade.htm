Angelique Mereret Lestrade

Angelique was named for her great great great grandmother, who was named in honor of a very dear friend of Inspector Lestrade, her great great great great grandfather.

Mega-Corp Ascendency thread notes

Ange lives with her family in the building above Lestrade and Petrie Books, a used and new book shop that has been in her family for generations. She works a variety of small jobs in the non mega-corp areas of London. She has skills that would benefit a mega-corp, although she prefers not to deal with the layers of complexity they involve. She is friends with Yasmine Raisul, who is a regular at the book shop and at the coffee shop where Ange has a second job.

**History**

Hometown / Region: born and reaised in London, Whitechapel area; currently living in Seoul

Biography:
Born to Amber and Paul Lestrade, owners of a local Whitechapel book shop. The book shop has been in her family for generations, having been bought by her great great great great grandfather in the late 1890's. He started the hidden collections, a collection of esoterica and ancient items that each generation has built upon. In the depths of the shop are entrances to passages and tunnels that lead to the Templar headquarters.

Mere was always interested in Oriental and Occult medicine. Two years before the Orachi Incident she received a scholarship to study in Seoul South Korea, with a world reknown group of practitioners. She was in Seoul when the world fell apart.
