Imhotep - 2650-2576 BCE - 3rd Dynasty

Imhotep was an early watcher. In his exhibit are several items that refer to this. Watcher’s council will want to see these. Mereret will want to recover these to see if there is anything about vampyr hunters in them.

There are several scrolls and items in the collection at the British Museum display that refer to Angelique's Necklace and the Anklet Shandi has. In particular there is one that is desired because it contains the ritual to activate a Cthulian Gate similar to the one at Stonehenge.
