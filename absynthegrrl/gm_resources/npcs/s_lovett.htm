Sarah Lovett

Owner of a meat pie shop on Fleet St. Local vampires use her to dispose of annoying corpses left over after a night of frivolity. Angelique has used her services from time to time to get rid of some street pest or the other. In return, she has used her resources to remove the left-over bits to reduce the displeasing odor of the dead.

The meat pies are a taste sensation and favored by many in the nearby shops and businesses. Many will make the trip from other parts of London for a fresh one.
