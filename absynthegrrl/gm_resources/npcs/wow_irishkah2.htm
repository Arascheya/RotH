Irishkah - Blood Elf - Paladin - Horde

Starts in Eversong Woods

Older sister of Narshala. She has been sent by the family to track down her sister. She was fond of her sister, but doesn't know what to make of the woman she finds. One who stoops to consorting with the undead and the barbarian trolls over her own kind.

Professions - Jewelcrafting and Mining
