Barake Milzithin

Daughter of Kaltereth and mother of Goraze. Barake was commander of the regular army at the time of the Hell Gate Wars. She served under Alucinia and met Irena of the Hut.

Barake has a retirement home in the R9 section nearest the palace. She is a close friend of the current calipha and has maintained contacts with many of the women from her command. Her advice on tactics is still valued by the current military command structure.
