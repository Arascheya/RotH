Aleister Crowley - 1865 - 1937 - (modified for this time-line)

Aleister Crowley - some type of servant of the Cthulians - he is watching from the shadows as the players emerge from the Hut. he will attempt to follow them and engage in conversation when they enter a public house or other place.

Crowley and the Golden Dawn are working to open a gate at Stonehenge for the Old Ones to return. Lord Willem Wellingham has gotten word of it and is getting the Door Keepers involved. Crowley has been made aware of the Hut and will seek audience with the people from it with the hope of getting majikal assistance from them.
