Sarahabra - The Trickster

Basics

God(dess) of mischief and chance. This deity is gender ambiguous and ambiguous about most other things as well. Everything is pleasing, but most particularly a good jest or bit of mischief. The mischief should result in no permanent physical or survivability harm to the target. Supreme embarassment is fine however.

Members must strive to see the humor in all situations. A truly enlighted member would be able to laugh as they lay dying.

Basic Membership

Requirements:

Tithe 5% of income to the temple

Sacrifice one point of power on holy days, 2 points on high holy days

Pull a minor prank at least once per month

Benefits:

Half cost training in Healing spells, Sleight of Hand, Pick Lock, Pick Pocket, Climb, Move Quietly, and Hide Self

Half cost training in Dagger, Staff, Staff Sling, Bastard Sword

Initiate Membership

Requirements:

Tithe 10% of income to the temple

Sacrifice two points of power on holy days, 3 points on high holy days

Pull a minor prank at least three times per month

Benefits:

Battle Magic Spell: Minor Assess Odds - cost: free

Battle Magic Spell: Alter Odds - cost: free

Face Dancer Ability and Training. Cost: 100 gold plus 8 points of power. The listed cost is for the begining setup. To begin this training the High Priest(ess) of the temple must first cast a specialty spell on the Initiate to prepare them for the training. Once the preparations are complete the training is 10 gold per session. Each session will give 1D4+1% improvement. See the Face Dancer listing for data on particular skills and levels. Initiates may train to Level 5 Face Dancer skills.

Priesthood Membership

Requirements:

Tithe 15% of income to the temple

Sacrifice three points of power on holy days, 5 points on high holy days

Pull a major prank at least once per month - this must embarrass soeone in prminence in the local social group or it must release information to the general public that has been secreted by a person or group.

Must have been an Initiate in good standing for several years.

Benefits:

Free lodging at any temple.

Access to Rune Spells listed below

Face Dancer skill training above level 5 for 5 gold per session.

Cult Specialty Spells

Battle Magic Spells

Minor Assess Odds

Power Cost: 2pts, Duration: Instantaneous

Gives the caster the approximate chance of success for a small venture or event.

Alter Odds

Power Cost: 2pts + Var, DUration: Instantaneous

This spell will alter the odds of an action succeeding. The basic spell gives a 2% adjustment. Each additional point of poweradded to the casting adds another 2%. This spell will not alter large scale events, only the chances the caster has in performing a particular personal action.

Rune Magic Spells

Minor Mischief

Power Cost: 1pts, Duration: 15 minutes

This spell causes the target to be effected by a minor occurrence of Murphy’s laws. Effects are never life threatening.

Major Mischief

Power Cost: 2pts, Duration: 15 minutes

This spell causes the target to be effected by a major occurrence of Murphy’s laws. Effects are never life threatening, however they may be emotionally or physically painful.

Embarrass

Power Cost: 2pts, Duration: 15 minutes

This spell causes the target to do or say things that will cause embarrassment. This is most effective when cast on the target in a public place or situation.

Assess Odds

Power Cost: 3pts, Duration: Instantaneous

Gives the caster the approximate chance of success for a venture or event. This is a more powerful and informative version of the Battle Magic Spell.

Role Reversal

Power Cost: 4pts, Duration: 15 minutes

This spell reverses the mental positions of the targets for 15 minutes. It surrounds the targets with a warding 6 and shield 4 for the duration (must have both spells, the durations for these are extended by the roll reversal spell). The targets must be in opposition about a common point of view situation. Max number of targets: 2 per side of disagreement.
