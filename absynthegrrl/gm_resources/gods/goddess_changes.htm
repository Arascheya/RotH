Goddess of Changes

She represents any change, from the gentle changing of the seasons, to the violence of holocaust.

Her face resembles the parasite race, she has many arms, some with gifts, some with weapons, some cradling Eritsani, some destroying them, she stands astride worlds. She is worshipped in many different ways depending on the desires and feelings of the faithful at the time. (draw on Kali for ideas)

Behold I am Eritsa, changer of worlds, look upon me and know fear.
