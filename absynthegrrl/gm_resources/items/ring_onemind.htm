Ring of Onemind

This item was created by the Drkzyrkth, as part of a suite of tech items for the Razaks. It contains the seed code for a mind melding genetic modification. It allows the updated organisms to link minds into a hive mind arrangement. There were seven rings originally, however, only one has survived the destruction of the Razak homeworld. This ring is currently on Zeltrop, in the possession of Amorama, a lich formed from the fighter pilot who recovered the ring from a Razak courier.

Older Concepts

When combined with the Crystal Skull it will call the Creation Team to its location.
