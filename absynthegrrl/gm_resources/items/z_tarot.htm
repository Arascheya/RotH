MAJOR ARCANA

0 - ADVENTUER

A young man stands on the nose of a nuclear warhead staring upward with a look of joy and expectation. He is wearing bright colors and carrying a stick with a bundle as if he were beginning a long journey. There is a double helix embroidered on his tunic and he is holding a white zerym flower in his left hand. (zerym = seven petaled flower with red center) The Sun-Star is in the upper right corner of the card.

Divinatory: Misuse, abuse of knowledge or power; folly; mania; extravagance; intoxication; delirium; frenzy; beginning of adventure; youth and energy

Reversed: negligence; absence; distribution; carelessness; apathy; vanity

2 - HIGH ELDER

A man dressed in a brown jump suit is seated in a green throne with rearing king cobras on the fronts of the arms. He wears a ring with a clear crystal with purple swirls in it. The throne is on a high mountain top with blackness forming the sky behind him. Over his right shoulder is a glowing purple sphynx with part of its light being drawn into a swirling vortex over his left shoulder. His face has a triangular scar running from his forehead, above his left eye, to the bridge of his nose, then down along the edge of his left eye to the middle of his cheek. His eyes are an intense piercing green.

Divinatory: life through thought and contemplation; infinity of possibilities; power; stability; knowledge; control; reason; psionic forces; confidence; leadership; willingness to listen to counsel; togetherness; action; decision; energy; magnanimity; fairness

Reversed: confusion to enemies; terror; condemnation; psionic death or loss of power; over control; over confidence; abuse of power; weakness; discord; disharmony; delusion

3 - HIGH PRIEST

A figure dressed in a hooded black robe, obscuring its face, is standing between two pillars and holding a brown ball candle in the left and a crooked staff in the right hand. Two others in black robes kneel before the figure. On the back of the chair is a five pointed star with balls on the points.

Divinatory: power of the gods; concentration; serving the gods or fate; order hierarchy; conformity; rune or priestly magic

Reversed: rigidity; inflexibility; disorder; conceit; false prophets; serving own desires

4 - MISTRESS OF MAGIC

A black haired woman stands on a windswept ridge with her hair blowing in the wind. She is holding a book bound in cobras in her left hand. Her right hand is raised and receiving/releasing lightning. She wears a scarlet robe and black cloak. The cloak clasp is a seven pointed star.

Divinatory: power of man; study; deep knowledge; individual roads; ostracism

Reversed: false knowledge; excess emotion; lust; lack of understanding

5 - ARTIFICER

A slight, 5' foot tall man with bronze eyes, dark skin, and short brown hair stands at a table. He is wearing a green robe belted with a serpent biting its tail. He holds a piece of clear crystal with purple swirls in his upraised right hand. His left hand is pointing towards the ground. On the table in front of him are various pieces of electronic equipment, wands, staves, crystal balls, and other things. Also on the table are the symbols for the four minor arcana suits. Over his head is an infinity symbol in the shape of a tipped over figure eight.

Divinatory: skill; confidence; will; creativity; flexibility

Reversed: physical and mental disease; disgrace; disquiet; lack of skill; pain; use of skills or will to destructive ends

6 - MYSTERY

An empty throne sits at the head of a table with twelve chairs, in a stark and empty room. Twelve beings, five men, five women, a succubus, and a tiefling sit around the table. A street urchin peers at them from a dark doorway.

Divinatory: mystery; unknown; anticipated revelation; benefit in the shadows

Reversed: detriment in the shadows; fearful unknown; anticipated horror

7 - EMOTION

/* need red moon based description */

Divinatory: emotion; passion; concern; care for friends and others

Reversed: suppressed emotions; dangerous emotional tensions; uncontrolled emotions or lusts

8 - THOUGHT

/* need blue moon based description */

Divinatory: balance or unity of emotion and logic; knowledge to be gained

Reversed: cold knowledge untempered by compassion

9 - DANCER

A dark haired woman dressed in brightly colored skirts and top dances with a tambourine. Behind her to the left is a gypsy wagon and camp, to her right is a library built in Moorish motif. Behind her is a fire blending into a fountain formed of three rearing king cobras. The images of the encampment and library fade into a dark area behind her and around the fire/fountain. Both scenes are lit with fire or torch light.

Divinatory: life through action and motion; secrets; mystery; womanhood; balance; life; vitality; intuition; change, layer upon layer of complexities, joy of action

Reversed: life; fun loving abandon; rejoicing; complement; truth behind illusion

10 - MULTI-VERSE

Two sphynxes face each other with a web of purple between them. There is a spiral galaxy visible through the web.

Divinatory: endless possibilities; hope; growth; expansion

Reversed: darkness; locked doors; self imposed mind blocks; shriveling; shallowness

11 - MAHASAN

A large library with a fountain formed of three rearing king cobras. The architecture is Moorish in design. One wall opens onto a beautiful garden with snow capped mountains rising in the background.

Divinatory: return; home; paradise; heart's desire; happiness; spiritual strength; salvation; completion; perfection

Reversed: return with loss; home has changed from what is remembered; spiritual chains; heart's desire impossible to attain; imprisonment; despair

12 - DEATH

A skeletal figure wearing a grey robe and a heads-up display battle helmet leads a group of soldiers down a featureless corridor. The entire group is carrying blasters and wearing light battle armor.

Divinatory: death of self; death of ideals; change of viewpoint; upheaval; death and rebirth

Reversed: as above but less intense; slow or partial change; stagnation; unable to transcend or move on

13 - FIERY CHARIOT

A man in full space battle armor stands under a delta winged air/space craft. There is a military looking insignia on the armor and on the tail of the craft.

Divinatory: war; battle; vengeance; trouble; possible journey; rushing into decisions

Reversed: loss; folly; disputes; conflict through indecision

14 - CONFUSION

A seven pointed star illuminates a large building with a graceful tower emerging from its many domed roofs. Lightning bolts strike the tower from storm clouds to the left of the building.

Divinatory: disruption; confusion; scattered plans; shattered dreams

Reversed: known unknowns; possible understanding; remembered dreams

15 - LOVERS

A red haired woman in tattered tunic is seated on a log near a fire. She is leaning against a reddish brown furred cat woman who is also seated on the log. They are tired but relaxed and their expressions and postures indicate they are lovers.

Divinatory: love; contentment; beauty; happiness; trust; deep feelings; commitment

Reversed: hard choices; rough times; trials; frustrations; interference by others; unwise plans and liaisons; separations

16 - SEEKER

A man in a brown robe holds a zerym shaped begging bowl and leans on a cobra staff. He is looking down a narrow, high walled, desert valley.

Divinatory: seeking; search for meaning or knowledge; life's hard road of trials

Reversed: goals in view; search approaching its end; revelation

17 - BALANCE

A bar balances on a yin yang symbol. On the right end of the bar is a black ring. On the left end of the bar is a glowing crystal skull.

Divinatory: balance; compliments; opposite yet joined; stability or forced stability; hope; help; aid; friends

Reversed: deadly opposites; bound enemies; the knife in the back; breaking of the balance; loneliness; false friends; instability; possible betrayal

18 - REVELATION

A large stone monolith pierced with an oval hole at its top stands in the center of a walled garden. It is lit by full red and blue moons. A shaft of red light shines through the hole in the monolith and illuminates a small section of ground. Where the beam strikes is a small stone patch ringed by flowers.

Divinatory: sudden insight; hidden revealed; ideals revealed

Reversed: faulty insights; twists; convolutions; truth concealed; faulty or confused ideals

19 - RAZAK

A squared eared, round eyed humanoid is holding a black ring shaped like a DNA double helix. From the ring come chains which are connected to collars around the necks of a man and a woman.

Divinatory: treachery; treason; corruption; slavery to false ideals; concealment; loss of personal will; loss of self; confinement fear; group mind set

Reversed: enemies revealed; release; overcoming insurmountable handicaps; gaining of self; individuality; beginning of self understanding

20 - SACRIFICE

A simple stone altar has a human bound on it. The body is impaled with a serpentine dagger.

Divinatory: letting go of the old: embracing the new; loss of something or someone loved; moral sacrifice; new experiences; readjustments

Reversed: as above but less intense; useless sacrifice

21 - OUTER WORLDS

A large starship circles a blue planet with clouds. The ship is formed of a spherical front hull with a cylindrical rear hull.

Divinatory: new horizons; broadening world view; travel; adventure; journey toward final goals

Reversed: internal broadening; opening to spirit or psionic forces; inner strength of being; travel into the Core of Being
