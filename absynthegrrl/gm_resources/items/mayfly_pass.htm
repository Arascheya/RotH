Mayfly Pass

An amethyst given to a potential Dark Carnival Mayfly (new comer) if they are hesitant about running away with the Carnival, but hesitant about remaining in their current location. It will glow and function as a tracking and calling device under the dominant moon of the world it is distributed on. It is distributed by Kayara to those she feels will definitely end up running away to join.

The effect will typically be a low level suggestion to travel to a point where the Carnival can arrive, without being noticed, and pick up the Mayfly. This usually requires a significant journey on the part of the Mayfly. The idea being, if they weren't ready to join when the Carnival was in town, they need to prove a certain level of determination for it to return for them.

The function of these was inspired by the Hut Summoning Gems used by Margla and Irena.
