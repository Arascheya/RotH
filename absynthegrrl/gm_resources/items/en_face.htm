Enlightenment Face

The Enlightenment Face is an old Chinese burial mask, made of red enameled porcelain, carved in the image of a serene, seemingly genderless Asian face, eyes half closed in contemplation.

Wearing the mask provides a potent defense against the mental invasions of oni and undead. The wearer receives a +4 enchantment bonus on WILL saves made to resist the mental tampering of outsiders and undead, whether from supernatural or spelllike abilities or spells.
