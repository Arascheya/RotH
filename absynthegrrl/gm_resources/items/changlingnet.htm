Changling Net

This technological device looks like a finely woven mesh of clearish fibers. When activated it projects a convincing hologram of another person. It will alter body shape and facial features. It works best when the body shapes of the disguise and the wearer are similar. It can create an energy field to simulate different sizes but this depletes the power system more quickly and a perceptive observer has a better chance of detecting the differences.

These are usually powered by small fuel cells or Fusion Reaction Packs if found in an Ancient Site.
