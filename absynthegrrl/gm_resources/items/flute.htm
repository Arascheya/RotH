Flute of Charming

Weapons Triad Item

Weapon Type: exotic

Damage: Special

Appearance: a plain silver flute

Int: - Pow: -

This flute will play the most haunting and enchanting tune the owner desires. It will charm anywhere from one to a room full of people, resolved as power vs power between the owner and the target(s). Once each day it can be commanded to fire a sleep dart at any particular target. The sleep potion on the dart will last 40-Con hours.

The charmed person(s) will do anything within reason that the owner of the flute suggests. Treat as a very successful hypnosis attack.

This item functions as a locator for the Hut.
