Vampyrites

Vampyrism is caused by a nanotechnology, vampyrites, produced by the Drkzyrkth, a now long-dead race. The vampyrites were designed as a self-replicating, self-spreading probe mechanism for sampling data from other races. They are blood/fluid born and require blood transfer to infect the target. Vampyrites are self replicating and through their bonding with the target biological they confer a type of immortality. They will maintain life at the "death" of the target, be it slow aging death or rapid death.

Unfortunately the vampyrites so radically alter the target organism that reliable data gathering is not possible. Another side-effect is the desire to spread the probes - this results in complete alteration of the target species, negating the effectiveness of the probes. This need to spread themselves is the strongest driving force for the goals of the infected.

There is one "cure" for vampyrites, however it was not found by Iskandera during her travels. There is a Reducer Nanite strain available on the Drkzyrkth world. These nanites will consume the vampyrites and then become inert. This will successfully remove vampyrism from the host if the host is young enough that life support can continue un-aided by the vampyrites.

A wandering Arcane, Iskandera, is the 0 Vector, having become infected by the vampyrites when visiting the homeworld.
