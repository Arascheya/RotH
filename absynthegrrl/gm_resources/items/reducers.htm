Reducers - nanite eaters

These were developed by the Drkzyrkth to destroy problem nanites they may have developed. They are broad spectrum with regards to Drkzyrkth designed units. Their effectiveness on tech from other races is not known.

Currently the only source for these is on the dead home world of the Drkzyrkth race. This is located somewhere in the Cluster 1 sub-galaxy near the Zeltropian galaxy.
