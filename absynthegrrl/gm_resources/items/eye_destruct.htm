Eye Of The Destructor

This item will allow the High Priest of the Destructor to cast Morgan's Red Death 2 times each day and Flames of Doom 2 times each day.

This item demands the use of its spells at least once each week to feed itself.

It will reveal any hidden, secret, concealed, planeshifted, invisible, ethereal, or astral being within 100 feet of itself to the wearer. This effect takes the form of a greenish outline painted around the beings detected. This glow is only visible to the holder of the Eye.

All demons below the rank of Lord must obey the holder.

This will allow teleport to any level of hell or the abyss if there is a fully opened Hell Gate nearby.

Will automatically heal the weilder as long as the spell uses above have been met.

The user can concentrate through the olivine eye itself and break through an Elder Sign with the sacrifice of 4 permanent power points.

This dread item belonged to the High Priest of the Destructor in the early times of the universe. It was buried with him when the last priest died in the last fight between Creation and Destruction that was not fought through proxies.

Currently its whereabouts are unknown.

Translations: Front: Top: Eye of Etesa; Bottom: reveals all; Back: Top: N LO LT L FR; Bottom: NL FL NR BK

The back is the order of access procedure - which sections to go to and light the lamps in each. Each lamp burns with a dark light, drawing light into itself and creating pools of inky blackness.

There are shadow versions of this item drifting through the multi-verse, with reduced and mutated powers.
