Lemon Drops

Lemon Drops are an arcane disinhibitor, a magical date rape drug. Lemon Drops are a predator’s tool, a favorite tactic for demonic sexual hunters.

Lemon Drops are grape sized crystals made of some strange amber mineral, their surface covered in shallow grooves and lines. When shattered, the crystal releases intoxicating amber and pink vapors, unleashing a red zone spell as cast by a 9th level mage.
