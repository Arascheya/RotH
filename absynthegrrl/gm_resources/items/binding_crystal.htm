Spirit Bind Crystal - Gem of Holding and Binding

This is usually a 1/2" to 1" diameter, multi-faceted, clear crystal.

These are the best items to bind spirits into. Their special properties allow for binding a spirit at a higher level of remaining power during combat.

Gem of Holding and Binding is the technical name as referenced in various arcane tomes and notes. Rarely is this name used outside these references.
