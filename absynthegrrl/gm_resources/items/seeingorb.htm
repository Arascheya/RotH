Seeing Orb

Appearance: a smokey grey crystal ball about 2 inches in diameter.

This orb is one of the communications devices used by the Hand to control or talk with his minions. It can function as a crude locator, can send suggestions to the holder, and will give two way communication with the Hand.

This item can also be used similar to a palantir, however there is a 25% chance each use that the Hand will become aware of the use and attempt to exert control over the user. Reslove as a Power vs Power with the Hand being Power 21.
