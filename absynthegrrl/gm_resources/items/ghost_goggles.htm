Ghost Sight Goggles

Ghost Sight Goggles seamlessly integrate technology and magic. A pair of military night vision goggles are enhanced by the addition of runic symbols and blessed lenses inserted into the system. Ghost Sight Goggles are standard field equipment for most of Black Tokyo’s elite demon hunters.

In addition to functioning as a mundane pair of night vision goggles, granting the wearer a technological darkvision, the Ghost Sight Goggles allow the wearer to detect magical auras, as if under the effects of the spell cast by a first level mage. Outsiders are surrounded by a faint orange-yellow aura; anyone who has had physical contact with any outsider within the last hour is surrounded by an even fainter amber outline.
