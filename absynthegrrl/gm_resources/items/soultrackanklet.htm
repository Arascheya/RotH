Anklet of the Soul Trackers

A silver anklet with tiny bells that allows a Soul Tracker to communicte with any sentient being throughout the multi-verse. It will conform to the ankle of the wearer and fit perfectly. The bells will chime when assimilation of the target language is complete.

Soul Tracker anklets attune to the wearer and cannot be transfered after attunement. Tiny fragments of Thought Crystal are embedded in the links of the top chain so they will come into contact with the wearer's skin. Silk cloth prevents attunement and is how they are stored and transported.
