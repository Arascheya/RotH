Cassi Items

Earrings of Purple 7 Petaled Flower with white gem stones set into their centers. Acts as power enhance 4 for Healing spells, power enhance 1 for other spells. Each item has a POW of 15 when more than one is being attuned to the same person at 1 POW point for each item to 15.

Necklace of a large Purple 7-petaled Flower with white gem stone set in its center, mounted on a gold chain. When worn it gives a Preserve Herb spell once a day, twice on Holy Days. Also it will cast a Forlorn Insistment once per month.

If the necklace is worn with the earrings, all Rune Spells are usable twice in one day but this power is only usable once per month.
