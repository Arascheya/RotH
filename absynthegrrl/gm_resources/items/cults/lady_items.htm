Lady Items

A bracer that breaks into inter-locking "bangle" type bracelets. Every conceivable color and metal have been used to make this item. It has a POW of 18, when it is attuned the pattern needed to put it back into a bracer is given to the attuned person. This bracer gives the wearer a Protection spell of 10 and a Counter Magic of 10. When in the bangle form it gives a Shimmer 10. Once a week the wearer can "think" a one line (11 words) message to a person known to them.

A hooded cape (fits any size) that will change the wearer's appearance and will change itself into the appropriate articles of clothing to match the disguise. It will do this 3 times a day. This cape has a POW of 18 if attuned with the bracer the effective POW is 20.
