Gedal Items

Two matching armbands made of rust colored iron. They will always be found together. In the right armband the following spells are stored:

Earth Power, Mud to Rock, Flesh to Stone, and Excavate

These spells are usable only once every 4 weeks at no POW cost to the wielder. They are usable for 2x normal POW cost for another 2 times.

The left armband has the following spells in it:

Heal Earth, Hold Avalanche, and Time Stop

These spells are only usable once per month with a cost of 1 permanent POW point from the wielder.

Heal Earth: is a spell used to return fertility to the earth after major disaster. It is also used once a year as part of the Ceremonies of Spring that Mara and Yimara hold.
