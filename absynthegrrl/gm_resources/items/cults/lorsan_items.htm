Lorsan Items

Ring of Red/Gold metal in the shape of a lion's head with topaz eyes. Gives the following powers 1 point of power give a 4 points of Parry, and adds 4 hit points to the weapon or shield in the hand it is worn on. The ring has a POW of 16.

The Sword of Lorsan the hilt is of shadow, the blade is of light with runes saying "Demon Slayer", on the tip of the blade is a 7-pointed star. INT 18, POW 18 it is non-encumbrance. Usable only by Lorsans of good standing. Made by the Elder Gods to banish demons to the hells. Lesser Demons are automatically slain when hit, Greater Demons are sent back to their planes of existence and are unable to return for two years. +4 to hit Greater Demons, +8 to hit Lesser Demons.
