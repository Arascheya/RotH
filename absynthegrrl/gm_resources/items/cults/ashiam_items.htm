Ashiam Items

Earrings are two reproductions of the moons in their dark phase. When attuned and worn by the High Priest/Priestess of the cult, they act as permanent power storage devices. Each will hold up to 3 points of power. Thus when both are full it will effectively give the wearer 6 points more power for defense and offensive spells. It is also usable to buy high powered rune spells that other wise would be unattainable and still be able to keep a rune priest standing in the cult. Each earring has a POW of 16, when both are being attuned at the same time their POW is 17.

The ring is a reproduction of the blue and red moons in the full phase, The moons are set in Eagle's claws, made of silver. When attuned and worn the wearer can cast a Free Ghost at twice the power of normal Free Ghost spell. If worn with the earrings the wearer can totally destroy undead (up to and including Nazguls) with a single touch, once per week. The ring has a POW of 17 if being attuned with one earring then their POW is 18 if the entire set is being attuned at once the POW is 19.
