Tomalon Items

A set of iron scales that will give the value of what ever is weighed on them in what ever currency is used on the other side. Example: a gem is put on one side and silver coins from Altarkran are placed on the other. The number of Altarkran silver coins is the value of said gem in Altarkran. If gold from the Lotus Empire is used it gives the value for the Lotus Empire. POW for the scales is 18, if attuned with the rods then the effective POW is 19.

Four green metal rods, when set about an established market area with an active Tomalon temple, produces a broad spectrum comprehend language spell for the entire market (language barriers are barriers to free trade). POW of the rods are 15.
