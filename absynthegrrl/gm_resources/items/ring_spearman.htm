Spearman’s Ring

This thick wooden ring is made from burnished cherry wood, and carved with lusty and perverse scenes in an archaic style. On the ring’s surface, samurai suck off goats, penetrate sheep and horses and mount animals of all kinds.

When worn over a man’s erect penis, the magical ring seems to flow and melt into his flesh. While wearing the Spearman’s Ring, a male gains the Phallic Spear Technique feat, even if he doesn’t meet the prerequisites.
