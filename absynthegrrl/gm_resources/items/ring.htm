Dragon Ring - Hut Triad item 3

The ring itself is made of red gold, its eyes are green jade, and the ball in its mouth is a rainbow diamond. The hair is actual human hair.

Value 50000 stars

Age 200

Intelligence 10

Ego 18

Alignment Neutral

This item was loaded by a 25th Order Arcane. It has the ability to cast Protection 3 at no cost to the owner. It will show any possessing entities in/on any other entity and will protect the wearer from any form of possession. It can cast a Cone of Cold and Personal Invisibility once per week.

This ring was stabilizing Margla - keeping the Consumer Demon from gaining the upper hand. Its loss at Skelos began her change to what she became. It also functions as a translator, and allows the wearer to see possessing entities of any type on/in their hosts, reveals hidden doors in the Hut, prevents possession of the wearer.
