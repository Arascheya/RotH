Shuriken

Weapon Type: throwing star

Damage: 1d6 and Special

Appearance: as pakua star only with 7 points.

Int: - Pow: -

These stars are created of adamantite alloy and will inflict double damage on any creature requiring majik to hit. If they do not kill the target they will burrow into it and act as a tracking device for the owner. For each hour they are inside a target they will move 2 inches closer to vital organs, striking to inflict maximum damage once they get there.

These items were created in Vilyanu for Shandi under the directions of her friends. They wanted her to have something to protect herself if they got split up.
