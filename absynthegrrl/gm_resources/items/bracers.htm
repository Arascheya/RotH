Snake Bracers - Hut Triad item

The bracers appear to be carved of various types of stone, carefully worked and carved to resemble asps. their detail is such that they appear real snakes frozen into this shape.

Value 50000 stars

Age 200

Intelligence 16

Ego 18

Alignment Neutral

These are also known as the Bracers of the Assassin. Each of the four snakes may be commanded once each month to go forth on a mission of the owner's choice. The tasks may be as simple as the recovery of an item they could successfully carry, or as complicated as the assassination of an important person. When worn they function as 20 point forarm protection and can cast Protection 3 on the wearer 3 times per day. Their powers are majikally made known to their owner if the owner is deemed worthy - Power vs Ego to resolve.

These bracers were created by Margla for Aristandilus Raisul in the time of the rise of Kharatoum. He was a servant of the Sultan of Kharatoum and a member of the Secret Police. He was also loyal to Margla, reporting to her anything of importance about the Sultanate. Through him she was seeking to keep track of Tesch Mar and possibly find Nyigroth.
