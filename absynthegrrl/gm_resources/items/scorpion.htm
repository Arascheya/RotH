Scorpion Tooth

Weapons Triad item 3

Weapon Type: dagger

Damage: 1d4+2 and Special

Appearance: as image

Int: 14 Pow: 18

Scorpion Tooth can hit as a Jamis Thorn once per day, when in combat will form a protective guantlet over the hand and forearm of the weilder.

Additionally Scorpion Tooth is enchanted as a tracking device for the Hut and has the ability to detect undead and demon taint at will. The detection spells costs it 1 point of power for each use. These spells are powered by the soul bound into it. It regains power as any other living being.

Scorpion Tooth will seek to attune itself to its weilder using regular attunement rules.
