Ankzmetze ra Mahklahzkna Nilikhnamaz (the Book of Blood Power)

This is the translation from the language of the destructionists as transcribed by Tregormas Riventongue. It is a long and lengthy tome of droning religious text. Of particular note is this prayer or ritual:

create the multiples of 7 stanzas that lead to the Kali Yuga temple, either directly or through the creation of a portal at the completion of the correct version. see Orbs Data for notes on how this access ritual should work
