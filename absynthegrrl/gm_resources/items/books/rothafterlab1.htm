22 Tomahas 6700 - It is a power amplifier, drawing on something unknown to function. Every spell we have sent through it emerges magnified over 100 fold.

25 Tomahas 6700 - Dorotea has left to research this item at the great libraries of Formanought.

27 Derethnish 6700 - Dorotea was only supposed to be gone a month. She still has not returned.

2 Cenetash 6700 - The artifact has been mounted in a magical projector as Tamaris commanded. All that remains is testing.

3 Cenetash 6700 - No time for tests, the war has come to us. Tamaris has moved the projector to her tower and ordered us to prepare potions and items for healing and battle.

5 Cenetash 6700 - I hope Dorotea is safe in Formnought and not on the road to be caught by the troupes surrounding the city. Tamaris is charging the projector for use in a few hours.
