Ice Dagger

This weapon looks like a dirty grey blade held in a jet black handle. It attacks as a regular dagger doing 1d6 points of damage. If it successfully hits with 20 or more percentage points less than required the tip will break off in the wound. This causes 1 point of cold damage per melee round until it is removed or the victim is dead. Healing spells can slow the effect to 1 point every turn, but only /*assign herb or majik potion capable of counter-acting*/ can actually remove the poisonous tip.
