Gummy Bear

Category: Mixed Drinks

Ingredients:

15 ml (0.5 oz.) Amaretto

15 ml (0.5 oz.) Grenadine

15 ml (0.5 oz.) Melon liqueur

90 ml (3 oz.) Orange Juice

90 ml (3 oz.) Pineapple Juice

15 ml (0.5 oz.) Southern Comfort®

Recipe:

Fill to 500 ml with pinnapple and OJ on the rocks with a splash of 7up

Serve in erlenmeyer flask
