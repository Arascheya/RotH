Mojito

type: Mixed Drinks

60 - 90 ml (2-3 oz) Light Rum

30 ml (1 oz) Lime Juice

2 tsp(s) Sugar

1 pinch(es) Mint

Fill to 500 ml with Soda Water

mixing: Place sugar, a large pinch (small handful) of mint, and splash of soda water in a pint glass. Use muddler to lightly press mint and dissolve sugar until it smells of spearmint gum. Squeeze both halves of lime into the glass, leaving one hull in the mixture. Add rum, stir, and fill with ice. Top with soda water and garnish with mint sprig.

Notes:

Serve in beaker
