Lotus Plants

Purple Lotus

Dust

A purplish powder made from the dried and crushed petals and pollen and stored in a graduated clear vial marked in numbers of flowers - maximum amount is dust from 4 flowers. The dust from one flower will cause sleep for 1d12 days. Save is con vs. 30. if save with less than half of what is required - no sleep, otherwise sleep time is halved.

It can be mixed into a liquid form with any alcoholic liquid. One flower will produce 12 turns of paralysis with a save to 6 turns.

Black Lotus

Dust

A black powder made from the dried and crushed petals and pollen and stored in a graduated clear vial marked in numbers of flowers - the maximum amount is dust from 4 flowers. This powder is a powerful neurotoxin which inflicts 12 points of brain/intelligence damage if inhaled. This damage can be reversed only by magical healing methods.

When mixed with honey, nuts, and a small amount of zerym flower extract it will produce a mildly hallucinogenic paste that can be chewed. It is a popular form of entertainment in some desert areas.

Roots

Used fresh, the roots are an integral component of Xtaze production.

Dried roots can be chewed to produce a mild euphoric reaction and an easing of hunger. Many travellers employ these dried roots when on extended trips to stretch their food supplies. There is a slight danger of starvation in those that are particularly sensitive to the effects of these roots.

Red Lotus

Dust

A red powder that is the dried pollen of these flowers. It is stored in a graduated clear vial marked in numbers of flowers. The dried pollen causes Red Rot, a condition where the victim rots at 1d12 every 6 turns until dead. It requires Cure Disease and Cure Heavy Wounds simultaneously to cure it.

Yellow Lotus

Powder

A black powder prepared from the dried roots and stored in a graduated clear vial marked in numbers of doses - the maximum amount is dust from 4 flowers. One dose will produce a light unconsciousness in the victim. A sharp noise will awaken them.
