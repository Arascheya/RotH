Zerym Plant

The zerym plant is a climbing vine with wide and short deep green leaves. It tends to grow near deciduous trees because it favors the nutrients from decaying leaf material. It blooms with many small seven petal flowers. The petals are delicate violet radiating from a deep red center point. These plants are self polinating.

The organic compounds that concentrate in the flowers and seed pods are highly prized for various medicinal purposes. These compounds are also essential in the production of Xtaze, a highly addictive halucinogen.
