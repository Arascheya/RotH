D.M.& I.R. #347

Duluth,Missabe, And Iron Range 2-8-0 #347

Taken, Chisholm, Minn. 5/28/52

Originally #347, Class "C-3", of Duluth Missabe & Northern. Built by Alco-Pittsburgh C/N 42285, 1907.

Renumbered #347, D.M.& I.R.

Cylinders = 22" x 28", Driver Dia. = 56", Wt. = 185,400 lb. Tr. Force = 39,080 lb.

Presented to Museum of Mining, Chisholm, Minn. 1953
