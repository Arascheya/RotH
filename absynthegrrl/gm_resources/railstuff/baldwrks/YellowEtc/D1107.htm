DM&IR #1107

Duluth,Missabe, And Iron Range, Class "A", 4-6-2 #1107

Taken Two Harbors, Minn. 5/11/51, 2:30 P.M.

Originally #107-110 of Duluth & Iron Range.

#107 was the first of only 4 class "A's" on the railroad. D.& I.R. didn't do a lot of passenger business. Built by Baldwin, 1913, C/N 39861-64. .

Renumbered #1107-1110, D.M.& I.R.

Cylinders = 22" x 28", Driver Dia. = 69", Wt. = 219,000 lb. Tr. Force = 33,400 lb.

Sold for scrap,1955

Note coal docks in background.
