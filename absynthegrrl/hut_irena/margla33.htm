33 - Main Stairway

Hut Room Details

This stairway links the various living sections of the current occupants. Each stair landing leads to a different living space. It attaches to the rest of the Hut through doors on the floor level in the image. The upper right door leads to the Dining Alcove, the upper right door with the sunlight leads to the Cloisters, a door at the bottom left - going out the bottom of the image - leads to the Crew Common Room, and the small center stairs lead to the Library.

These stairs are "endless" with the top of the stairwell linking to the bottom at the base of the shown stairs at the bottom left. The height of the stairwell is determined by how many living sections link to it. It expands and contracts to link to the current number of living sections defined.
