Hut Room Details

The attic looks very much like what one would expect to see, except that no chimney rises through it from the stove, and the ceiling isn't pointed like the roof of the Hut. Scattered about the floor of the attic are trunks, chests, and boxes full of old clothing, used-up wands and burnt-out amulets, and the other useless paraphernalia that an ancient witch might accumulate. A spiral stair leads up, a trap door leads down, and two normal doors lead out.

One of the trunks contains 20-80 gems, 20-50 jewels, and a pouch of 4 Summoning Gems. Beneath this treasure is a silk cloth covering up a symbol of insanity inscribed on the trunk's bottom. A second trunk contains a Power Sharing Crystal hidden in a false bottom.
