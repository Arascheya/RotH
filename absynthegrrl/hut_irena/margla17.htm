17 - Formal Dining Hall

CH, 10' flat in room A, 20' in room B; IL, I CL in room A, see below for room B.

Hut Room Details

This area is divided into two rooms, a small entry chamber that connects to other sections of the hut and a large, formal dining hall.

False windows in the room have illusions cast on them to make it appear that the viewer is looking out over a pastoral countryside from the dining hall, with bright sunlight shining down and a gentle breeze blowing through windows that are half open. It is not possible to exit through the windows.
