Tesch Mar Hypothesis

Unified Universal Magic

Magic is the use of naturally occurring energies to achieve a desired result. These energies are universal and well defined. Any form of magic inherently draws upon these energies regardless of the method used for gathering. The gathering methods are just different techniques taught to access the same energies. Some techniques are easier to master and thus are easier to teach and use.

Each spell is a pattern of energy control that produces a predictable and desired result. These patterns may be taught or learned by intense study and experimentation.

Each spell consists of several distinct sections. The first section is the preparation of the user's awareness and concentration, allowing the user to visualize the energies used to create the desired pattern. The second section is the actual construction of the magical pattern for the spell and draws on the inherent psychic power of the user. The third section is the gathering and binding of external, supplemental energy required by the spell. This step often requires additional energies from the user to control the gathered energy. The final step is the targeting and release of the finished spell.

Spells are taught as complete patterns to the neophyte, only as the user's skill and abilities grow is the user able to figure out how to change the amount of external energy required to vary the effect of the spell. Subtle changes in the pattern creation will allow changing the range or area of effect of the spell.

Magic identification is done by following the patterning of the spell(s) on the item in question. To be adept at this skill the identifier must have had exposure to a broad base of spells and pattern examples.

Items are created by first weaving a binding spell (enchant) into the material parts of the item. Once the item is assembled then the actual effect spell(s) are woven into the hooks left by the enchant base spell.

A 12th level spell, Encryption, allows the creator of an item to put a randomized pattern into the overall pattern of the item. This randomizer constantly changes so it is very difficult to unweave the magic on the item. Exhaustive research is required to attempt to dispel magic that has been encrypted this way. Usually this requires access to many spells and items created by the developer of the item to be dispelled. The caster of the encryption can unweave this pattern at any time.

End of Tesch Mar Hypothesis

Tesch Mar Thesis Brief

Gates

There are two forms of gates on the average world. These are the "Natural" gate and the "Hell" gate. Both come into being where the boundary between "Real Space" (an Ancient term for the Prime Material Plane) and the "Gate Dimension" are thin. Through my researches I intend to determine the usual number and placement strategy of these gates. I also plan to prove the connection between Hell gates and Natural gates. I believe the two are connected phenomenon.

Further I hope to prove these gates are actually linked to local magical nodes (called Earth Nodes in some papers) in some way. I plan to prove these gate nodes are actually super nodes in the earth node network. As such mathematical analysis indicates there should be seven super nodes for each local network in the Prime Material Plan. (mathematical modeling based on the Tesch Mar Hypothesis of Unified Universal Magic)

Natural gates may be used to travel between places in the Prime Material Plane. All that is required is the appropriate magic spells. These gates may be stabilized with powerful spells and held in one place, otherwise they tend to open, close, and drift randomly.

Hell gates open, close and drift similar to Natural gates but their stabilization requires sacrifices of sentient beings at each gate location.

End of Tesch Mar Thesis Brief

PROPHECIES ABOUT MY RELEASE

My old friend Kayara has appeared to me in a dream and told me to seek a Gypsy seer of great skill. I have seen the woman and am recording all her statements that I may follow the one path that shall free me.

FIRST MEETING

The web of your destiny entangles many times and many lives, yet I see a strand that may free you. The path is long and full of pain yet you may succeed. You shall have two daughters and they shall be the keys to your salvation.

One shall be born of a desert nomad and left to foster with his tribe. You shall feign death as the mother of the child, seeking respite from the burning sands that took your mate. This girl shall grow strong and powerful. She shall be touched by the High Elder, the Perverter of Spiders, and the Butcher. Through all she shall come into her own power.

The second daughter shall be born of a chance encounter. More I will not know until the birth of your first daughter. Seek me then.

BIRTH OF MY FIRST DAUGHTER

Your second daughter shall be fathered by a random encounter in the city of your friend, the Lady of Pain. Seek drink and distraction at the Twelve Factols 7 zerads from now. The father of your child shall be a worlder that has wandered to the City of Doors by accident. He shall spend his last pleasant night in your embrace. You shall not consume him, for he is destined to fall elsewhere in the service of another power. Seek me once more when your second daughter’s birth is near.

SECOND DAUGHTER BIRTH IS NEAR

Once she is born you will take her to the place where the Eldest of the Seven was punished by the One from Before, the one known as the High Elder. Here was the Perverter of Spiders created and here was the place where three times met and became one. Go to the tower of the Star Mages and search for the Cavern of the Gate nearby. Within its depths shall you see the way to the place your daughter must go. This must you do before you leave your daughter. Make for her a duplicate of the Key to your Hut. This Key must be an identical copy of yours and bound to it as if they were one. Bind it to her in a pocket place that is with her always. It shall open if anything should happen to you. Trust the settings upon the Gate Lock for they are the ones for the time and the place of your daughter’s fostering. Place her where the blue baby is.

Once this is in motion seek me again in thirteen Naramahasans when I shall know more.

NARAMAHASAN MEETING

Your daughters are well. They prepare for their destinies even now, though they know nothing of them as yet. Know then that this is what you must do.

Three triads must be formed. The first shall be known as the Second Triad. It shall be their purpose to take the place of the Three-As-One, the First Triad, that you now are. The other two shall be known as the Weapons Triads and they shall be the protectors of the Second Triad. To them falls the honor of protection and service.

The Second Triad must be formed of three that shall come from diverse places and times. Your daughter fostered where the Perverter of Spiders was created shall be one. She shall be returned to you through another continuum. She carries the duplicate Key and through this Key will you be able to help this Triad survive. Another shall also strive for them, using her majiks for her own desires.

The second shall possess the Bracers of the Assassin that you created for Aristandilus Raisul in the time of the rise of Kharatoum.

The final member of this triad shall come from a far away place, a place where you formed the first arcane school. More shall I know of him later..

The weapons for the Weapons Triads are still hidden from me. Seek me at Mahasan and I shall know more.

MAHASAN MEETING

You must create several weapons for the Weapons Triads. Those that I have seen are a flute, two daggers, a bastard sword, and a katana. The weapons shall be crafted by the weapons master Hiromata, weapons master to the Jewel of the Lotus. The enchantments shall be created by you based on the prophecies.

The flute is part of the Weapons Triads arsenal for it shall sustain its wielder and thus keep the strength of the Triads undiminished. Into this flute shall be placed the spell of charming. This charming will be in the form of music so sweet and delightful to the hearers that any thought of free will and opposition shall be driven from them. The wielder may suggest actions to any listener that will be followed as long as they are not deliberately hurtful to the listener. Further the flute shall be able to fire a sleeping dart at a single target once each day. This dart shall render the target to sleep for a duration based on the target’s physical strength, but for no less time than half of a night.

Two daggers shall be made. The first, Scorpion Tooth, shall create living armor around the hand of the wielder. In combat it will grow a majikal tail ending in a stinger that will dispense paralysis poison each time it hits. The Scorpion Tooth must first have tasted of the flesh of the victim before it can sting. The final enchantments shall bind it to the Hut as a tracking device and give it the power to detect undead and demon taint. Scorpion Tooth shall have a spirit bound to it so that it may attune itself to its wielder and power its own detection spells.

The second dagger blade shall be formed of simple weapons steel, the finest Hiromata can forge, and it shall be set into a handle of demon bone. The likeness of a goat shall be carved on it. Bind into this weapon the hooks for holding spells that you may store two in it when you shall have finished its creation. At the time of its completion the first two spells you think of shall be the ones it carries.

The bastard sword shall be crafted in the same way as the goat dagger. Once again the spells it shall contain shall be the first that you think of once it is completed.

The katana must be an exact replica of the katana he forged before the Hell Gate Wars. Into this weapon you must bind many and various majiks. The greatest of which shall be the spell binding it to the hut and binding it to its wielder. This spell shall be the link by which you shall bind the wielder to the hut when the time of your freedom is at hand.

Scorpion Tooth must you place in the hands of your first daughter when she is driven from her home in the brothel of Altarkran. She may know it came from you only upon your death.

The owner of the katana will be the son of the owner of the original katana. He shall receive a katana from his family that is said to have come down from the Hell Gate Wars. This must be the replacement you have enchanted rather than the original his mother carried. The family realizes not what it has.

The owner of the second dagger will be a friend of the owner of the katana. He shall receive the dagger from his family that is said to have come down from the Hell Gate Wars. This must be the replacement you have enchanted rather than the original his father carried. The family realizes not what it has.

The bastard sword shall be gifted to the companion of your daughter as she returns from the other continuum.

Those that shall wield these weapons I have not yet seen. Nor have I seen the other members of the Second Triad. The mists of time are clouded by the powers of the Factions that seek to pierce them.

Seek me again once the weapons have been enchanted.
